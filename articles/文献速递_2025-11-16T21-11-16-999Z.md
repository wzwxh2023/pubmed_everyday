## 1. 病程调节难治性癫痫中α振荡的BOLD信号表征：一项同步脑电图与功能性磁共振成像研究

**原文链接：** [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41239980](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41239980)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41239980
**DOI：** 10.1111/epi.18685

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Disease duration modulates BOLD representations of alpha oscillations in drug-resistant epilepsy: A concurrent EEG and fMRI study.

**英文摘要原文：**
OBJECTIVE: Alpha oscillations play a fundamental role in top-down cognitive processes by regulating cortical excitability. Building on previous reports of altered alpha activity in patients with drug-resistant epilepsy (DRE), we conducted concurrent electroencephalography (EEG) and functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) studies to investigate the blood oxygen level-dependent (BOLD) correlates of alpha oscillations and assess whether these responses are modulated by disease duration.

METHODS: Twenty-seven adults with focal DRE were recruited to undergo concurrent EEG and fMRI studies. Alpha oscillations were defined as the frequency range spanning from 7 Hz to the individual alpha peak frequency (IAPF) plus 2 Hz. Both relative alpha power fluctuations and epilepsy duration were included in the fMRI data analysis.

RESULTS: The BOLD responses associated with alpha oscillations in patients with DRE differed from those previously reported in healthy individuals. Epilepsy duration was negatively correlated with IAPF and modulated alpha-related activity in brain regions implicated in sympathetic regulation: (1) increased activation in the paracentral lobule and right anterior and posterior insular cortices, and (2) decreased activation in the bilateral angular gyri and left orbitofrontal gyrus.

SIGNIFICANCE: These findings advance our understanding of how epilepsy interacts with the central autonomic system by revealing distinct modulation of alpha activity. Although brain imaging is critical in epilepsy assessment, MRI findings in patients with DRE are often unremarkable. This study provides an alternative approach to characterizing disease progression in DRE.

**中文摘要译文：**
目的：α振荡通过调节皮层兴奋性，在自上而下的认知过程中发挥着基础性作用。基于先前关于难治性癫痫（DRE）患者α活动改变的报道，我们进行了同步脑电图（EEG）和功能性磁共振成像（fMRI）研究，旨在探究α振荡的血氧水平依赖（BOLD）相关信号，并评估这些反应是否受病程的调节。

方法：本研究招募了27名局灶性DRE成人患者进行同步EEG和fMRI研究。α振荡的定义为从7赫兹到个体α峰值频率（IAPF）加2赫兹的频率范围。相对α功率波动和癫痫病程均被纳入fMRI数据分析中。

结果：DRE患者中与α振荡相关的BOLD反应不同于先前在健康个体中报道的结果。癫痫病程与IAPF呈负相关，并调节了与交感神经调控相关的脑区的α相关活动：（1）旁中央小叶、右侧前部及后部岛叶皮层的激活增强，以及（2）双侧角回和左侧眶额回的激活减弱。

意义：这些发现揭示了α活动的独特性调节机制，从而加深了我们对癫痫如何与中枢自主神经系统相互作用的理解。尽管脑成像在癫痫评估中至关重要，但DRE患者的MRI检查结果往往无特殊表现。本研究为表征DRE的疾病进展提供了一种替代方法。

## 2. 每日间歇性禁食在失神癫痫临床前模型中是一种有效的多尺度治疗方法

**原文链接：** [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41239913](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41239913)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41239913
**DOI：** 10.1111/epi.18704

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Daily intermittent fasting is an effective multiscale treatment in preclinical models of absence epilepsy.

**英文摘要原文：**
OBJECTIVE: Absence epilepsy is characterized by brief but frequent seizures with loss of consciousness. Existing treatments, which come with heavy side effects, are only partially effective and do not address the associated comorbidities, including cognitive and social deficits. A tripartite link between seizures, cognitive deficits, and diet has been established. Intermittent fasting (IF) is a regime where daily periods of fasting are alternated with periods of food intake. To date, the effects of IF on infantile epilepsy have not been addressed. We evaluated the therapeutic potential of a daily, 1-month protocol of IF on an established model of generalized absence epilepsy, the Grm7 knock-in mouse.

METHODS: To assess the effects of IF in mice, we combined electroencephalography, behavior analysis, high-throughput RNA sequencing, and histology approaches.

RESULTS: We demonstrate for the first time that daily IF reduces social recognition deficits and epileptic seizures. These effects are associated with the remodeling of the cerebral vascular system. We identified a deregulation of genes involved in vascularization, reflected in the malformation of blood vessels in one of the key brain areas of the absence seizure circuit, the thalamus. Along with its antiseizure effects, we show that IF is able to counteract both abnormal gene expression and vessel morphology. Finally, we demonstrate that IF successfully reduces both seizure frequency and social interaction deficits in the AY-9944 mouse, a preclinical model of atypical absence seizures, found in more severe epileptic syndromes.

SIGNIFICANCE: Taken together, this study is the first to demonstrate the positive and multiscale effects of IF in the treatment of absence epilepsy.

**中文摘要译文：**
目的：失神癫痫的特征是短暂但频繁的伴有意识丧失的发作。现有治疗方法伴有严重的副作用，不仅疗效有限，也无法解决相关的合并症，包括认知和社交缺陷。癫痫发作、认知缺陷和饮食之间的三方联系已被证实。间歇性禁食（IF）是一种每日禁食期与进食期交替的饮食方案。迄今为止，IF对婴儿癫痫的影响尚未被研究。我们评估了为期1个月的每日IF方案对已建立的全身性失神癫痫模型——Grm7基因敲入小鼠的治疗潜力。

方法：为了评估IF在小鼠中的效果，我们结合了脑电图、行为分析、高通量RNA测序和组织学方法。

结果：我们首次证明，每日IF能减少社交识别缺陷和癫痫发作。这些效果与脑血管系统的重塑有关。我们发现，在失神癫痫回路的关键脑区之一——丘脑中，参与血管化的基因表达失调，这反映为血管畸形。我们发现，除了抗癫痫发作作用外，IF还能够逆转异常的基因表达和血管形态。最后，我们证明IF能成功减少AY-9944小鼠（一种用于更严重癫痫综合征的非典型失神发作临床前模型）的发作频率和社交互动缺陷。

意义：总而言之，本研究首次证实了IF在治疗失神癫痫中的积极和多尺度效应。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究旨在探究每日间歇性禁食（IF）对失神癫痫的治疗潜力。研究人员利用两种临床前小鼠模型，综合运用脑电图、行为学、RNA测序和组织学等方法，发现IF不仅能有效减少癫痫发作和改善社交缺陷，还能逆转丘脑区域的血管畸形和相关基因的异常表达。此项研究首次从多尺度层面揭示了IF治疗失神癫痫的积极作用，为探索饮食干预作为一种新型、副作用更少的癫痫治疗策略提供了重要的临床前证据，并指出了脑血管重塑可能是其关键作用机制。
