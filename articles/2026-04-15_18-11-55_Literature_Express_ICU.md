## 1. 重症监护领域的性别平等与公平：一项国际德尔菲共识研究。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41984151)

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Gender equality and equity in intensive care: an international Delphi consensus study.

> **英文摘要：**
> PURPOSE: We used Delphi methodology to provide guidance on gender equality and equity issues in professional life in intensive care, where information is evolving and no clear standard exists.
> 
> METHODS: A 12-member Steering Committee (7 women, 5 men) from 7 countries and 46 international panelists [(23 women, 21 men, 2 preferred not to disclose; median age 52 (33-75) years] from 32 countries (43% low- and middle-income) including intensive care practitioners, scientists, researchers, and trainees voted on 57 statements addressing issues related to gender equality and equity in 10 domains of professional life. Delphi rounds were conducted using online surveys. Consensus (at least 75% of panelists voting for a response option) and stability (consistent responses on iterative rounds) were assessed.
> 
> RESULTS: Six Delphi rounds were conducted between May and July 2025. A 100% response rate was achieved in each round. Consensus and stability were achieved on 43 (75%) of 57 statements from which 37 professional practice guidance statements were developed. Across domains, greater consensus was achieved on equality [23/27 (85.2%)] versus equity [12/18 (66.7%)] statements. Discordant equity statements primarily pertained to academia and engagement in multiprofessional meetings and the workplace.
> 
> CONCLUSION: Using a Delphi method, international experts reached consensus to generate 37 professional practice guidance statements. The consensus statements provide needed guidance for professional engagement and highlight areas for policy development to advance gender equity and equality for healthcare workers in intensive care. The discordant statements highlight areas for future research.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：我们采用德尔菲方法，旨在为重症监护职业生活中的性别平等与公平问题提供指导，因为在这一领域，相关信息仍在不断发展，且尚无明确标准。
> 
> 方法：一个由来自7个国家的12名成员（7名女性，5名男性）组成的指导委员会，以及来自32个国家（43%为中低收入国家）的46名国际专家组成员（23名女性，21名男性，2名不愿透露；中位年龄52岁，范围33-75岁），包括重症监护从业者、科学家、研究人员和受训人员，就涉及10个职业生活领域的性别平等与公平问题的57项声明进行了投票。德尔菲回合通过在线调查进行。我们对共识（至少75%的专家组成员投票选择某一选项）和稳定性（在迭代回合中反应一致）进行了评估。
> 
> 结果：在2025年5月至7月期间，共进行了六轮德尔菲调查。每一轮都实现了100%的回复率。在57项声明中，有43项（75%）达成了共识和稳定性，并据此制定了37项专业实践指导声明。在各个领域中，关于平等的声明 [23/27 (85.2%)] 比关于公平的声明 [12/18 (66.7%)] 获得了更高的共识。存在分歧的公平性声明主要涉及学术界、多专业会议的参与以及工作场所。
> 
> 结论：通过德尔菲方法，国际专家达成共识，生成了37项专业实践指导声明。这些共识声明为专业参与提供了必要的指导，并指出了为推进重症监护领域医护人员性别公平与平等而需要制定政策的领域。存在分歧的声明则突显了未来需要研究的领域。

## 2. 神经退行性疾病中的免疫信号传导与功能

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41983391)
**期刊：** The Journal of clinical investigation
**PMID：** 41983391
**DOI：** 10.1172/JCI199850

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Immune signaling and function in neurodegeneration.

> **英文摘要：**
> Neurodegenerative diseases arise from interactions among pathogenic proteins, immune responses, and diverse environmental or age-related stressors that disrupt CNS homeostasis. CNS resident microglia detect self-derived danger signals through pattern recognition receptors, and their activation can promote clearance of aberrant proteins, including amyloid-β, tau, α-synuclein, and TAR DNA-binding protein 43. However, microglial activation may also drive maladaptive states that amplify neuroinflammation. Microglial transitions are further shaped by receptor-mediated signaling and antigen presentation pathways that integrate environmental cues with functional responses. Adaptive immune cells contribute additional layers of regulation, with CD8+ and CD4+ T cells exerting neuroprotective or neurotoxic effects depending on disease context, activation state, and antigen specificity. The identification of granzyme K-expressing CD8+ T cells in several neurodegenerative conditions highlights the growing recognition that distinct T cell subsets may have specialized roles in disease. Aging, repetitive head injury, and viral infection further alter microglial phenotypes, weaken barrier integrity, promote T cell recruitment, and prime the CNS for chronic inflammation. In this review, we synthesize current knowledge of innate and adaptive immune mechanisms in neurodegeneration, examine how external factors influence these responses, and consider how these insights may guide future therapeutic strategies.

> **中文摘要：**
> 神经退行性疾病源于致病蛋白、免疫反应以及破坏中枢神经系统（CNS）稳态的各种环境或年龄相关应激源之间的相互作用。CNS 驻留的小胶质细胞通过模式识别受体检测源自自身的危险信号，其激活可促进异常蛋白质的清除，包括 β-淀粉样蛋白、tau 蛋白、α-突触核蛋白和 TAR DNA 结合蛋白 43。然而，小胶质细胞的激活也可能导致加剧神经炎症的适应不良状态。受体介导的信号传导和抗原提呈途径进一步塑造了小胶质细胞的转变，这些途径将环境线索与功能反应相结合。适应性免疫细胞贡献了额外的调节层级，CD8+ 和 CD4+ T 细胞根据疾病背景、激活状态和抗原特异性发挥神经保护或神经毒性作用。在几种神经退行性疾病中发现表达颗粒酶 K 的 CD8+ T 细胞，突显了人们日益认识到不同的 T 细胞亚群在疾病中可能具有专门的作用。衰老、重复性头部损伤和病毒感染进一步改变了小胶质细胞的表型，削弱了屏障完整性，促进了 T 细胞招募，并使中枢神经系统进入慢性炎症状态。在本综述中，我们综合了神经退行性疾病中先天性和适应性免疫机制的现有知识，探讨了外部因素如何影响这些反应，并思考了这些见解将如何指导未来的治疗策略。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该综述系统地总结了先天性与适应性免疫在神经退行性疾病中的双刃剑作用，深入探讨了小胶质细胞和 T 细胞在维持 CNS 稳态与驱动神经炎症之间的动态平衡。文章不仅涵盖了致病蛋白清除的经典免疫机制，还前瞻性地分析了环境、衰老及病毒感染对免疫微环境的重塑作用，特别是对特定 T 细胞亚群（如 GZMK+ CD8+ T 细胞）的功能描述具有显著的前沿性。该文献为理解免疫介导的神经损伤提供了全景视角，对未来基于免疫调节的神经保护疗法开发具有重要指导意义。

## 3. 降钙素原检测对社区获得性肺炎住院患者抗生素使用的影响：一项多中心队列研究

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41981820)
**期刊：** Clinical infectious diseases : an official publication of the Infectious Diseases Society of America
**PMID：** 41981820
**DOI：** 10.1093/cid/ciag165

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Impact of Procalcitonin Testing on Antibiotic Use in Patients Hospitalized With Community-Acquired Pneumonia: A Multi-Hospital Cohort Study.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Procalcitonin (PCT) testing may guide antibiotic decisions in community-acquired pneumonia (CAP), but real-world evidence of its impact on antibiotic prescribing in the era of shorter antibiotic courses remains limited. METHODS: We conducted a retrospective cohort study of adults with CAP admitted to the wards of 12 hospitals between November 2022 and January 2025. Primary analyses included: (1) PCT testing rates at admission, (2) the association between PCT testing and duration of antibiotic therapy, and (3) among patients initiated on extended-spectrum antibiotics (ESAs), the relationship between PCT results and early de-escalation. We compared outcomes between PCT-tested and untested patients and between those with low (<0.1 μg/L) vs high (≥0.1 μg/L) PCT values using propensity score overlap weighting to control for potential confounders. RESULTS: Among 17 290 non-intensive care unit (ICU) admissions for CAP, PCT testing was performed for 10 658 patients (59.5%) within 24 hours of admission. After adjustments, PCT testing was associated with shorter antibiotic duration (adjusted mean difference, -0.32 days; 95% confidence interval [CI], -.52 to -.12) but was not significantly associated with ESA de-escalation compared with no PCT testing (hazard ratio, 1.05; 95% CI, .99-1.12). Patients with low PCT values received shorter antibiotic courses compared with those with high values (adjusted mean difference, -0.56 days; 95% confidence interval [CI], -.85 to -.49). CONCLUSIONS: In non-ICU patients with CAP, PCT testing was associated with modest differences in antibiotic duration, primarily when values were low.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：降钙素原（PCT）检测可能指导社区获得性肺炎（CAP）的抗生素决策，但在抗生素疗程缩短的时代，其对实际抗生素处方影响的现实证据仍然有限。方法：我们对2022年11月至2025年1月期间在12家医院普通病房住院的社区获得性肺炎成年患者进行了一项回顾性队列研究。主要分析包括：（1）入院时的PCT检测率；（2）PCT检测与抗生素治疗持续时间之间的关联；（3）在启动广谱抗生素（ESA）治疗的患者中，PCT结果与早期降级治疗之间的关系。我们使用倾向评分重叠加权法来控制潜在的混杂因素，比较了PCT检测组与未检测组，以及PCT低值（<0.1 μg/L）与高值（≥0.1 μg/L）患者之间的结局。结果：在17,290例非重症监护病房（ICU）的社区获得性肺炎住院患者中，有10,658例（59.5%）在入院24小时内进行了PCT检测。调整后，PCT检测与较短的抗生素疗程相关（调整后的平均差异为-0.32天；95%置信区间 [CI] 为 -0.52至-0.12），但与不进行PCT检测相比，其与广谱抗生素的降级无显著相关性（风险比为1.05；95% CI为0.99-1.12）。与高PCT值患者相比，PCT低值患者接受的抗生素疗程更短（调整后的平均差异为-0.56天；95% CI为 -0.85至-0.49）。结论：在非ICU的社区获得性肺炎患者中，PCT检测与抗生素疗程的轻微差异相关，特别是在数值较低时。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过大规模多中心回顾性队列，系统评估了当代临床背景下PCT检测对非重症CAP患者抗生素使用的实际价值。研究发现，虽然PCT检测能微弱缩短抗生素疗程（约0.3天），但在促进广谱抗生素早期降级方面并无显著统计学意义，其临床获益主要局限于PCT低值患者。这一结果提示，在当前普遍推行短疗程抗生素治疗的背景下，常规开展PCT检测对优化抗生素管理的边际贡献可能低于预期，其在临床中的决策权重需审慎评估。

## 4. 反义寡核苷酸介导的基因敲降疗法在两名重症KCNT1相关性癫痫性脑病患儿中的应用

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41981306)
**期刊：** Nature medicine
**PMID：** 41981306
**DOI：** 10.1038/s41591-026-04314-9

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Antisense oligonucleotide-mediated knockdown therapy in two infants with severe KCNT1 epileptic encephalopathy.

> **英文摘要：**
> KCNT1-related epileptic encephalopathy, including epilepsy of infancy with migrating focal seizures, is a severe neurodevelopmental disorder associated with refractory seizures, profound neurologic impairment and premature death. It is caused by de novo genetic variants in KCNT1 that alter the function of Slack, an evolutionarily conserved sodium-gated potassium channel that modulates neuronal firing patterns and excitability. Pathogenic KCNT1 variants lead to overactive Slack channels, boosting total neuronal potassium currents by up to 40%, driving cortical hyperexcitability and causing seizures. Here we investigate antisense oligonucleotide-mediated KCNT1 knockdown as a therapeutic strategy for patients with epilepsy of infancy with migrating focal seizures. Intrathecal delivery of an experimental, non-allele-specific, KCNT1-targeting antisense oligonucleotide by lumbar puncture in two 2-year-old females with KCNT1 p.R474H, a severe, recurrent pathogenic variant, led to a significant reduction in seizure frequency and intensity. However, investigational treatment was also associated with the development of ventricular enlargement or hydrocephalus in both patients, prompting in one case the redirection of goals of care, pointing to a potential monitorable toxicity of some intrathecal antisense oligonucleotides.

> **中文摘要：**
> KCNT1相关性癫痫性脑病，包括婴儿迁移性局灶性癫痫，是一种严重的神经发育障碍，与难治性癫痫、严重神经功能损害和过早死亡相关。 该病由KCNT1基因的新发变异引起，这些变异改变了Slack的功能，后者是一种进化上保守的钠门控钾离子通道，负责调节神经元的放电模式和兴奋性。 致病性KCNT1变异导致Slack通道过度活跃，使神经元总钾电流增加高达40%，从而驱动皮层过度兴奋并引发癫痫。 本研究旨在探讨反义寡核苷酸介导的KCNT1基因敲降作为婴儿迁移性局灶性癫痫患者的一种治疗策略。 研究中，两名携带严重、复发性致病变异KCNT1 p.R474H的两岁女性患儿，通过腰椎穿刺接受了一种实验性的、非等位基因特异性的、靶向KCNT1的反义寡核苷酸鞘内注射治疗，结果显示癫痫发作的频率和强度均显著降低。 然而，该试验性治疗也与两名患者出现脑室扩大或脑积水有关，这导致其中一例的治疗目标被重新调整，提示某些鞘内注射的反义寡核苷酸可能存在可监测的潜在毒性。

## 5. 携带APOB罕见变异是发生严重脂肪性肝病和肝细胞癌的易感因素。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41665936)
**期刊：** The Journal of clinical investigation
**PMID：** 41665936
**DOI：** 10.1172/JCI201762

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Carriage of rare APOB variants predisposes to severe steatotic liver disease and hepatocellular carcinoma.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUNDMetabolic dysfunction-associated steatotic liver disease (MASLD) has a substantial inherited component. Rare variants in apolipoprotein B gene (APOB) have been implicated in susceptibility to liver steatosis, but their role in disease progression and outcomes is unclear.METHODSWe investigated APOB rare variants in a case-control cohort of people with advanced MASLD versus healthy controls (n = 510 and 261, respectively), a family-based study (n = 43 and literature meta-analysis), the Million Veteran Program (MVP) cohort (n = 94,885), and the UK Biobank (UKBB) (n = 417,657).RESULTSIn the clinical cohort, APOB variants were enriched in people with advanced MASLD (OR 13.8, 95% CI: 2.7-70.7, P = 0.002) and associated with lower circulating lipids, but higher MASLD activity and fibrosis (P < 0.05). In the family study, APOB variants segregated with hepatic steatosis and fibrosis (P < 0.05). Cross-ancestry meta-analysis of the study cohorts yielded pooled ORs for cirrhosis and hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) of 1.82, 95% CI: 1.33-2.49 and 3.53, 95% CI: 2.09-5.98, respectively. Variants affecting specifically ApoB100 had a 3-fold greater effect on hepatic lipid metabolism compared with those impairing also ApoB48 and were specifically protective against coronary artery disease (P < 0.05). The variants affected cirrhosis risk similarly, but ApoB48/100 had a larger effect on HCC (P < 0.05).CONCLUSIONSRare APOB variants predispose individuals to advanced MASLD and HCC, with distinct contributions from disrupted VLDL and chylomicrons secretion. These findings highlight the interplay between hepatic and intestinal lipid handling, suggesting that APOB genotyping may enhance MASLD risk stratification and patient identification.FUNDINGEuropean Union, Italian Ministry of Health, Swedish Research Council, Veterans Health Administration, NIH.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：代谢功能障碍相关的脂肪性肝病（MASLD）具有显著的遗传因素。载脂蛋白B基因（APOB）的罕见变异被认为与肝脏脂肪变性的易感性有关，但其在疾病进展和结局中的作用尚不清楚。方法：我们在一个晚期MASLD患者与健康对照的病例对照队列（分别为n=510和261）、一个基于家庭的研究（n=43和文献荟萃分析）、百万老兵计划（MVP）队列（n=94,885）以及英国生物银行（UKBB）（n=417,657）中研究了APOB的罕见变异。结果：在临床队列中，APOB变异在晚期MASLD患者中富集（OR 13.8, 95% CI: 2.7-70.7, P = 0.002），并与较低的循环血脂水平、但较高的MASLD活动度和纤维化程度相关（P < 0.05）。在家庭研究中，APOB变异与肝脏脂肪变性和纤维化共分离（P < 0.05）。对研究队列进行的跨种族荟萃分析显示，肝硬化和肝细胞癌（HCC）的汇总比值比（OR）分别为1.82（95% CI: 1.33-2.49）和3.53（95% CI: 2.09-5.98）。与同时影响ApoB48的变异相比，特异性影响ApoB100的变异对肝脏脂质代谢的影响要大3倍，并对冠状动脉疾病具有特异性保护作用（P < 0.05）。这些变异对肝硬化风险的影响相似，但影响ApoB48/100的变异对HCC的影响更大（P < 0.05）。结论：APOB罕见变异使个体易患晚期MASLD和HCC，这与极低密度脂蛋白（VLDL）和乳糜微粒分泌受扰的不同作用有关。这些发现揭示了肝脏和肠道脂质处理之间的相互作用，表明APOB基因分型可能有助于加强MASLD的风险分层和患者识别。资金来源：欧洲联盟、意大利卫生部、瑞典研究理事会、退伍军人健康管理局、美国国立卫生研究院。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过对多个大型队列的综合分析，系统性地阐明了APOB罕见变异在推动代谢功能障碍相关脂肪性肝病（MASLD）向肝硬化和肝细胞癌等严重阶段进展中的关键作用。研究的创新性在于，不仅确证了APOB变异是疾病进展的强风险因子，还首次区分了仅影响ApoB100（肝源性）与同时影响ApoB48/100（肝肠源性）的变异对肝脏、血脂及心血管结局的不同影响。这些发现深刻揭示了肝-肠脂质代谢轴在MASLD病理生理中的核心地位，并提示APOB基因分型可作为一种有潜力的生物标志物，用于MASLD患者的早期风险分层与精准识别。
