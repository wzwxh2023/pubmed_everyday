## 1. 低热量低蛋白喂养与标准热量蛋白喂养对伴休克的通气成年患者肾功能障碍的影响：一项NUTRIREA-3试验的事后分析。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41989569)
**期刊：** Intensive care medicine
**PMID：** 41989569
**DOI：** 10.1007/s00134-026-08369-7

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Low versus standard calorie and protein feeding and renal dysfunction in ventilated adults with shock: a NUTRIREA-3 post hoc analysis.

> **英文摘要：**
> PURPOSE: The optimal intake of artificial nutrition in critically ill patients remains unclear. While calorie and protein intakes affect glomerular function in patients with chronic kidney disease, their relation to renal function at the acute phase of intensive care is insufficiently documented. We aimed to study associations of a low-calorie and low-protein diet with renal outcomes in critically ill patients. METHODS: This post hoc analysis of the NUTRIREA-3 randomized-controlled trial included 3036 mechanically ventilated patients with shock. Calorie and protein intakes during the first 7 days were either low (6 kcal/kg and 0.2-0.4 g protein/kg/d) or standard (25 kcal/kg and 1.0-1.3 g protein/kg/d). The primary outcome was the incidence of acute kidney disease (AKD) during the ICU stay (up to ICU discharge or day 90 after inclusion, whichever occurred first). RESULTS: AKD during the ICU stay occurred in 669 (44.6%) low-group patients and 691 (46.1%) standard-group patients (hazard ratio, 0.97; 95% CI 0.88-1.07; P = 0.53). The highest urea level and the urea level at ICU discharge were significantly lower in the low group (P = 0.002). No differences were found for renal replacement therapy requirements or other renal outcomes. The results were similar in patients with early kidney dysfunction, severe organ failures, or end-stage chronic kidney disease. CONCLUSION: In critically ill patients with shock, early low-calorie and low-protein nutrition for 7 days was not associated with worse renal outcomes or mortality compared to standard feeding, even in patients with preexisting renal dysfunction.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：危重症患者人工营养的最佳摄入量仍不清楚。虽然热量和蛋白质的摄入会影响慢性肾病患者的肾小球功能，但其与重症监护急性期肾功能的关系尚未得到充分证明。我们旨在研究低热量低蛋白饮食与危重症患者肾脏结局的关联。方法：这项对NUTRIREA-3随机对照试验的事后分析纳入了3036名伴有休克的机械通气患者。在最初7天内，热量和蛋白质的摄入量分为低剂量组（6 kcal/kg和0.2-0.4 g/kg/d的蛋白质）或标准剂量组（25 kcal/kg和1.0-1.3 g/kg/d的蛋白质）。主要结局是ICU住院期间（截至ICU出院或入组后第90天，以先发生者为准）急性肾脏病（AKD）的发生率。结果：在ICU住院期间，低剂量组有669名（44.6%）患者发生AKD，标准剂量组有691名（46.1%）患者发生AKD（风险比为0.97；95% CI 0.88-1.07；P = 0.53）。低剂量组的最高尿素水平和ICU出院时的尿素水平显著更低（P = 0.002）。在肾脏替代治疗需求或其他肾脏结局方面未发现差异。在早期肾功能不全、严重器官衰竭或终末期慢性肾病的患者中，结果相似。结论：在伴有休克的危重症患者中，与标准喂养相比，为期7天的早期低热量低蛋白营养策略与更差的肾脏结局或死亡率无相关性，即使在预先存在肾功能不全的患者中也是如此。

## 2. 利用临床变量和人工智能辅助的放射学评估结核病病情严重程度

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41989328)
**期刊：** The Journal of infectious diseases
**PMID：** 41989328
**DOI：** 10.1093/infdis/jiag149

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Tuberculosis Disease Severity Assessment Using Clinical Variables and Radiology Enabled by Artificial Intelligence.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Chest X-ray (CXR) can assess pulmonary tuberculosis (TB) severity and may guide duration of treatment. However, the optimal radiological metric and its integration with clinical variables for predicting treatment outcomes remains unclear. METHODS: We used logistic regression to associate human-read and commercial artificial intelligence-generated CXR metrics with unfavorable outcome in the TB Portals real-world dataset (n = 2809). We assessed the standalone predictive accuracy for each of 10 radiological features for unfavorable outcomes, and combined the best-performing features with other clinical data. We fine-tuned ensembles of convolutional neural nets (CNNs) to automate human-read percent of lung involved (PLI) measurement directly from CXR images (n = 5261). RESULTS: Human-read PLI is the only CXR finding associated with outcome across drug resistance and human immunodeficiency virus (HIV) subgroups and is optimally combined with age, sex, and smear grade for predicting treatment outcome. PLI predicts outcomes better than cavitation (area under the curve [AUC], 0.654 vs 0.581, respectively), performs better than all tested Qure.ai automated features (qXR v2), and improves outcome prediction when added to sex + age + smear grade (ΔAUC, 0.028 [95% confidence interval, .007-.042]). The CNN ensemble for predicting PLI >25% achieves an AUC of 0.850. CONCLUSIONS: PLI performs better than cavitation as a radiological marker for predicting TB treatment outcome, and improves risk stratification when combined with key clinical variables. Automation of PLI can be predicted using CNNs to enable scalability and accurate assessment.

> **中文摘要：**
> 【背景】胸部X射线（CXR）可以评估肺结核（TB）的严重程度，并可能指导治疗持续时间。然而，用于预测治疗结果的最佳放射学指标及其与临床变量的整合方式尚不明确。【方法】我们使用逻辑回归分析了 TB Portals 真实世界数据集（n = 2809）中人工阅读和商业人工智能生成的 CXR 指标与不良结局的相关性。我们评估了 10 种放射学特征对不良结局的独立预测准确性，并将表现最佳的特征与其他临床数据相结合。我们微调了卷积神经网络（CNN）集成模型，以直接从 CXR 图像中自动测量人工阅读的受累肺部百分比（PLI）（n = 5261）。【结果】人工阅读的 PLI 是唯一在耐药和人类免疫缺陷病毒（HIV）亚组中均与结局相关的 CXR 发现，并且与年龄、性别和痰涂片分级结合时，在预测治疗结局方面达到最佳。PLI 对结局的预测优于空洞（曲线下面积 [AUC] 分别为 0.654 和 0.581），其表现优于所有测试的 Qure.ai 自动化特征（qXR v2），并且在添加到性别 + 年龄 + 痰涂片分级模型中时，改善了结局预测（ΔAUC 为 0.028 [95% 置信区间，0.007-0.042]）。用于预测 PLI >25% 的 CNN 集成模型实现了 0.850 的 AUC。【结论】作为预测结核病治疗结局的放射学标志物，PLI 的表现优于空洞，且在与关键临床变量结合时可改善风险分层。可以使用 CNN 对 PLI 进行自动化预测，从而实现可扩展性和准确评估。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究确立了“受累肺部百分比（PLI）”作为结核病预后预测的核心放射学指标，证明其预测效能显著优于传统的空洞特征。研究通过将PLI与年龄、性别及涂片分级等临床变量整合，构建了更精准的风险分层模型。最具创新性的是，研究利用深度学习技术实现了PLI的自动化评估，克服了人工阅片的主观性与不可扩展性，为结核病的数字化精准诊疗提供了重要证据。

**英文原标题：** A miniature magnetic switch unlocking multimodal, chip-free, and batteryless airway sensing.

> **英文摘要：**
> Continuously monitoring airway physiological properties in situ is critical for enabling early intervention. Unlike conventional approaches relying on on-board chips and batteries, chip-free sensing based on inductive coupling offers a promising pathway for fully wireless and battery-free monitoring. However, current chip-free strategies are limited in achieving multimodal sensing due to their narrow operational frequency ranges. Here, we introduce a miniature magnetic switch that enables wirelessly controlled channel switching through a cantilever beam mechanism, which anchors, rotates, and toggles between channels in response to external magnetic fields. Integrated with an inductive coil and capacitive sensors, the system generates resonance-based signals that can be selectively and wirelessly detected using a vector network analyzer (VNA). The concept is demonstrated through sensing tissue stiffness by switching between deformation and stress sensing modes, spatiotemporal mapping of tissue pressure, and multimodal sensing of multiple mucus properties. The proposed magnetic switch thus enables next-generation smart airway devices for continuous, minimally invasive, and multimodal sensing in diverse airway diseases.

## 8. ICU通气期间消化道的选择性去污染

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41159880)
**期刊：** The New England journal of medicine
**PMID：** 41159880
**DOI：** 10.1056/NEJMoa2506398

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Selective Decontamination of the Digestive Tract during Ventilation in the ICU.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Whether selective decontamination of the digestive tract (SDD) reduces mortality among patients undergoing mechanical ventilation and whether it adversely affects microbial ecology in the intensive care unit (ICU) remain unclear. In an earlier analysis of data from Australia, SDD did not result in a lower incidence of in-hospital death than standard care, but data from the full international trial are needed.
> 
> METHODS: We randomly assigned ICUs in Australia and Canada to use SDD or to continue standard care for two 12-month periods in patients undergoing mechanical ventilation. Patients in the SDD group received specific oral and gastric antimicrobial interventions for the duration of ventilation and an intravenous antibiotic agent for the first 4 days after enrollment. All other patients in the ICU were included in an observational ecologic assessment. Previously reported data from Australia are now combined with data from Canada. The primary outcome was in-hospital death from any cause at 90 days. The secondary clinical outcomes, assessed at 90 days, were death in the ICU and the number of days alive and free of mechanical ventilation, ICU admission, and hospitalization. Microbiologic secondary outcomes included new positive cultures for bloodstream infections and antibiotic-resistant organisms. For the ecologic assessment, the microbiologic outcomes were tested for noninferiority (noninferiority margin, 2 percentage points).
> 
> RESULTS: In this trial involving 20,000 patients in 26 ICUs, 9289 patients were enrolled in the randomized trial and 10,711 were included in the ecologic assessment. At 90 days, 1175 of 4215 patients (27.9%) in the SDD group and 1494 of 5065 (29.5%) in the standard-care group had died before hospital discharge (odds ratio, 0.94; 95% confidence interval [CI], 0.84 to 1.05; P = 0.27). New bloodstream infections occurred in 4.9% of the patients in the SDD group and in 6.8% of those in the standard-care group (adjusted mean difference, -1.30 percentage points; 95% CI, -2.55 to -0.05); antibiotic-resistant organisms were cultured in 16.8% and 26.8%, respectively (adjusted mean difference, -9.60 percentage points; 95% CI, -12.40 to -6.80). In the ecologic assessment, noninferiority of SDD was not confirmed for the development of new antibiotic-resistant organisms. Adverse events considered to be related to SDD or standard care were reported in 12 patients (0.3%) in the SDD group and in no patients in the standard-care group. Serious adverse events occurred in 47 patients (1.1%) and 59 patients (1.2%), respectively.
> 
> CONCLUSIONS: Among critically ill patients undergoing mechanical ventilation, SDD did not result in a lower incidence of in-hospital death than standard care. (Funded by the National Health and Medical Research Council of Australia and the Canadian Institutes of Health Research; ClinicalTrials.gov number, NCT02389036.).

> 背景：选择性消化道去污染（SDD）能否降低接受机械通气患者的死亡率，以及其是否对重症监护室（ICU）的微生物生态产生不利影响，目前尚不清楚。在早期对澳大利亚数据的分析中，SDD并未比标准治疗带来更低的院内死亡率，但仍需完整的国际试验数据。
> 
> 方法：我们在澳大利亚和加拿大的ICU中，随机分配其在两个为期12个月的周期内，对接受机械通气的患者采用SDD或继续标准治疗。SDD组患者在整个通气期间接受特定的口部和胃部抗菌干预，并在入组后的前4天接受静脉注射抗生素。ICU中所有其他患者均被纳入观察性生态学评估。先前报告的澳大利亚数据现与加拿大数据合并。主要结局是90天内任何原因导致的院内死亡。在90天评估的次要临床结局包括ICU内死亡，以及存活且无需机械通气、ICU住院和医院住院的天数。微生物学次要结局包括菌血症和耐药菌的新发阳性培养。对于生态学评估，微生物学结局进行了非劣效性检验（非劣效界值为2个百分点）。
> 
> 结果：该试验涉及26个ICU的20,000名患者，其中9289名患者被纳入随机试验，10711名患者被纳入生态学评估。在90天时，SDD组的4215名患者中有1175人（27.9%）在出院前死亡，而标准治疗组的5065名患者中有1494人（29.5%）死亡（比值比为0.94；95%置信区间[CI]为0.84至1.05；P=0.27）。SDD组有4.9%的患者出现新发菌血症，标准治疗组为6.8%（调整后平均差异为-1.30个百分点；95% CI为-2.55至-0.05）；两组分别在16.8%和26.8%的患者中培养出耐药菌（调整后平均差异为-9.60个百分点；95% CI为-12.40至-6.80）。在生态学评估中，SDD在产生新耐药菌方面的非劣效性未得到证实。被认为与SDD或标准治疗相关的不良事件在SDD组有12名患者（0.3%）报告，而标准治疗组没有。严重不良事件分别发生在47名（1.1%）和59名（1.2%）患者中。
> 
> 结论：在接受机械通气的危重患者中，与标准治疗相比，SDD并未导致更低的院内死亡率。（由澳大利亚国家健康与医学研究理事会和加拿大健康研究院资助；ClinicalTrials.gov注册号：NCT02389036。）

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

这项大规模、国际性的随机对照试验旨在明确选择性消化道去污染（SDD）对ICU机械通气患者死亡率的真实影响。研究通过在澳大利亚和加拿大的多家ICU中进行交叉设计，严谨地比较了SDD与标准治疗。核心发现是，尽管SDD能显著降低新发菌血症和耐药菌的检出率，但并未能有效降低患者在90天内的院内死亡率。 该研究的创新之处在于其庞大的样本量和跨国合作，为这一争议性话题提供了强有力的证据，但也提示其局限性，即生态学评估未能证实SDD在遏制新耐药菌出现方面的非劣效性，这对于临床推广构成了潜在顾虑。
