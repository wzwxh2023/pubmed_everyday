## 1. 神经元高兴奋性：揭示拉福拉病中海马突触功能障碍的关键

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41408964)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41408964
**DOI：** 10.1111/epi.70024

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Neuronal hyperexcitability: A key to unraveling hippocampal synaptic dysfunction in Lafora disease.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND AND OBJECTIVE: Lafora disease (LD) is a rare progressive disorder caused by mutations in the EPM2A or EPM2B genes, characterized by the accumulation of Lafora bodies, drug-resistant epilepsy, and cognitive decline. To investigate the early molecular mechanisms of LD, we studied electrophysiological changes in the dentate gyrus (DG) of the Epm2a knock-in mouse model at various ages.
> 
> METHODS: Electrophysiological recordings measured neuronal membrane properties, epileptic-like activity, epileptic thresholds, and synaptic plasticity in Epm2a mice at 1, 3, and 12 months. We also employed Periodic Acid-Schiff (PAS) diastase staining, immunofluorescence, and Western blotting to detect Lafora bodies, amyloid beta deposition, and the expression of glutamate receptor subunits.
> 
> RESULTS: Epileptic-like activity began at 1 month and intensified with age. Aberrant long-term potentiation (LTP) appeared at 3 months and worsened by 12 months. Notably, cannabidiol treatment reduced excitability and restored LTP in older mice, suggesting its potential therapeutic value.
> 
> SIGNIFICANCE: The reversibility of synaptopathy, even at advanced stages, reinforces the importance of early detection of hyperexcitability and the development of effective therapeutic approaches.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景与目的：拉福拉病（Lafora disease，LD）是一种由 EPM2A 或 EPM2B 基因突变引起的罕见进行性疾病，其特征为拉福拉小体的积聚、耐药性癫痫及认知衰退。为探究 LD 的早期分子机制，我们研究了不同年龄段 Epm2a 基因敲入小鼠齿状回（DG）的电生理变化。
> 
> 方法：通过电生理记录测定 1、3 和 12 月龄 Epm2a 小鼠的神经元膜特性、类癫痫样活动、癫痫阈值及突触可塑性。同时采用过碘酸-Schiff（PAS）-淀粉酶染色、免疫荧光及蛋白印迹（Western blot）检测拉福拉小体、淀粉样β沉积以及谷氨酸受体亚基的表达。
> 
> 结果：类癫痫样活动在 1 月龄时出现，且随年龄增长而加剧。异常的长时程增强（LTP）在 3 月龄时出现，并在 12 月龄时进一步恶化。值得注意的是，大麻二酚（CBD）治疗降低了兴奋性，并恢复了老年小鼠的 LTP，提示其潜在的治疗价值。
> 
> 意义：即使在疾病晚期，突触病变仍具有可逆性，这强化了早期检测高兴奋性及开发有效治疗手段的重要性。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究聚焦于拉福拉病的早期神经元电生理机制，系统评估了不同发育阶段 Epm2a 敲入小鼠的齿状回兴奋性及突触可塑性变化。结果显示神经元高兴奋性和 LTP 异常随着年龄进展而加重，大麻二酚可部分逆转这些异常，提示其治疗潜力。研究创新性地提出高兴奋性是突触功能障碍的早期标志，支持早期干预的重要性。局限性在于模型物种与人类病理之间仍需验证。

## 2. 颞叶癫痫中脑–心功能网络功能障碍：基于微状态的分析

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41405405)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41405405
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70059

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Brain-heart functional network dysfunction in temporal lobe epilepsy: A microstate-based analysis.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: Epilepsy patients face significantly elevated cardiovascular risks, with cardiac arrhythmias occurring 2-3 times more frequently than in the general population. Current knowledge of brain-heart functional coupling abnormalities in epilepsy, particularly during interictal periods, remains limited. We investigated brain-heart interplay characteristics in temporal lobe epilepsy through synchronized electroencephalographic-electrocardiographic analysis using a synthetic data generation model and microstate analysis.
> 
> METHODS: We enrolled 52 patients with temporal lobe epilepsy (mean age = 33.4 ± 12.7 years) and 42 age-matched healthy controls (mean age = 31.95 ± 11.03 years). Twenty-minute artifact-free electroencephalographic segments were analyzed during resting states. Four directional brain-heart coupling sequences were extracted: C, C, C, and C, representing bidirectional interactions between brain activity and cardiac components.
> 
> RESULTS: Six microstate topologies were consistently identified across all brain-heart interplay sequences, with temporal lobe epilepsy patients demonstrating significantly more complex and unstable topological characteristics compared to healthy controls. For C coupling, patients exhibited significantly reduced mean duration of microstate 3 (.34 ± .09 s vs. .38 ± .07 s, p = .02), increased occurrence rates of microstates 3 and 4 (both p < .001), and altered temporal coverage patterns. Similar abnormalities were observed across all sequences, with patients showing shortened microstate durations, altered occurrence rates, and disrupted temporal coverage. Spatial dissimilarity analysis revealed significant topological abnormalities across all microstates. A logistic regression model incorporating microstate parameters achieved 94.7% diagnostic accuracy for temporal lobe epilepsy, with an F1 score of .952 and an area under the curve of .932.
> 
> SIGNIFICANCE: Temporal lobe epilepsy is characterized by profound disruptions in brain-heart functional coupling during interictal periods, manifesting as altered microstate topographies and temporal dynamics. These findings establish microstate-based analysis as a promising framework for characterizing brain-heart axis dysfunction in epilepsy.

> **中文摘要：**
> 研究目的：癫痫患者面临显著升高的心血管风险，心律失常的发生率较一般人群高出2至3倍。当前关于癫痫患者脑-心功能耦合异常的认识，尤其是在发作间期，仍然有限。本研究通过合成数据生成模型和基于微状态的分析，利用同步脑电图和心电图分析，探讨颞叶癫痫中脑-心相互作用的特征。
> 
> 研究方法：共纳入52例颞叶癫痫患者（平均年龄33.4 ± 12.7岁）及42例年龄匹配的健康对照（平均年龄31.95 ± 11.03岁）。在静息状态下分析20分钟无伪迹的脑电图片段。提取了四种方向性的脑-心耦合序列：C、C、C和C，分别代表脑活动与心脏成分之间的双向交互。
> 
> 研究结果：在所有脑-心相互作用序列中均一致识别出六种微状态拓扑结构。与健康对照相比，颞叶癫痫患者表现出显著更复杂且更不稳定的拓扑特征。在C耦合中，患者的微状态3平均持续时间显著缩短（0.34 ± 0.09秒 vs. 0.38 ± 0.07秒，p = 0.02），微状态3和4的出现频率显著增高（均p < 0.001），且时间覆盖模式发生改变。类似异常在所有序列中均有观察到，表现为微状态持续时间缩短、出现率改变及时间覆盖受损。空间差异性分析显示，所有微状态均存在显著拓扑异常。结合微状态参数的逻辑回归模型对颞叶癫痫的诊断准确率达94.7%，F1评分为0.952，曲线下面积为0.932。
> 
> 研究意义：颞叶癫痫患者在发作间期表现为脑-心功能耦合的显著紊乱，这种紊乱体现在微状态拓扑结构和时间动态的改变。这些发现表明，基于微状态的分析可成为刻画癫痫中脑-心轴功能障碍的有前景的新方法。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究创新性地结合脑电与心电信号，基于微状态分析框架，揭示了颞叶癫痫患者在发作间期存在显著的脑-心功能紊乱。方法学上，采用方向耦合分析和机器学习模型，准确区分了患者与健康人群。结果显示脑-心网络的拓扑与动态特征均异常，为癫痫相关自主神经功能障碍提供了新的量化指标。研究具有较高的诊断潜力，但样本量与可推广性仍需进一步验证。
