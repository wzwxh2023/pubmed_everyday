## 1. 体外膜肺氧合患者的肾脏替代治疗：一篇叙述性综述。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42069650)
**期刊：** Critical care (London, England)

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Renal replacement therapy in patients under extracorporeal membrane oxygenation: a narrative review.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: The use of extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) has expanded for severe respiratory and circulatory failure. Acute kidney injury (AKI) is one of the most frequent complications. Up to 85% of ECMO patients develop AKI and approximately half of them require renal replacement therapy (RRT) making a comprehensive understanding of both therapies and their interaction essential for patient management. However, evidence to guide ECMO-specific RRT strategies remains limited.
> 
> MAIN BODY: ECMO-related AKI arises from a complex interplay between patient and circuit factors that promote inflammation, endothelial injury, and tubular damage. Timing of RRT initiation in ECMO patients often relies on criteria used in the general critically ill population. Fluid overload is consistently associated with worse outcomes, and observational data suggest that earlier RRT, primarily to control fluid balance, may improve survival although randomized trials in ECMO patients are lacking. All RRT modalities can theoretically be used, but continuous techniques are preferred. RRT can be delivered via a parallel circuit using a dedicated venous catheter or integrated into the ECMO circuit. Integrated configurations reduce the need for additional vascular access and may prolong filter lifespan but require careful pressure management and team expertise to avoid alarms, hemolysis, and air embolism. Anticoagulation strategies must balance bleeding and thrombosis risk across both circuits; unfractionated heparin remains standard, while regional citrate anticoagulation can safely extend filter lifespan in selected patients although data is lacking in patients under veno-arterial ECMO. RRT during ECMO is associated with higher short-term mortality and an increased burden of chronic kidney disease among survivors.
> 
> CONCLUSIONS: The management of AKI in ECMO patients remains a major clinical challenge. While RRT is often required in this population, optimal strategies for its initiation, modality selection, and integration with ECMO circuits are still evolving. Current evidence underscores the need for individualized approaches based on patient characteristics. Future research should focus on defining standardized protocols for RRT implementation in ECMO, use of regional citrate anticoagulation, optimizing patient selection, and evaluating long-term renal and survival outcomes.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：体外膜肺氧合（ECMO）的应用已扩展至严重呼吸和循环衰竭的治疗。急性肾损伤（AKI）是最常见的并发症之一。高达85%的ECMO患者会发生AKI，其中约半数需要肾脏替代治疗（RRT），这使得全面理解两种疗法及其相互作用对患者管理至关重要。然而，指导ECMO特异性RRT策略的证据仍然有限。
> 
> 正文：ECMO相关的AKI源于患者与体外循环管路因素之间复杂的相互作用，这些因素促进了炎症、内皮损伤和肾小管损害。ECMO患者启动RRT的时机通常依赖于普通重症患者群体所使用的标准。液体超负荷与较差的预后持续相关，观察性数据表明，主要为控制液体平衡而早期进行RRT可能会提高生存率，尽管缺乏针对ECMO患者的随机试验。理论上所有RRT模式均可使用，但首选连续性技术。RRT可通过使用专用静脉导管的并联管路或整合到ECMO管路中进行。整合配置减少了额外血管通路的需要，并可能延长滤器寿命，但需要精细的压力管理和团队专业知识以避免警报、溶血和空气栓塞。抗凝策略必须在两个循环管路中平衡出血和血栓形成的风险；普通肝素仍是标准方法，而局部枸橼酸盐抗凝可在特定患者中安全地延长滤器寿命，尽管在接受静脉-动脉ECMO的患者中数据尚缺。ECMO期间的RRT与较高的短期死亡率以及幸存者中慢性肾脏病负担的增加相关。
> 
> 结论：ECMO患者中AKI的管理仍然是一个重大的临床挑战。虽然该人群常需要RRT，但其启动、模式选择以及与ECMO管路整合的最佳策略仍在发展中。现有证据强调了基于患者特征采取个体化方法的必要性。未来的研究应侧重于为ECMO中实施RRT制定标准化方案、应用局部枸橼酸盐抗凝、优化患者选择以及评估长期肾脏和生存结局。
