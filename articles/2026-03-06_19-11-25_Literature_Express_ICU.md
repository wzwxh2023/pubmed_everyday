## 1. 驱动压限制策略对ARDS患者预后的影响：随机对照试验的荟萃分析

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41787551)
**期刊：** Critical care (London, England)
**PMID：** 41787551
**DOI：** 10.1186/s13054-026-05876-3

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Effect of driving pressure-limiting strategies on outcomes of patients with ARDS: a meta-analysis of randomized controlled trials.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Lower driving pressure is associated with better outcomes in patients with acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS) based on observational studies. However, individual randomized controlled trials provided inconclusive evidence. We synthesized evidence from randomized controlled trials to examine whether implementation of driving pressure-limiting strategies is feasible and improves outcomes. METHODS: This meta-analysis was registered with PROSPERO (CRD420251141653). PubMed, Scopus, CENTRAL and references were searched for trials comparing driving pressure-limiting strategies on top of lung protective ventilation ("intervention" group) versus lung protective ventilation alone ("control" group) in ARDS. Outcomes were feasibility of intervention, all-cause mortality, ventilator-free days and length of intensive care unit (ICU) stay. RESULTS: Four trials, enrolling 431 patients and implementing heterogenous driving pressure-limiting strategies (namely, tidal volume reduction and/or positive end-expiratory pressure titration), were included. There were no statistically significant mean differences in post-randomization driving pressure (namely, - 2.17, - 2.09 and - 2.15 cmHO on study day 1, 2 and 3, respectively) between groups. There was no statistically significant difference between "intervention" versus "control" group in terms of mortality [35.7% versus 39.0%; relative risk 0.63, 95% confidence interval (CI) 0.20 to 1.94, p = 0.28] or ventilator-free days (p = 0.88). Driving pressure-limiting strategies were associated with shorter length of ICU stay (mean difference - 2.40 days, 95% CI - 3.31 to - 1.49, p = 0.004). CONCLUSIONS: When lung protective ventilation is already applied, further limitation of driving pressure may be hard to achieve. This inability to meaningfully limit driving pressure might explain the neutral effect of driving pressure-limiting strategies on outcomes, such as mortality and ventilator-free days.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：根据观察性研究，较低的驱动压与急性呼吸窘迫综合征（ARDS）患者较好的预后相关。然而，各别随机对照试验提供的证据并不确切。我们综合了随机对照试验的证据，以探讨实施驱动压限制策略是否可行以及是否能改善预后。方法：该荟萃分析已在PROSPERO注册（CRD420251141653）。在PubMed、Scopus、CENTRAL和参考文献中搜索了在肺保护性通气基础上比较驱动压限制策略（“干预”组）与单纯肺保护性通气（“对照”组）治疗ARDS的试验。结局指标包括干预的可行性、全因死亡率、无呼吸机天数和重症监护病房（ICU）住院时间。结果：纳入了四项试验，共涉及431名患者，并实施了异质性的驱动压限制策略（即减少潮气量和/或滴定呼气末正压）。两组间在随机化后的驱动压方面（即研究第1、2和3天分别减少-2.17、-2.09和-2.15 cmH2O）无统计学显著的平均差异。在死亡率方面，“干预”组与“对照”组之间无统计学显著差异[35.7%对39.0%；相对风险 0.63，95%置信区间 (CI) 0.20至1.94，p = 0.28]，无呼吸机天数也无显著差异 (p = 0.88)。驱动压限制策略与较短的ICU住院时间相关（平均差异 -2.40天，95% CI -3.31至-1.49，p = 0.004）。结论：当已经应用肺保护性通气时，可能难以进一步限制驱动压。这种无法显著限制驱动压的情况可能解释了驱动压限制策略对死亡率和无呼吸机天数等结局产生中性效应的原因。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本项荟萃分析评估了在常规肺保护性通气基础上进一步限制驱动压对ARDS患者的临床获益。研究发现，在已实施标准肺保护性通气的背景下，进一步降低驱动压在临床实践中难以实现，且未能显著改善患者的死亡率或无呼吸机天数，仅观察到ICU住院时间的缩短。该结果提示，虽然驱动压是重要的预后指标，但其作为直接治疗靶点的有效性受限于临床实施难度，为优化ARDS机械通气策略提供了审慎的参考。

## 4. 鉴定 CD164 为趋异腺相关病毒的关键进入受体

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41785320)
**期刊：** Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America
**PMID：** 41785320
**DOI：** 10.1073/pnas.2525865123

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Identification of CD164 as an essential entry receptor for divergent adeno-associated viruses.

> **英文摘要：**
> Recombinant adeno-associated viruses (rAAVs) are widely used for in vivo gene delivery. While KIAA0319L, known as AAV receptor (AAVR), is essential for the transduction of multiserotype AAVs, it is dispensable for AAV4-related (Clade G) AAVs. We conducted a genome-wide CRISPR/Cas9 screen and identified CD164, a type I transmembrane sialomucin, as an essential entry receptor for Clade G AAVs. Ablation of CD164 expression substantially impaired both entry and transduction of Clade G AAVs. CD164-targeting antibodies and soluble CD164 ectodomain effectively blocked transduction. AAV4 capsids colocalized with CD164 at the plasma membrane and in endosomal compartments. In vitro, CD164 interacted with AAV4 or AAVrh32.33 capsids at high affinity. Importantly, systemic administration of rAAV4 or rAAVrh32.33 in knockout (KO) mice resulted in nearly complete loss of transgene expression. These findings establish CD164 as an essential entry receptor for Clade G AAV vectors and uncover a distinct AAVR-independent mechanism of AAV tropism.

> **中文摘要：**
> 重组腺相关病毒 (rAAVs) 广泛用于体内基因递送。虽然被称为 AAV 受体 (AAVR) 的 KIAA0319L 对多种血清型 AAV 的转导至关重要，但它对 AAV4 相关（G 进化支）AAV 并非必需。我们进行了全基因组 CRISPR/Cas9 筛选，并鉴定出 I 型跨膜唾液酸粘蛋白 CD164 是 G 进化支 AAV 的关键进入受体。CD164 表达的敲除显著损害了 G 进化支 AAV 的进入和转导。靶向 CD164 的抗体和可溶性 CD164 胞外结构域能有效阻断转导。AAV4 衣壳在质膜和内体区室中与 CD164 共定位。在体外，CD164 与 AAV4 或 AAVrh32.33 衣壳表现出高亲和力相互作用。重要的是，在敲除 (KO) 小鼠中系统给药 rAAV4 或 rAAVrh32.33 会导致转基因表达几乎完全丧失。这些发现确定了 CD164 是 G 进化支 AAV 载体的关键进入受体，并揭示了一种独特且独立于 AAVR 的 AAV 嗜性机制。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过全基因组 CRISPR 筛选，成功鉴定了 CD164 是 AAV4 相关进化支（Clade G）病毒的关键受体，填补了长期以来对 AAVR 非依赖性感染机制的认知空白。研究通过体外生化实验、细胞成像以及 CD164 敲除小鼠模型，多维度且严谨地验证了 CD164 在病毒进入和转导过程中的核心作用。这一发现不仅深入阐明了不同 AAV 血清型的组织趋性差异，也为优化针对特定组织、逃避预存免疫的下一代基因治疗载体设计提供了关键的分子靶点。
