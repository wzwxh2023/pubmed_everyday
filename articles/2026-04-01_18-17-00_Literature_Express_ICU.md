## 5. 慢性感染干扰抗病毒B细胞的亲和力等级体系

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41915741)
**期刊：** Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America
**PMID：** 41915741
**DOI：** 10.1073/pnas.2532422123

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Chronic infection perturbs the affinity hierarchy of antiviral B cells.

> **英文摘要：**
> A key function of the germinal center (GC) reaction consists in the preferential expansion and enrichment of high-affinity B cell clones. Whether and how persistent viral infection thwarts this purpose remains ill-defined. Here, we transferred monoclonal lymphocytic choriomeningitis virus-specific B cells into persistently infected hosts. High-affinity B cells expanded vigorously, forming GCs and abundant antibody-secreting cells. When failing to clear viremia or to drive viral mutational escape, the expanded B cell population contracted, ending in its quasi-complete disappearance from the spleen, a process we termed "attrition." In stark contrast, low-affinity B cells expanded and persisted irrespective of high viral loads. B cell attrition was associated with phenotypic and transcriptional alterations including a prominent Blimp-1 transcriptional signature in high-affinity GC B cells. Blimp-1-deficient B cells were resistant to attrition, suggesting a B cell-intrinsic process. Moreover, exogenously supplied antibody feedback prevented attrition, suggesting that the latter may have resulted from excessive antigenic stimulation. Our findings suggest that in chronic viral infection, the incessant exposure to overwhelming amounts of antigen perturbs B cell affinity hierarchies by preferentially dysregulating high-affinity B cells.

> **中文摘要：**
> 生发中心（GC）反应的一个核心功能在于优先扩增和富集高亲和力B细胞克隆。持续性病毒感染是否以及如何挫败这一目标仍不明确。在本研究中，我们将单克隆淋巴细胞性脉络丛脑膜炎病毒特异性B细胞转移到持续感染的宿主体内。结果显示，高亲和力B细胞剧烈扩增，形成了生发中心和大量的抗体分泌细胞。然而，当未能清除病毒血症或驱动病毒突变逃逸时，扩增的B细胞群体发生收缩，最终在脾脏中几乎完全消失，我们将这一过程称为“耗损（attrition）”。与之形成鲜明对比的是，低亲和力B细胞无论病毒载量高低，均能发生扩增并持久存在。B细胞耗损与表型和转录组学改变有关，包括高亲和力GC B细胞中显著的Blimp-1转录特征。Blimp-1缺失的B细胞对耗损具有抗性，这表明该过程属于B细胞内在程序。此外，外源提供的抗体反馈防止了耗损，提示后者可能源于过度的抗原刺激。我们的研究结果表明，在慢性病毒感染中，持续暴露于压倒性的抗原量会通过优先失调高亲和力B细胞，从而干扰B细胞的亲和力等级体系。

## 7. 苯磺酸瑞马唑仑与丙泊酚用于ICU短期浅镇静的比较（SHOSREB）：一项多中心、随机、单盲、对照试验

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Short-term light sedation with remimazolam besylate versus propofol in the ICU (SHOSREB): a multicentre, randomized, single-blind, controlled trial.

> **英文摘要：**
> PURPOSE: To explore the efficacy and safety of remimazolam besylate in short-term sedation in the ICU in comparison with propofol.
> 
> METHOD: A multicenter, randomized, single-blind, non-inferiority trial was conducted in mechanically ventilated patients, who needed light sedation, defined as Richmond Agitation-Sedation Scale (RASS) scores between -2 and + 1. The primary outcome was successful sedation, which was defined as no rescue sedative administered during the study drug infusion period while maintaining RASS scores between -2 and + 1 for at least 70% of the total infusion time.
> 
> RESULTS: A total of 164 participants were included and randomized equally into the remimazolam group and the propofol group. Remimazolam was continuously infused for 10.5 [8.1-14.7] h and propofol for 11.0 [7.0-14.3] h (p = 0.534). The median average maintaining dose of remimazolam and propofol was 0.20 [0.19-0.30] mg·kg⁻·h⁻ and 0.61 [0.30-1.17] mg·kg⁻·h⁻, respectively. The percentage of participants successfully sedated in both groups was 97.5% (p = 0.497) with a difference of 0 (95% confidence interval (CI) -6.5% to 6.4%), above the predefined non-inferiority margin of -8%. There was no significant difference in the mean percentage of time with target RASS scores between the remimazolam group (88.6%; 95% CI: 84.2%-92.9%) and the propofol group (89.4%; 95% CI: 85.0%-93.8%) (p = 0.543).
>

## 76. 遗传证据揭示血浆磷脂酰胆碱水平与脓毒症相关死亡率之间的因果关联，且免疫细胞介导了这一关联。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40333460)
**期刊：** Shock (Augusta, Ga.)
**PMID：** 40333460
**DOI：** 10.1097/SHK.0000000000002617

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Genetic Evidence Reveals a Causal Association Between Plasma Phosphatidylcholine Levels and Sepsis Associated Mortality, With Immune Cells Mediating This Association.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Sepsis is a critical and life-threatening clinical syndrome. Abnormal plasma lipid levels are associated with disease progression. However, causal links remain uncertain due to limitations of observational studies. METHODS: This study conducted a comprehensive two-sample Mendelian randomization (MR) analysis utilizing publicly available pooled genome-wide association study data on 179 plasma phospholipid subtypes, 731 immune cell phenotypes, and genetic data related to sepsis-related mortality outcomes. The causal associations between plasma lipids, immune cell phenotypes, and the risk of sepsis-related death were investigated. RESULTS: MR results demonstrated significant causal associations between five subtypes of phosphatidylcholine (PC) and one sphingomyelin (SM) and the clinical outcome of 28-day mortality in sepsis patients. Notably, PC (16:0_18:2), PC (18:0_18:1), PC (18:0_20:4), PC (O-16:1_16:0), and SM (d34:0) demonstrated a protective effect in reducing the risk of 28-day mortality ( P  < 0.05). Conversely, PC (16:1_20:4) was associated with an increased risk of 28-day mortality in sepsis patients( P  < 0.05). Comprehensive sensitivity analyses confirmed the robustness of these results. The inverse Mendelian randomization analysis did not reveal a significant causal effect of sepsis on the aforementioned lipid profile. Mediation analysis identified two key immune cell-mediated factors, including circulating CD39 on CD39+ CD8br cells and CX3CR1 on CD14+ CD16+ monocytes, as mediators of the reduced risk of sepsis-associated mortality due to PC (O-16:1_16:0), with mediation proportions of 17.4% and 13.6%, respectively. CONCLUSIONS: Our study supports a causal relationship between plasma lipid metabolism disorders and sepsis-associated mortality and provides new insights into the critical role of immune cell alterations induced by lipid metabolism disorders in sepsis progression.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：脓毒症是一种危急且危及生命的临床综合征。异常的血浆脂质水平与疾病进展相关。然而，由于观察性研究的局限性，因果联系仍不确定。方法：本研究利用公开获取的 179 种血浆磷脂亚型、731 种免疫细胞表型以及与脓毒症相关死亡结局相关的遗传数据的汇总全基因组关联研究（GWAS）数据，进行了全面的两样本孟德尔随机化（MR）分析。调查了血浆脂质、免疫细胞表型与脓毒症相关死亡风险之间的因果关联。结果：MR 结果显示，五种磷脂酰胆碱（PC）亚型和一种鞘磷脂（SM）与脓毒症患者 28 天死亡率的临床结局之间存在显著的因果关联。值得注意的是，PC (16:0_18:2)、PC (18:0_18:1)、PC (18:0_20:4)、PC (O-16:1_16:0) 和 SM (d34:0) 在降低 28 天死亡风险方面表现出保护作用（P < 0.05）。相反，PC (16:1_20:4) 与脓毒症患者 28 天死亡风险增加相关（P < 0.05）。全面的敏感性分析证实了这些结果的稳健性。逆向孟德尔随机化分析未发现脓毒症对上述脂质谱有显著的因果效应。中介分析确定了两个关键的免疫细胞介导因子，包括 CD39+ CD8br 细胞上的循环 CD39 和 CD14+ CD16+ 单核细胞上的 CX3CR1，它们是 PC (O-16:1_16:0) 降低脓毒症相关死亡风险的中介因素，中介比例分别为 17.4% 和 13.6%。结论：我们的研究支持血浆脂质代谢紊乱与脓毒症相关死亡率之间的因果关系，并为脂质代谢紊乱诱导的免疫细胞改变在脓毒症进展中的关键作用提供了新见解。
