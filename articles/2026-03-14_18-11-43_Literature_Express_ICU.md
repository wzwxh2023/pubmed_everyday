**期刊：** The Journal of infectious diseases
**PMID：** 41824657
**DOI：** 10.1093/infdis/jiag025

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Emergence of Carbapenem-Resistant Escherichia coli Coharboring blaCTX-M-199 and Penicillin-Binding Protein 3 Insertion Conferring Increased Aztreonam/Avibactam Resistance in South China.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Infections caused by carbapenem-resistant Escherichia coli (CREC) pose a serious global public health threat due to limited therapeutic options. This study aimed to investigate the molecular epidemiology and the susceptibility to novel β-lactam/β-lactamase inhibitor combinations in clinical CREC isolates. METHODS: Whole-genome sequencing analysis including multilocus sequence types, antimicrobial resistance genes, serotypes and phylogeny was conducted. Antimicrobial susceptibility testing was performed by the broth microdilution method. Filter mating assays were performed to evaluate the conjugation of the plasmid. RESULTS: We identified an emerging extended-spectrum β-lactamase-encoding gene, blaCTX-M-199, in 8.9% (26/291) of CREC strains isolated in South China. Notably, 96.2% (25/26) of blaCTX-M-199-positive CREC strains exhibited aztreonam/avibactam (ATM/AVI) nonsusceptibility, with 38.5% (10/26) being fully resistant. All 26 blaCTX-M-199-positive strains harbored blaNDM-5 and YRIK/YRIN insertions of penicillin-binding protein 3, belonging to ST410 (n = 10) and ST167 (n = 16). Whole-genome sequencing revealed that blaCTX-M-199 can be located on the chromosome or on transferable plasmids. Transconjugants exhibited a 2- to 4-fold increase in the minimum inhibitory concentration of ATM/AVI. CONCLUSIONS: These findings suggest that blaCTX-M-199 contributes to reduced ATM/AVI susceptibility and, in synergy with YRIK/YRIN insertions, likely confers significant ATM/AVI resistance. Notably, CREC strains coharboring blaCTX-M-199 and YRIK/YRIN insertions are emerging within international high-risk clones ST410 and ST167. Therefore, our study highlights the importance of monitoring the prevalence of blaCTX-M-199-harboring CREC and implementing its detection in clinical practice.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：由于治疗方案有限，由耐碳青霉烯类大肠埃希菌（CREC）引起的感染构成了严重的全球公共卫生威胁。本研究旨在调查临床 CREC 分离株的分子流行病学以及对新型 β-内酰胺/β-内酰胺酶抑制剂组合的敏感性。方法：进行了全基因组测序分析，包括多位点序列类型、抗生素耐药基因、血清型和系统发育分析。通过微量肉汤稀释法进行抗生素敏感性试验。进行滤纸接合试验以评估质粒的接合转移。结果：我们在华南地区分离的 8.9% (26/291) CREC 菌株中发现了一种新兴的超广谱 β-内酰胺酶编码基因 blaCTX-M-199。值得注意的是，96.2% (25/26) 的 blaCTX-M-199 阳性 CREC 菌株表现出对氨曲南/阿维巴坦 (ATM/AVI) 的非敏感性，其中 38.5% (10/26) 为完全耐药。所有 26 株 blaCTX-M-199 阳性菌株均携带 blaNDM-5 和青霉素结合蛋白 3 的 YRIK/YRIN 插入，属于 ST410 (n = 10) 和 ST167 (n = 16)。全基因组测序显示 blaCTX-M-199 可定位于染色体或可转移质粒上。接合子表现出 ATM/AVI 的最小抑菌浓度增加了 2 到 4 倍。结论：这些研究结果表明，blaCTX-M-199 有助于降低 ATM/AVI 的敏感性，并与 YRIK/YRIN 插入协同作用，可能导致显著的 ATM/AVI 耐药。值得注意的是，协同携带 blaCTX-M-199 和 YRIK/YRIN 插入的 CREC 菌株正在国际高风险克隆 ST410 和 ST167 中出现。因此，我们的研究强调了监测携带 blaCTX-M-199 的 CREC 流行情况并在临床实践中实施其检测的重要性。

## 4. 中国浙江省多重耐药鲍曼不动杆菌的分子流行病学与持久性（2015-2020）：耐药性、毒力及传播动力学的见解

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41824655)
**期刊：** The Journal of infectious diseases
**PMID：** 41824655
**DOI：** 10.1093/infdis/jiaf425

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Molecular Epidemiology and Persistence of Multidrug-Resistant Acinetobacter baumannii in Zhejiang, China (2015-2020): Insights Into Resistance, Virulence, and Transmission Dynamics.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: This study investigate the molecular epidemiology and population characteristics of 184 Acinetobacter baumannii clinical isolates collected in Zhejiang (2015-2020), shedding light on the persistence and success of ST208-KL2 lineage. METHODS: Whole-genome sequencing and bioinformatic analysis characterized the population structure and transmission dynamics of 184 A. baumannii. Plasmid analysis identified the surrounding structures of blaOXA-58 and blaNDM-1 genes. Serum inhibition assays and Galleria mellonella virulence experiments, combined with global epidemiological analysis, explored the persistence and dominance of ST208-KL2. RESULTS: Of the isolates, approximately 78.8% (145/184) exhibited carbapenem resistance, primarily driven by blaOXA-23, blaOXA-72, blaOXA-58, and blaNDM-1. The A. baumannii population was divided into 2 clusters, distinguished by differences in ST types and resistance islands profiles. Cluster2 accounted for 71 department-time transmission events, mainly involving long-term interdepartmental spread, particularly in the rehabilitation medicine department and intensive care unit. Recombination analysis revealed that recombination occurred mainly in capsular locus (KL) regions, prophage regions, and predicted protein regions. Phenotypic experiments found that ST208-KL2 isolates displayed increased virulence and greater resistance to serum inhibition compared to ST208-KL7 isolates. This finding likely explains its persistently higher prevalence throughout the study period, except in 2019. CONCLUSIONS: Cluster2 isolates demonstrated significant advantages in ARG prevalence, resistance rates, and transmission capacity. ST208-KL2, with its superior serum resistance and virulence compared to ST208-KL7, has emerged as a dominant lineage with global implications for infection control and public health.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：本研究调查了2015年至2020年间浙江地区收集的184株鲍曼不动杆菌临床分离株的分子流行病学和群体特征，阐明了ST208-KL2谱系的持久性及其成功的机制。方法：通过全基因组测序和生物信息学分析，对184株鲍曼不动杆菌的群体结构和传播动力学进行了表征。质粒分析鉴定了blaOXA-58和blaNDM-1基因的周边结构。结合全球流行病学分析，通过血清抑制试验和大蜡螟毒力实验探讨了ST208-KL2的持久性和优势地位。结果：在分离株中，约78.8% (145/184) 表现出碳青霉烯类耐药性，主要由blaOXA-23、blaOXA-72、blaOXA-58和blaNDM-1基因驱动。鲍曼不动杆菌群体分为2个簇，其区别在于ST类型和耐药岛谱图的差异。簇2（Cluster2）涉及71次科室时间传播事件，主要涉及长期的跨科室扩散，特别是在康复医学科和重症监护室。重组分析显示，重组主要发生在荚膜基因座（KL）区域、前噬菌体区域和预测蛋白区域。表型实验发现，与ST208-KL7分离株相比，ST208-KL2分离株显示出更强的毒力和对血清抑制更强的耐药性。这一发现可能解释了其在除2019年外的整个研究期间持续较高流行率的原因。结论：簇2分离株在耐药基因（ARG）流行率、耐药率和传播能力方面表现出显著优势。与ST208-KL7相比，ST208-KL2具有卓越的血清抗性和毒力，已成为一种具有全球感染控制和公共卫生影响的优势谱系。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过对浙江地区六年间鲍曼不动杆菌分离株的纵向监测，系统揭明了ST208-KL2谱系的演变与流行机制。研究综合运用全基因组测序、传播动力学分析及生物学表型实验，精准识别了驱动耐药性传播的关键耐药基因（如blaOXA-58, blaNDM-1）及其质粒环境。其核心贡献在于通过对比实验阐明了ST208-KL2相对于ST208-KL7在血清抗性和毒力上的竞争优势，为多重耐药菌（MDR）在医院环境中的精准防控提供了重要的科学依据。

## 5. 中国某教学医院引起血流感染的耐碳青霉烯类肺炎克雷伯菌的临床及分子特征研究

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Clinical and Molecular Characteristics of Carbapenem-Resistant Klebsiella pneumoniae Causing Bloodstream Infection in a Chinese Teaching Hospital.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Bloodstream infection (BSI) caused by carbapenem-resistant Klebsiella pneumoniae (CRKP) is a serious and urgent threat for hospitalized patients. This study aims to describe the clinical and molecular characteristics of CRKP causing BSI in a tertiary care hospital in Chengdu, China. METHODS: In total, 123 nonconsecutive CRKP strains were collected in the hospital from 2020 to 2022 and sent for whole genome sequencing. String tests were performed of all CRKP strains, and clinical information of relevant cases was collected. RESULTS: Among the 123 CRKP strains collected, the median age of the patients was 53 years, 77.2% of patients with CRKP BSI were elderly men, 62.6% were admitted to the intensive care unit, 80.5% underwent intravascular catheters procedures, and 86.7% had underlying lung diseases. The rate of 30-day mortality was 21.1%. Microbiological characteristics showed that carbapenem resistance was primarily mediated by carbapenemase acquisition, predominantly blaKPC-2 (93.5%). The 123 CRKP isolates comprised 9 sequence types (STs) and 10 serotypes, with ST11 being dominant (84.6%). The serotype KL64 accounted for 73.2%, followed by KL47 at 7.3%. The positive rate of the string test was 13.8%, and virulence genes (rmpA2, iuc-iutA) were detected in 48.8% of ST11-KL64 strains. Phylogenetic analysis indicated that most ST11 strains were closely related. Genomic transmission analyses revealed that 6.7% of pairs had ≤20 pairwise single-nucleotide polymorphisms and were thus designated as "highly likely" transmission. CONCLUSIONS: This study poses an urgent need for enhancing infection control measures in the hospital, especially CRKP strains with hypervirulent genes.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：由耐碳青霉烯类肺炎克雷伯菌（CRKP）引起的血流感染（BSI）对住院患者构成了严重且紧急的威胁。本研究旨在描述中国成都一家三级医院内引起血流感染的CRKP的临床和分子特征。方法：2020年至2022年期间，在该医院共收集了123株非连续的CRKP菌株，并进行了全基因组测序。对所有CRKP菌株进行了拉网试验，并收集了相关病例的临床信息。结果：在收集的123株CRKP菌株中，患者的中位年龄为53岁，77.2%的CRKP血流感染患者为老年男性，62.6%被收入重症监护病房，80.5%接受了血管内导管操作，86.7%患有基础肺部疾病。30天死亡率为21.1%。微生物特征显示，碳青霉烯类耐药主要通过获得碳青霉烯酶介导，其中以blaKPC-2为主（93.5%）。123株CRKP分离株包含9个序列类型（STs）和10个血清型，其中ST11占主导地位（84.6%）。血清型KL64占73.2%，其次是KL47占7.3%。拉网试验阳性率为13.8%，在48.8%的ST11-KL64菌株中检测到毒力基因（rmpA2, iuc-iutA）。进化树分析表明，大多数ST11菌株亲缘关系密切。基因组传播分析显示，6.7%的菌株对具有≤20个两两单核苷酸多态性，因此被指定为“极有可能”传播。结论：本研究提示急需加强医院的感染控制措施，特别是针对携带高毒力基因的CRKP菌株。

## 6. 高毒力耐碳青霉烯类肺炎克雷伯菌感染的临床结局：一项队列研究

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41824648)
**期刊：** The Journal of infectious diseases
**PMID：** 41824648
**DOI：** 10.1093/infdis/jiaf460

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Clinical Outcomes in Hypervirulent Carbapenem-Resistant Klebsiella pneumoniae Infection: A Cohort Study.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Klebsiella pneumoniae is a major pathogen with carbapenem resistance and hypervirulence as common evolutional pathways leading to poor outcomes. Hypervirulent carbapenem-resistant K. pneumoniae (hvCRKP) has emerged, typically via acquisition of virulence plasmids encoding hypervirulence factors by CRKP. However, whether hypervirulence worsens patient outcomes in the setting of carbapenem resistance remains unclear. METHODS: We conducted an observational cohort study comprising 3-year (2019-2022) data in a Chinese university hospital. We performed genome sequencing for CRKP strains, compared adult patients infected by hvCRKP with those infected by nonhypervirulent "classic" CRKP (cCRKP), and used propensity score matching for imbalanced baseline characteristics. We defined 30-day all-cause and attributed mortality rates and 30-day ranking outcomes (death, no response/progress, or remission) as primary outcomes and postinfection stay and survival as secondary outcomes. RESULTS: Patients with hvCRKP (n = 262) or cCRKP (n = 294) infection had similar 30-day mortality rates (all-cause, 30.9% vs 33.7% [P = .49]; infection attributed, 24.4% vs 26.2%b [P = .77]), no response/progress rate (19.8% vs 20.4%; P = .72), postinfection hospital stay (median, 13 vs 15 days; P = .19), and postinfection survival (median, 18.5 vs 24.5 days; P = .22). All outcomes were also similar (P > .05) between the 2 groups after propensity score matching and in all subgroup analyses. CONCLUSIONS: The hypervirulence of K. pneumoniae remains not well defined. The concept of hvCRKP in current form should not be overemphasized.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：肺炎克雷伯菌是一种重要的病原体，耐碳青霉烯和高毒力是导致其预后不良的常见进化途径。高毒力耐碳青霉烯类肺炎克雷伯菌（hvCRKP）已经出现，通常由耐碳青霉烯类肺炎克雷伯菌（CRKP）获得编码高毒力因子的毒力质粒而产生。然而，在耐碳青霉烯的背景下，高毒力是否会恶化患者的预后仍不清楚。
> 
> 方法：我们开展了一项为期3年（2019-2022年）的观察性队列研究，数据来源于中国的一家大学医院。我们对CRKP菌株进行了基因组测序，将hvCRKP感染的成年患者与非高毒力的“经典”CRKP（cCRKP）感染患者进行了比较，并针对不平衡的基线特征采用了倾向评分匹配法。我们将30天全因死亡率、归因死亡率以及30天等级结局（死亡、无反应/进展或缓解）定义为主要结局，将感染后住院时间及生存期定义为次要结局。
> 
> 结果：hvCRKP组（n = 262）和cCRKP组（n = 294）患者的30天死亡率相似（全因死亡率为30.9%对33.7% [P = .49]；感染归因死亡率为24.4%对26.2% [P = .77]），无反应/进展率相似（19.8%对20.4%；P = .72），感染后住院时间（中位数为13天对15天；P = .19）以及感染后生存期（中位数为18.5天对24.5天；P = .22）亦无显著差异。在倾向评分匹配后及所有亚组分析中，两组间的所有结局也均相似（P > .05）。
> 
> 结论：肺炎克雷伯菌的高毒力定义尚不明确。目前形式下的hvCRKP概念不应被过度强调。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

这项研究通过对556例耐碳青霉烯类肺炎克雷伯菌（CRKP）感染者的队列分析，挑战了“高毒力（hv）”会显著加剧CRKP临床病情的传统观念。研究利用基因组测序与倾向评分匹配等严谨方法，发现hvCRKP与经典CRKP感染在30天死亡率及住院时间等多项临床结局指标上并无显著差异。其核心价值在于提醒学术界，肺炎克雷伯菌的高毒力定义仍存在模糊性，在临床决策中不应盲目过度夸大目前定义的hvCRKP对预后的负面影响。
