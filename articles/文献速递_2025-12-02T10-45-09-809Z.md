## 1. 异基因CCR5Δ32杂合干细胞移植后获得持续HIV-1缓解

**原文链接：** [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41326734](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41326734)
**期刊：** Nature
**PMID：** 41326734
**DOI：** 10.1038/s41586-025-09893-0

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Sustained HIV-1 remission after heterozygous CCR5Δ32 stem cell transplantation.

**英文摘要原文：**
HIV cure is exceptionally rare, documented in only six cases among the estimated 88 million individuals who have acquired HIV since the epidemic's onset. Successful cures, including the pioneering Berlin patient, are limited to individuals receiving allogeneic stem cell transplants (allo-SCT) for hematological cancers. HIV resistance from stem cell donors with the rare homozygous CCR5 Δ32 mutation was long considered the main mechanism for HIV remission without antiretroviral therapy (ART), but recent reports highlight CCR5-independent mechanisms as important contributors to HIV cure. Here, we provide new evidence for this conceptual shift, reporting exceptionally long, treatment-free HIV remission following allo-SCT with functionally active CCR5. A heterozygous CCR5 wild-type/Δ32 male living with HIV received allo-SCT from an HLA-matched unrelated heterozygous CCR5 wild-type/Δ32 donor as treatment for acute myeloid leukemia. Three years after allo-SCT, the patient discontinued ART. To date, HIV remission has been sustained for over six years with undetectable plasma HIV RNA. Reservoir analysis revealed intact proviral HIV before transplantation, but no replication-competent virus in blood or intestinal tissues after allo-SCT. Declining or absent HIV-specific antibody and T cell responses support the absence of viral activity. High antibody-dependent cellular cytotoxicity (ADCC) activity at the time of transplantation may have contributed to HIV reservoir clearance. These results demonstrate that CCR5Δ32-mediated HIV resistance is not essential for durable remission, underscoring the importance of effective viral reservoir reductions in HIV cure strategies.

**中文摘要译文：**
HIV治愈极为罕见，自疫情爆发以来，在估计8800万感染HIV的个体中，仅有六例治愈记录。成功的治愈案例，包括开创性的柏林病人，仅限于因血液系统恶性肿瘤接受异基因造血干细胞移植（allo-SCT）的个体。长期以来，携带罕见纯合CCR5 Δ32突变的干细胞供体所赋予的HIV抗性被认为是无需抗逆转录病毒治疗（ART）即可获得HIV缓解的主要机制，但最近的报告强调CCR5非依赖性机制是HIV治愈的重要贡献因素。在此，我们为这一概念转变提供了新的证据，报告了一例在具有功能性活性CCR5的allo-SCT后获得异常长期、无需治疗的HIV缓解病例。一名携带HIV的CCR5野生型/Δ32杂合男性患者，因急性髓系白血病接受了来自HLA匹配的无关CCR5野生型/Δ32杂合供体的allo-SCT治疗。allo-SCT三年后，患者停用了ART。迄今为止，HIV缓解已持续超过六年，血浆HIV RNA检测不到。病毒库分析显示移植前存在完整的原病毒HIV，但allo-SCT后在血液或肠道组织中未检测到具有复制能力的病毒。下降或缺失的HIV特异性抗体和T细胞反应支持了病毒活动的缺失。移植时的高抗体依赖性细胞毒性（ADCC）活性可能有助于HIV病毒库的清除。这些结果表明，CCR5Δ32介导的HIV抗性并非持久缓解所必需，强调了有效减少病毒库在HIV治愈策略中的重要性。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究旨在探索HIV治愈的新机制，挑战了传统认为CCR5Δ32纯合突变是HIV缓解必要条件的观点。研究团队通过一例接受异基因造血干细胞移植的HIV感染者病例，采用长期随访、病毒库分析和免疫学检测等方法，发现即使供体为CCR5Δ32杂合状态（即CCR5功能正常），患者仍能获得超过六年的无治疗HIV缓解。关键发现揭示了CCR5非依赖性机制在HIV治愈中的重要作用，特别是高ADCC活性可能促进了病毒库清除。这项研究的创新性在于突破了HIV治愈机制的固有认知，为开发不依赖CCR5突变的HIV治愈策略提供了重要理论依据，具有显著的临床转化价值。然而，研究局限性在于仅为单病例报告，其普遍性仍需更大样本量验证，且具体机制细节有待进一步阐明。
