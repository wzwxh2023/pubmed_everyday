### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Ensitrelvir for the treatment of hospitalized adults with COVID-19: an international phase 3 randomized placebo-controlled trial.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Antivirals remain an important treatment strategy for persons who experience severe and life-threatening COVID-19. Ensitrelvir is an oral 3CL protease inhibitor with potent antiviral activity.
> 
> METHODS: We conducted an international randomized, placebo-controlled trial of ensitrelvir with standard of care (SOC) among adults hospitalized for COVID-19. The primary outcome was clinical recovery assessed by the Days to Recovery Scale through Day 60 (DRS-60), analyzed using a Van Elteren test.
> 
> RESULTS: From 2023 to 2025, 589 participants received blinded study treatment (293 ensitrelvir and 296 placebo). Median age was 69 years, 49% were female, 68% were White, and SOC commonly included corticosteroids (61% and 54%) and remdesivir (62% and 60%) in ensitrelvir and placebo groups, respectively. Median DRS-60 category was 6 (IQR: 3-15) in the ensitrelvir and 5.5 (IQR: 3-12) in the placebo group (p=0.19), and the OR was 0.82 (95% CI: 0.62-1.09) for a better DRS-60 category with ensitrelvir. Ensitrelvir participants had lower detectable viral antigen in plasma at Day 5 (13.4% vs 25.1%; p<0.001). There was no difference in secondary clinical outcomes or pre-specified safety outcomes, though the mortality rate was 6.1% vs 4.4% and the frequency of hemorrhagic events was 3.4% vs 0.3% among ensitrelvir and placebo groups, respectively.
> 
> CONCLUSIONS: Ensitrelvir treatment did not improve clinical recovery in addition to SOC for adults hospitalized for COVID-19. The lower illness severity in the Omicron era compared to earlier periods in the COVID-19 pandemic, and high use of remdesivir and corticosteroids, may have contributed to the lack of clinical benefit.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：对于经历严重和危及生命的COVID-19患者，抗病毒药物仍然是一项重要的治疗策略。Ensitrelvir是一种具有强效抗病毒活性的口服3CL蛋白酶抑制剂。
> 
> 方法：我们在因COVID-19住院的成年人中，进行了一项国际性、随机、安慰剂对照的ensitrelvir联合标准治疗（SOC）的试验。主要结局是通过60天恢复日量表（DRS-60）评估的临床恢复情况，并使用Van Elteren检验进行分析。
> 
> 结果：从2023年到2025年，589名参与者接受了盲法研究治疗（293名ensitrelvir组和296名安慰剂组）。中位年龄为69岁，49%为女性，68%为白人，在ensitrelvir组和安慰剂组中，标准治疗通常分别包括皮质类固醇（61%和54%）和瑞德西韦（62%和60%）。Ensitrelvir组的中位DRS-60类别为6（IQR：3-15），安慰剂组为5.5（IQR：3-12）（p=0.19），ensitrelvir组获得更好DRS-60类别的OR值为0.82（95% CI：0.62-1.09）。在第5天，ensitrelvir组参与者血浆中可检测到的病毒抗原水平较低（13.4% vs 25.1%；p<0.001）。次要临床结局或预先设定的安全性结局没有差异，尽管ensitrelvir组和安慰剂组的死亡率分别为6.1% vs 4.4%，出血事件发生率分别为3.4% vs 0.3%。
> 
> 结论：对于因COVID-19住院的成年患者，在标准治疗基础上加用Ensitrelvir并不能改善临床恢复。与COVID-19大流行早期相比，奥密克戎时代疾病严重程度较低，以及瑞德西韦和皮质类固醇的高使用率，可能导致了临床获益的缺乏。

**英文原标题：** The intestinal microbiota impacts nutritional immunity and resistance to pneumonia.

> **英文摘要：**
> Broad-spectrum antibiotics are frequently administered to intensive care unit patients as part of empiric care. This treatment has been associated with subsequent infections by the emerging nosocomial pathogen ; however, the mechanisms underlying this linkage remain unclear. Here, we observe an association between antibiotic treatment and microbiota disruption that precedes infection in a hospitalized patient cohort and demonstrate in a murine model that broad-spectrum antibiotic administration drives susceptibility to intranasal infection with this pathogen. Reconstitution of the intestinal microbiota by fecal microbiota transplant restores control of bloodstream dissemination, implicating microbiota dysbiosis as a key driver of pulmonary disease. Depletion studies identify neutrophils and inflammatory monocytes as central mediators of microbiota-dependent protection, and loss of the nutritional immunity components lipocalin-2 or calprotectin abrogates the effects of antibiotics on infected mice, demonstrating a causal relationship between microbiota dysbiosis and impaired phagocyte-mediated nutritional immunity. Together, these findings provide a mechanism for the increased severity of pneumonia following antibiotic exposure and highlight the intestinal microbiota as a potential therapeutic target to prevent nosocomial infections with this and other healthcare-associated pathogens.

## 5. 靶向多烯链是优化多烯类抗真菌药物的一种辅助策略。

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Targeting the polyene chain represents an adjuvant strategy for optimizing polyene antifungals.

> **英文摘要：**
> Polyene antifungals (PAs) have long constituted an irreplaceable lifesaving treatment for lethal fungal infections. However, PAs induce significant dose-dependent nephrotoxicity, primarily because of their binding to cholesterol in the renal cell membrane, which is structurally similar to their fungicidal target ergosterol in the fungal membrane. Efforts to optimize PAs have focused mainly on their mycosamine group and polyol chain, while the potential of the polyene chain remains underexplored. Here, we report that the polyene chain can function as a molecular switch, enabling hydrophobic coupling with structurally compatible adjuvants repurposed from clinically approved drugs. This hydrophobic coupling triggers conformational rearrangements of PAs, which are characterized by a reorientation of the mycosamine group, and increases the rigidity and length of the polyene chain, which can asymmetrically modulate the binding of PAs to membrane cholesterol and ergosterol, thereby attenuating nephrotoxicity and increasing effectiveness. Among them, vitamin D demonstrated immediate clinical value, characterized by its broad safety profile and substantial dual adjuvant activities in multiple animal models and retrospective clinical data. Thus, exploiting the polyene chain as a molecular switch, modulated via specific polyene chain-coupled adjuvants, is a strategy to concurrently attenuate nephrotoxicity and potentiate the effectiveness of PAs.

> **中文摘要：**
> 长期以来，多烯类抗真菌药物（PAs）是治疗致死性真菌感染不可或缺的救命疗法。然而，PAs会诱发显著的剂量依赖性肾毒性，这主要是因为它们会与肾细胞膜上的胆固醇结合，而胆固醇在结构上与其在真菌膜上的杀菌靶点麦角固醇相似。优化PAs的努力主要集中在其麦角糖胺基团和多元醇链上，而多烯链的潜力仍未得到充分探索。在此，我们报告多烯链可以作为一个分子开关，与从临床已批准药物中重新利用的结构相容性佐剂实现疏水耦合。这种疏水耦合触发了PAs的构象重排，其特征是麦角糖胺基团的重新定向，并增加了多烯链的刚性和长度，从而能够不对称地调节PAs与膜胆固醇和麦角固醇的结合，进而减弱肾毒性并提高药效。其中，维生素D展现了直接的临床价值，其特点是广泛的安全性以及在多种动物模型和回顾性临床数据中表现出显著的双重辅助活性。因此，利用多烯链作为分子开关，通过特异性的多烯链耦合佐剂进行调节，是一种能同时减弱肾毒性并增强PAs药效的策略。

## 6. eGFR不一致及其与异基因造血干细胞移植和过继性T细胞疗法后临床结局的相关性

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42012898)
**期刊：** Journal of the American Society of Nephrology : JASN
**PMID：** 42012898
**DOI：** 10.1681/ASN.0000001119

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** eGFR Discordance and its Association with Outcomes following Allogeneic Hematopoietic Stem Cell Transplantation and Adoptive T-Cell Therapies.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Discordance between estimated glomerular filtration rate (eGFR) based on serum creatinine (eGFRcr) versus cystatin C (eGFRcys) is associated with adverse outcomes in patients with and without cancer. We hypothesized that eGFR discordance is associated with acute kidney injury (AKI) and death following hematopoietic stem cell transplant (HSCT) or receipt of adoptive T-cell therapies. METHODS: We conducted a multicenter study of adults receiving allogeneic HSCT and adoptive T-cell therapies who had paired serum creatinine and cystatin C values obtained in the 30 days preceding conditioning or lymphodepleting chemotherapy. The primary exposure was eGFR discordance, defined as eGFRcys ≥30% lower than eGFRcr. The primary outcome was a composite outcome of AKI (≥50% increase in serum creatinine or receipt of kidney replacement therapy) or death within 90 days following HSCT or adoptive T-cell infusion. Secondary outcomes included time-to-platelet engraftment in HSCT and prolonged cytopenia in adoptive T-cell recipients. We used multivariable logistic regression and cause-specific Cox regression with inverse probability weighting to adjust for confounders. RESULTS: Of 274 patients receiving HSCT (median age 65 years, interquartile range [IQR] 52-71]; 43% female), 86 (31%) had an eGFR discordance. Among 236 adoptive T-cell recipients (median age 67 years, IQR [60-74]; 59% female), 78 (33%) had a discordance in eGFR. eGFR discordance was associated with higher odds of AKI or death in HSCT (odds ratio [OR] 1.75; 95% confidence interval [CI] 1.03-3.02) and adoptive T-cell recipients (OR 2.37; 95% CI 1.12-4.94). However, adjustment for eGFRcr-cys attenuated these associations in both cohorts. eGFR discordance was also associated with slower time-to-platelet engraftment in HSCT recipients and prolonged cytopenia following adoptive T-cell therapy. CONCLUSIONS: A pre-treatment eGFR discordance was associated with AKI or death in HSCT and adoptive T-cell recipients; however, this association was attenuated after adjusting for eGFRcr-cys.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：基于血清肌酐（eGFRcr）与基于胱抑素C（eGFRcys）计算的估算肾小球滤过率（eGFR）之间的不一致与有或无癌症患者的不良结局相关。我们假设eGFR不一致与造血干细胞移植（HSCT）或接受过继性T细胞疗法后的急性肾损伤（AKI）和死亡相关。方法：我们进行了一项多中心研究，纳入了接受异基因HSCT和过继性T细胞疗法的成年患者，这些患者在预处理或淋巴细胞清除性化疗前的30天内获得了配对的血清肌酐和胱抑素C值。主要暴露因素为eGFR不一致，定义为eGFRcys比eGFRcr低30%或以上。主要结局是HSCT或过继性T细胞输注后90天内的AKI（血清肌酐升高≥50%或接受肾脏替代治疗）或死亡的复合结局。次要结局包括HSCT中的血小板植入时间以及过继性T细胞受试者的持续性血细胞减少。我们使用多变量逻辑回归和具有逆概率加权的病因特异性Cox回归来调整混杂因素。结果：在274名接受HSCT的患者中（中位年龄65岁，四分位间距[IQR] 52-71；43%为女性），86人（31%）存在eGFR不一致。在236名过继性T细胞受试者中（中位年龄67岁，IQR [60-74]；59%为女性），78人（33%）存在eGFR不一致。eGFR不一致与HSCT患者（优势比[OR] 1.75；95%置信区间[CI] 1.03-3.02）和过继性T细胞受试者（OR 2.37；95% CI 1.12-4.94）发生AKI或死亡的较高概率相关。然而，在对eGFRcr-cys平均值进行调整后，这两个队列中的这种相关性均有所减弱。eGFR不一致还与HSCT受试者较慢的血小板植入时间以及过继性T细胞疗法后的持续性血细胞减少相关。结论：治疗前的eGFR不一致与HSCT和过继性T细胞受试者的AKI或死亡相关；然而，在调整eGFRcr-cys平均值后，这种相关性有所减弱。

本研究聚焦于细胞疗法受试者中常见的肾功能评估指标差异，通过多中心队列证实了eGFRcys显著低于eGFRcr（不一致性）是患者术后发生AKI、死亡及造血功能恢复延迟的重要预后因子。研究方法严谨，采用了逆概率加权等统计学手段调整混杂因素，其创新性在于将肾功能不一致性这一生物标志物引入了高度复杂的HSCT和CAR-T等过继性T细胞治疗领域。尽管调整联合eGFR后相关性减弱提示了eGFRcr-cys可能具有更强的预测力，但研究结论依然强调了在对此类重症肿瘤患者进行风险分层时，同时监测肌酐和胱抑素C的临床必要性。

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** A Pilot Randomized Controlled Trial of Ultrasound Evaluation of Fluid Tolerance for Acute Kidney Injury (USE-the-FORCE-for-AKI trial).

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Indiscriminate fluid administration in acute kidney injury (AKI) can be harmful and point-of-care ultrasound (POCUS) could be used to assess for fluid tolerance. The objective of pilot study is to determine the feasibility of a randomized trial comparing a fluid management strategy including POCUS to usual care. METHODS: This is a single centre open-label pilot randomized controlled trial that recruited non-critically ill patients with AKI for whom fluid administration had begun or was considered. The intervention consisted in the transmission to the nephrology team of a POCUS report on fluid tolerance (assessment of pulmonary B-lines and systemic venous congestion with the Venous Excess Ultrasound (VExUS) score). The control was usual care without POCUS. The primary outcome was feasibility, defined as protocol adherence. RESULTS: Eighty patients underwent randomization, 40 were randomized in the POCUS group and 40 in the usual care group, but one withdrew consent before initiating trial procedures. Protocol adherence was achieved in all patients. In the intervention arm, 50% of initial ultrasound reports led to a change in clinical conduct and the provided information was perceived as useful by clinicians (median 4.5/5 (interquartile range: 4.0-5.0)). The intervention led to a higher use of diuretics during the first day after randomization (40% vs 15%, p=0.01) but did not result in difference in cumulative fluid balance or diuretic use at five days, progression to higher stages of AKI, or composite outcome of death and escalation of care. CONCLUSIONS: This pilot trial demonstrate the feasibility of a randomized trial investigating the clinical impact of providing a POCUS fluid tolerance evaluation in AKI. Its impact on clinical management and perceived usefulness support the rationale for future, larger-scale studies.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：在急性肾损伤（AKI）中盲目补液可能有害，而点式超声（POCUS）可用于评估补液耐受性。这项先导性研究的目的是确定开展一项比较包含 POCUS 的液体管理策略与常规护理的随机试验的可行性。方法：这是一项单中心、开放标签的先导性随机对照试验，招募了已开始或考虑进行补液治疗的非危重症 AKI 患者。干预措施包括向肾脏病团队传输一份关于补液耐受性的 POCUS 报告（评估肺部 B 线和利用静脉过载超声 [VExUS] 评分评估系统性静脉淤血）。对照组为不使用 POCUS 的常规护理。主要结局指标是可行性，定义为协议遵循率。结果：80 名患者接受了随机分组，40 人进入 POCUS 组，40 人进入常规护理组，但有一名患者在启动试验程序前撤回了知情同意。所有患者均实现了协议遵循。在干预组中，50% 的初始超声报告导致了临床处理的改变，且临床医生认为提供的信息很有用（中位数 4.5/5 [四分位间距：4.0-5.0]）。干预措施导致随机化后第一天利尿剂的使用率更高（40% vs 15%，p=0.01），但在五天时的累积液体平衡或利尿剂使用、AKI 向更高级别的进展，以及死亡和升级治疗的复合结局方面没有显著差异。结论：这项先导性试验展示了开展一项调查在 AKI 中提供 POCUS 补液耐受性评估临床影响的随机试验的可行性。其对临床管理的影响和感知的实用性为未来更大规模的研究提供了理论依据。

该研究（USE-the-FORCE-for-AKI）是一项针对非危重症急性肾损伤患者的先导性随机对照试验，旨在验证使用 POCUS（结合 VExUS 评分）评估补液耐受性的临床可行性。研究结果显示该方案具有极高的方案依从性，且超声评估结果显著改变了临床决策，增加了早期利尿剂的使用，证明了其实践价值。虽然初步结果在长期液体平衡和预后方面未见显著统计学差异，但该试验为未来开展大规模、多中心临床研究以明确 POCUS 指导液体管理在改善 AKI 预后中的作用奠定了坚实基础。

## 8. 住院非复杂性社区获得性肺炎成年患者抗生素使用的电子临床质量评价指标

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42012353)
**期刊：** Clinical infectious diseases : an official publication of the Infectious Diseases Society of America
**PMID：** 42012353
**DOI：** 10.1093/cid/ciag273

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Electronic Clinical Quality Measures of Antibiotic Use in Hospitalized Adults with Uncomplicated Community-acquired Pneumonia.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Community-acquired pneumonia (CAP) is the top indication for inpatient antibiotic use and overuse. We describe the development of two electronic clinical quality measures (eCQMs) assessing the percentage of hospitalized patients with uncomplicated CAP who had unsupported a) antibiotic duration beyond 5 days and b) empiric antibiotics targeting methicillin-resistant Staphylococcus aureus and/or Pseudomonas aeruginosa. METHODS: Both eCQMs were developed based on clinical guidelines, literature, and expert feedback and then specified electronically. The eCQMs were then applied to patient data from two academic medical centers and 109 VA healthcare systems to evaluate importance, reliability, validity, and feasibility, as defined by Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services. RESULTS: Across all hospitals, the mean percentage of eligible patients with unsupported excess duration and inappropriate empiric antibiotic use ranged from 40%-55% and 17%-56%, respectively. Within the VA cohort, reliability (median [1st -10th decile, n]) for duration and empiric eCQMs was high: 93% (63%-97%, 28,238) and 91% (63%-96%, 47,034). When compared to chart review at one academic hospital, both eCQMs had high sensitivity (96%, both) and specificity (92% and 93%, for duration and empiric eCQMs, respectively). Feasibility was maximized by using only data elements in structured electronic health record fields. Some technically complex elements (e.g., clinical stability) were retained for face validity. CONCLUSIONS: Two eCQMs identifying unsupported excess antibiotic duration and inappropriately broad empiric antibiotic use in uncomplicated CAP patients had high importance, reliability, validity, and feasibility. These eCQMs could be benchmarked to assist antibiotic stewardship programs in understanding and improving their antibiotic use.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：社区获得性肺炎（CAP）是住院患者抗生素使用及过度使用的首要原因。我们描述了两种电子临床质量评价指标（eCQMs）的开发过程，这些指标用于评估住院非复杂性 CAP 患者中，缺乏证据支持的 a) 超过 5 天的抗生素使用时长，以及 b) 针对耐甲氧西林金黄色葡萄球菌（MRSA）和/或铜绿假单胞菌的经验性抗生素使用比例。方法：根据临床指南、文献和专家反馈开发了这两种 eCQM，并进行了电子化规范。随后将这些 eCQM 应用于两家学术医学中心和 109 个退伍军人事务部（VA）医疗系统的患者数据，以评估美国联邦医疗保险和联邦医疗救助服务中心（CMS）定义的显著性、可靠性、有效性和可行性。结果：在所有医院中，缺乏证据支持的过度用药时长和不当经验性抗生素使用的符合条件患者平均比例分别为 40%-55% 和 17%-56%。在 VA 队列中，用药时长和经验性用药 eCQM 的可靠性（中位数 [第 1-10 十分位数，n]）很高，分别为 93%（63%-97%，28,238）和 91%（63%-96%，47,034）。与某学术医院的病历审查相比，两种 eCQM 均具有较高的灵敏度（均为 96%）和特异性（用药时长 eCQM 为 92%，经验性用药 eCQM 为 93%）。通过仅使用结构化电子健康档案字段中的数据元素，实现了可行性的最大化。为了保证表面效度，保留了一些技术上复杂的元素（如临床稳定性）。结论：用于识别非复杂性 CAP 患者中缺乏证据支持的过度抗生素使用时长和不当广谱经验性抗生素使用的两种 eCQM 具有高度的显著性、可靠性、有效性和可行性。这些 eCQM 可作为基准，协助抗生素管理计划理解并改善其抗生素使用情况。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究成功开发并验证了两项针对非复杂性社区获得性肺炎（CAP）的电子临床质量评价指标（eCQMs），旨在通过结构化电子健康档案数据精准识别抗生素过度使用和不当选择。通过在100多家医疗机构的大规模验证，这些指标展现出了极高的可靠性与临床效度，并与传统病历审查结果高度一致。此项工作的核心创新在于实现了复杂临床指标的自动化监测，虽然在临床稳定性等非结构化参数的精准抓取上仍面临挑战，但为医院抗生素管理计划（ASP）提供了强有力的标准化评估工具，具有显著的临床推广价值。
