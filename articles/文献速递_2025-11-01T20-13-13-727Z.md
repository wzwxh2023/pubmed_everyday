## 1. 一项关于JNJ-40411813联合左乙拉西坦或布立西坦治疗局灶性癫痫发作患者的有效性与安全性的随机、双盲、安慰剂对照、事件发生时间研究

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** A randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, time-to-event study of the efficacy and safety of JNJ-40411813 in combination with levetiracetam or brivaracetam in patients with focal onset seizures.

**英文摘要原文：**
OBJECTIVE: Up to 30% of people with epilepsy are refractory to current antiseizure medications (ASMs). JNJ-40411813 is a positive allosteric modulator of metabotropic glutamate 2 receptor, a presynaptic glutamate release inhibitor, and was hypothesized to reduce glutamate-mediated neuronal toxicity through inhibition of excessive glutamate release during seizures.

METHODS: This 12-week, double-blinded, placebo-controlled phase 2a study, which employed a rational polypharmacy approach using a novel time-to-event endpoint for epilepsy clinical trials, evaluated JNJ-40411813 (Cohort 1: 50/100 mg; Cohort 2: 100/200 mg) as adjunctive treatment for focal seizures in participants aged 18-64 years receiving levetiracetam or brivaracetam and ≤3 other ASMs. Participants were stratified as induced or noninduced based on treatment or no treatment with a cytochrome P450 3A4 enzyme-inducing ASM. The primary endpoint was time-to-baseline monthly seizure count.

RESULTS: Cohorts 1 and 2 randomized 60 and 50 patients, respectively. No significant clinical benefits were observed in the median time (95% confidence interval [CI]) to reach the baseline monthly seizure count in Cohort 1 (JNJ-40411813: 34 days [27-48], placebo 32 days [28-37], hazard ratio [HR] = .75 [.41-1.38], p = .36) or Cohort 2 (JNJ-40411813: 38 days [28-48], placebo: 29 days [22-69], HR = .83 [.40-1.75], p = .63). Similarly, no clinical benefits of JNJ-40411813 were observed for any of the secondary endpoints. JNJ-40411813 displayed an acceptable safety profile; treatment-emergent adverse events were mild to moderate in severity and not treatment limiting.

SIGNIFICANCE: JNJ-40411813 adjunctive to levetiracetam or brivaracetam showed no significant clinical benefit over placebo in reducing time-to-baseline monthly seizure count or improvement in other key measures in patients with focal onset seizures. The time-to-event study design was successful at reducing participant exposure to ineffective treatment. Despite an acceptable safety profile, the overall efficacy and benefit-risk assessment of JNJ-40411813 does not support its use for patients with focal seizures.

## 2. 棘波-快节律活动的相互作用：通往癫痫发作的门户

**原文链接：** [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41171197](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41171197)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41171197
**DOI：** 10.1111/epi.18703

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Spike-fast activity interplay: A gateway to seizure.

**英文摘要原文：**
OBJECTIVE: The transition from interictal discharges to ictal high-frequency activity (HFA) remains poorly understood. We investigated whether spike-associated high-frequency oscillations (Sp-HFOs) during interictal and preictal periods contribute to the emergence of ictal HFA.

METHODS: We retrospectively analyzed the interictal to ictal transition in seizures from six patients with drug-resistant focal epilepsy who underwent stereo-electroencephalography (EEG) and subsequent surgical resection. Various interictal periods preceding seizure onset were selected for comparison. Time-frequency analysis (TFA) was used to characterize Sp-HFOs and ictal HFA. Frequency overlap was measured using the Ideal Fusion (I-Fusion) metric, a novel metric inspired by the F-measure that integrates frequency range overlap and band matching. I-Fusion values range from 0 (no overlap) to 1 (perfect match). Linear regression analyzed changes in I-Fusion values over time, with R indicating the strength of the correlation.

RESULTS: Visual analysis of the time series revealed a preictal phase in all patients, during which brief HFA gradually emerged within spikes (Sp-HFOs), ultimately transitioning into sustained ictal HFA at the same frequency. TFA demonstrated increasing frequency similarity with time between Sp-HFOs and ictal HFA. I-Fusion values and R coefficients rose consistently, indicating a progressive convergence in frequency content. Notably, Sp-HFOs and ictal HFA shared narrow-band frequency features within the same electrode contacts, especially in the epileptogenic zone (EZ).

SIGNIFICANCE: Our findings support a dynamic, frequency-specific evolution from interictal Sp-HFOs to ictal HFA, suggesting that seizure onset is preceded by a gradual preparatory phase rather than an abrupt transformation. The progressive nature and spectral continuity of Sp-HFOs may reflect increasing neuronal synchrony, providing potential early biomarkers for seizure prediction and improved localization of the EZ.

**中文摘要译文：**
目的：从发作间期放电到发作期高频活动（HFA）的转变过程仍不甚明了。我们旨在研究发作间期和发作前期出现的棘波相关高频振荡（Sp-HFOs）是否促进了发作期HFA的产生。

方法：我们回顾性分析了六名接受立体定向脑电图（SEEG）及后续手术切除治疗的耐药性局灶性癫痫患者的发作间期至发作期的转变过程。我们选取了癫痫发作前的不同发作间期阶段进行比较。采用时频分析（TFA）来表征Sp-HFOs和发作期HFA的特征。频率重叠度采用一种名为理想融合（I-Fusion）的新型指标进行测量，该指标受F-measure启发，整合了频率范围重叠和频带匹配。I-Fusion值的范围为0（无重叠）到1（完全匹配）。通过线性回归分析I-Fusion值随时间的变化，其中R值表示相关性的强度。

结果：对时间序列的视觉分析显示，所有患者均存在一个发作前期，在此期间，短暂的HFA在棘波内（Sp-HFOs）逐渐出现，并最终在相同频率上转变为持续的发作期HFA。TFA显示，随着时间的推移，Sp-HFOs与发作期HFA之间的频率相似性逐渐增加。I-Fusion值和R系数持续上升，表明其频率内容逐渐趋同。值得注意的是，Sp-HFOs和发作期HFA在同一电极触点内共享窄带频率特征，尤其是在致痫区（EZ）内。

意义：我们的研究结果支持从发作间期Sp-HFOs到发作期HFA存在一个动态的、频率特异性的演变过程，这表明癫痫发作前存在一个渐进的准备阶段，而非突然的转变。Sp-HFOs的渐进性特征和频谱连续性可能反映了神经元同步性的增强，为癫痫发作的预测和致痫区的精确定位提供了潜在的早期生物标志物。

## 3. 朗道-克莱夫纳综合征的临床与分子遗传学特征：一项观察性队列与实验性研究

**原文链接：** [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40944498](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40944498)
**期刊：** Annals of neurology
**PMID：** 40944498
**DOI：** 10.1002/ana.27306

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Clinical and Molecular Genetic Characterization of Landau Kleffner Syndrome: An Observational Cohort and Experimental Study.

**英文摘要原文：**
OBJECTIVE: Landau-Kleffner syndrome (LKS), is a rare, poorly-understood epileptic encephalopathy with spike-wave activation in sleep associated with mutations in GRIN2A, encoding the N-Methyl-D-Aspartate receptor (NMDAR) GluN2A subunit. Physicians rely on empirical treatments, with scarce information on treatment efficacy and outcomes. This study aims to improve the understanding and clinical management of LKS.

METHODS: Fifty-two patients with LKS were recruited via one quaternary referral center. Case-notes review delineated clinical features, long-term outcomes, and prognostic factors. Generalized estimating equations were used to determine the longitudinal association among electroencephalogram abnormalities, steroid therapy, and neuropsychological findings. After genetic screening, the impact of identified GRIN2A missense variants on NMDAR function was assessed using homology modeling, cell-surface trafficking assays, and electrophysiology in artificial synapses. Whole exome/genome sequencing was performed on GRIN2A-negative patients to identify novel gene associations.

RESULTS: LKS is complex with significant clinical and genetic heterogeneity. Besides speech and language impairment, many patients had other co-morbidities and almost half have long-term disability. Early age at disease onset was associated with worse outcomes. There was no reliable correlation between electroencephalogram findings and developmental scores. Steroid therapy improved language outcomes independently of electroencephalogram findings. GRIN2A mutations were identified in 15.5% of the cohort. Likely pathogenic variants in GABBR2, SCN1A, TRPC1, ERRFI1, CTXN3, IRX6, and IQCA1 were identified in 7 GRIN2A-negative individuals.

INTERPRETATION: For LKS, early intervention is important for long-term outcomes. Furthermore, management should not be based solely on electroencephalogram findings. Genetic and functional investigations offer insights into disease pathophysiology and facilitate development of future targeted therapies. ANN NEUROL 2025;98:951-966.

**中文摘要译文：**
目的：朗道-克莱夫纳综合征（LKS）是一种罕见且机制尚不明确的癫痫性脑病，其特征为睡眠期棘慢波激活，并与编码N-甲基-D-天冬氨酸受体（NMDAR）GluN2A亚基的GRIN2A基因突变相关。临床医生主要依赖经验性治疗，关于治疗效果和预后的信息甚少。本研究旨在增进对LKS的理解并改善其临床管理。

方法：通过一个四级转诊中心招募了52名LKS患者。通过回顾病历，描绘了临床特征、长期预后及预后因素。采用广义估计方程确定了脑电图异常、类固醇治疗与神经心理学发现之间的纵向关联。进行基因筛查后，利用同源建模、细胞表面转运分析和人工突触电生理学评估了已识别的GRIN2A错义变异对NMDAR功能的影响。对GRIN2A阴性患者进行了全外显子组/基因组测序，以鉴定新的相关基因。

结果：LKS是一种复杂的疾病，具有显著的临床和遗传异质性。除言语和语言障碍外，许多患者还存在其他共病，且近半数患者有长期残疾。发病年龄早与较差的预后相关。脑电图结果与发育评分之间无可靠相关性。类固醇治疗能够改善语言预后，且独立于脑电图结果。在该队列中，15.5%的患者检测到GRIN2A突变。在7名GRIN2A阴性个体中，鉴定出了GABBR2、SCN1A、TRPC1、ERRFI1、CTXN3、IRX6和IQCA1基因中可能致病的变异。

结论：对于LKS，早期干预对长期预后至关重要。此外，临床管理不应仅依赖脑电图结果。遗传学和功能性研究为揭示疾病的病理生理机制提供了见解，并有助于未来靶向疗法的开发。ANN NEUROL 2025;98:951-966.

## 4. 行为-颅内脑电-频谱功率相关性分析：界定自然行为的神经基础

**原文链接：** [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40817733](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40817733)
**期刊：** Annals of neurology
**PMID：** 40817733
**DOI：** 10.1002/ana.70002

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Behavior-iEEG-Spectral-Power-Correlation: Defining Neural Substrates of Naturalistic Behavior.

**英文摘要原文：**
OBJECTIVE: Intracranial language localization with electrical stimulation mapping (ESM) and high-gamma modulation (HGM) mapping relies on artificial, repetitive tasks, requiring sustained cooperation from patients. Herein, we tested the validity of unstructured, interpersonal, naturalistic conversation for language localization, using a novel methodology: Behavior-iEEG-Spectral-Power correlation (BESPoC). We first validated BESPoC against ESM, HGM, and neuropsychological outcomes using well-established language tasks, then demonstrated the validity of naturalistic conversation.

METHODS: We included 134 patients (59 females), aged 2-29 years, undergoing standard-of-care stereo-electroencephalography monitoring who engaged in picture naming, auditory naming, story listening, and conversed with a family member. ESM and HGM analysis were performed using established methods. BESPoC methodology quantified correlation between stereo-electroencephalography spectral power from task recordings, obviating the need for any trial-based epochs, and behavioral markers. The large sample size allowed mixed-effects modeling to compare BESPoC with HGM and ESM.

RESULTS: BESPoC showed high specificity (0.79-0.83) and sensitivity (0.64-0.86) for localizing HGM language sites across the tasks. BESPoC also compared well with HGM across all language tasks for localizing ESM speech/language sites. With conventional tasks, BESPoC was superior to HGM for modeling neuropsychological deficits seen, despite preserving ESM speech/language sites. Naturalistic conversation compared well with standard tasks for localization of HGM and ESM language sites, and determined neuropsychological outcomes better than conventional tasks.

INTERPRETATION: Using BESPoC methodology naturalistic conversation is shown to produce valid cortical language maps of both expressive and receptive language, and determine neuropsychological outcomes after epilepsy surgery. ANN NEUROL 2025;98:1096-1110.

**中文摘要译文：**
目的：通过电刺激标测（ESM）和高伽马调制（HGM）标测进行颅内语言定位依赖于需要患者持续配合的人为、重复性任务。在此，我们采用一种新方法——行为-颅内脑电-频谱功率相关性分析（BESPoC），检验了非结构化、人际间、自然对话在语言定位中的有效性。我们首先使用成熟的语言任务，将BESPoC与ESM、HGM和神经心理学结局进行对比验证，然后证明了自然对话的有效性。

方法：我们纳入了134名（59名女性）年龄在2-29岁的患者，他们接受了标准的立体定向脑电图监测，并参与了图片命名、听觉命名、故事倾听以及与家人对话等活动。ESM和HGM分析采用既定方法进行。BESPoC方法量化了任务记录期间立体定向脑电图频谱功率与行为标志物之间的相关性，从而无需任何基于试验的分段。大样本量使得我们能够使用混合效应模型来比较BESPoC与HGM和ESM。

结果：在各项任务中，BESPoC在定位HGM语言位点方面表现出高特异性（0.79-0.83）和高敏感性（0.64-0.86）。在所有语言任务中，BESPoC在定位ESM言语/语言位点方面也与HGM表现相当。在使用传统任务时，尽管保留了ESM言语/语言位点，BESPoC在模拟观察到的神经心理学缺陷方面优于HGM。自然对话在定位HGM和ESM语言位点方面与标准任务表现相当，并且比传统任务更能决定神经心理学结局。

解释：研究表明，使用BESPoC方法，自然对话能够生成有效的表达性和感受性语言的皮层图谱，并能决定癫痫手术后的神经心理学结局。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究旨在验证一种名为BESPoC的创新分析方法，用于通过自然对话进行大脑语言功能定位，以替代传统的人工重复性任务。研究通过分析134名患者在标准任务和自然对话中的颅内脑电数据，证实了BESPoC方法在定位语言区方面具有高特异性和敏感性，其效果媲美金标准ESM和HGM。该研究的最大亮点在于证明了自然、非结构化的日常交流可作为有效的脑功能标测工具，这不仅极大减轻了患者（尤其是儿童）的配合负担，也为术前评估和预测术后神经心理结局提供了更具生态效度的精准新范式。

## 5. 岛叶前部与后部癫痫：基于立体定向脑电图的症状学关联研究

**原文链接：** [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40799141](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40799141)
**期刊：** Annals of neurology
**PMID：** 40799141
**DOI：** 10.1002/ana.70015

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Anterior Versus Posterior Insular Epilepsy: Correlations with Semiology Based on Stereoelectroencephalography.

**英文摘要原文：**
OBJECTIVE: To characterize insular seizure semiology and correlate with stereoelectroencephalography (SEEG) seizure onset in a well-defined cohort, in particular examining differences between anterior and posterior insular seizures.

METHODS: We documented all semiological signs and the timing of emergence for 45 patients with SEEG-confirmed insular epilepsy, along with the precise location of the seizure onset zone (SOZ) within the insula. Semiological signs and other non-invasive data were compared between those with anterior and posterior insula SOZ, with more detailed insular subregion description when appropriate. Co-occurrence patterns of insular semiological signs were also investigated.

RESULTS: A total of 87% reported auras and the corresponding insular SOZ demonstrated an anterior-to-posterior gradient by aura type. The absence of aura was significantly associated with an anterior insula SOZ (p = 0.01). Late grunting/moaning (p = 0.07), symmetric mouth elementary motor (p = 0.1), blinking (p = 0.05), and chewing/repetitive swallowing (p = 0.09) also suggested an anterior insula SOZ. Unilateral non-painful somatosensory aura (p = 0.07) and early arm/hand elementary motor (p = 0.1) suggested a posterior insular SOZ.

INTERPRETATION: We report the characteristics and semiological features in a large cohort of primary insular epilepsy patients. The type of aura is crucial for identifying the SOZ and the timing of semiological emergence contributes to the anatomo-electro-clinical localization of an insular SOZ. These findings can enhance identification of insular epilepsy, making targeted curative treatment feasible. ANN NEUROL 2025;98:1111-1124.

**中文摘要译文：**
目的：在一个明确定义的队列中，描述岛叶癫痫发作的症状学特征，并将其与立体定向脑电图（SEEG）确定的癫痫发作起始区相关联，特别是旨在探究前部与后部岛叶癫痫发作之间的差异。

方法：我们记录了45例经SEEG证实的岛叶癫痫患者的所有症状学体征及其出现时间，并确定了癫痫发作起始区（SOZ）在岛叶内的精确位置。我们对癫痫发作起始区位于岛叶前部和后部的患者的症状学体征及其他非侵入性数据进行了比较，并在适当时对岛叶亚区进行了更详细的描述。此外，我们还研究了岛叶症状学体征的共现模式。

## 6. 通过信号复杂度标测癫痫发作后失语：一项立体定向脑电图研究

**原文链接：** [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40646712](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40646712)
**期刊：** Annals of neurology
**PMID：** 40646712
**DOI：** 10.1002/ana.27307

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Mapping Postictal Aphasia through Signal Complexity: A Stereo-Electroencephalography Study.

**英文摘要原文：**
OBJECTIVE: The postictal period provides an opportunity to investigate the pathophysiology underlying aphasia and recovery following epileptic seizures. This study examines postictal aphasia in stereo-electroencephalography (SEEG)-explored patients to identify brain regions associated with task-specific language deficits using signal complexity analysis.

METHODS: We evaluated video-SEEG-recorded focal seizures with and without postictal aphasia in patients with SEEG-confirmed hemispheric language dominance. SEEG traces were analyzed using permutation entropy (PE), with the postictal period quantified by a PE-based metric, the Postictal Alteration Time (PAT). Brain region PAT was correlated with language function recovery (eg, repetition). Electro-clinical recuperation was also assessed within the dorsal-ventral language stream framework. Additionally, a bedside testing battery was developed to evaluate postictal aphasia severity and task-specific deficits.

RESULTS: A total of 322 seizures from 98 patients were analyzed. Seizures with postictal aphasia had longer PAT than those without. Task-specific language recovery correlated with regional PAT (eg, naming - middle temporal gyrus). Moreover, the dorsal stream recovered faster than the ventral stream. Additionally, the Postictal Aphasia Scale (PAS) was developed, evaluating naming, reading, repetition, and comprehension (verbal and written) and automatic speech. Higher PAS scores (indicating milder deficits) correlated with faster regional complexity recovery. At 5 and 10 minutes postictally, PAS revealed a global aphasia pattern, with comprehension deficits gradually resolving. By 15 minutes, aphasia was primarily production-related, particularly affecting naming.

INTERPRETATION: This study provides new insights into the pathophysiology of postictal aphasia and introduces PAS as a tool for assessing postictal aphasia severity and domain-specific deficits, aiding surgical planning and rehabilitation. ANN NEUROL 2025;98:1080-1095.

**中文摘要译文：**
目的：癫痫发作后期为研究失语症的病理生理机制以及癫痫发作后的恢复过程提供了机会。本研究利用信号复杂度分析，对接受立体定向脑电图（SEEG）探查的患者中的癫痫发作后失语进行研究，以确定与特定任务语言缺陷相关的脑区。

方法：我们评估了经SEEG证实存在半球语言优势的患者中，伴有和不伴有癫痫发作后失语的局灶性癫痫发作的视频-SEEG记录。使用排列熵（PE）分析SEEG轨迹，并通过一个基于PE的指标——发作后改变时间（PAT）来量化发作后期。我们将脑区PAT与语言功能恢复（如复述能力）进行关联分析。同时，在背侧-腹侧语言流框架内评估了电生理-临床恢复情况。此外，我们还开发了一套床旁测试组合，用于评估癫痫发作后失语的严重程度和特定任务的缺陷。

结果：共分析了来自98名患者的322次癫痫发作。伴有癫痫发作后失语的发作其PAT长于不伴有此症状的发作。特定任务的语言恢复与区域性PAT相关（例如，命名能力与中颞回相关）。此外，背侧语言流的恢复速度快于腹侧语言流。我们还开发了癫痫发作后失语量表（PAS），用于评估命名、阅读、复述、理解（口头和书面）以及自动言语能力。较高的PAS分数（表示缺陷较轻）与更快的区域信号复杂度恢复相关。在癫痫发作后5分钟和10分钟，PAS揭示了一种全面性失语模式，其中理解障碍逐渐消退。到15分钟时，失语主要与语言产出相关，尤其影响命名能力。

解读：本研究为癫痫发作后失语的病理生理学提供了新的见解，并引入了PAS作为评估癫痫发作后失语严重程度和特定领域缺陷的工具，有助于手术规划和康复治疗。ANN NEUROL 2025;98:1080-1095。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究创新性地采用立体定向脑电图（SEEG）的信号复杂度分析方法，旨在揭示癫痫发作后失语的神经生理机制。通过引入排列熵和发作后改变时间（PAT）这一量化指标，研究成功地将特定脑区的电生理恢复过程与命名、复述等具体语言功能的恢复动态关联起来。其关键发现不仅深化了对背侧与腹侧语言流恢复差异的理解，更重要的是，开发并验证了“癫痫发作后失语量表（PAS）”，为临床快速评估、手术规划及康复提供了极具价值的客观工具。
