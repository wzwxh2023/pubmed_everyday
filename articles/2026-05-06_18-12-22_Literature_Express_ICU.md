**英文原标题：** Evaluating the efficacy of ceftazidime/avibactam plus amikacin combination therapy as a carbapenem-sparing agent against ceftazidime/avibactam-resistant  using a hollow fiber infection model: a proof-of-concept study.

> **英文摘要：**
> Carbapenems, often regarded as the last line of defense in treatment of urinary tract infections and urosepsis caused by , are under pressure from emerging resistance. Thus, it is necessary to investigate carbapenem-sparing agents to mitigate the dissemination of carbapenem resistance. In this study, we aimed to evaluate the efficacy of ceftazidime/avibactam plus amikacin combination therapy against two ceftazidime/avibactam-resistant  clinical isolates harboring IMP-4 carbapenemase (CTAP #226 and CTAP #233). To achieve this, we used a hollow fiber infection model (HFIM) with clinically relevant regimens of amikacin (15 mg/kg q24h) and ceftazidime/avibactam (2 g ceftazidime/0.5 g avibactam q8h), which were administered to the HFIM either alone or in combination over a 7-day treatment course. Initial inoculum was ~1 × 10 CFU/mL. Both amikacin and ceftazidime/avibactam monotherapies resulted in bacterial killing (~4 log and ~3 log, respectively) within the first 8 h; however, regrowth surpassing the baseline was observed over the following 7 days. Conversely, combination therapy resulted in bacterial killing to <the limit of quantification (10 CFU/mL) within the first 48 h, which was sustained for the entirety of the experiment. This study showed that the combination of amikacin and ceftazidime/avibactam was superior to both monotherapies and therefore presents a promising carbapenem-sparing option for the treatment of ceftazidime/avibactam-resistant . Further clinical studies are required to assess the suitability for use .

## 3. 重症疾病幸存者的家庭收入下降与失业：一项全国性队列研究。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42089734)
**期刊：** Critical care medicine
**PMID：** 42089734
**DOI：** 10.1097/CCM.0000000000007152

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Household Income Decline and Job Loss Among Survivors of Critical Illness: A Nationwide Cohort Study.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVES: To assess the socioeconomic consequences of critical illness by quantifying changes in household income level and unemployment among ICU survivors in a universal health coverage setting.
> 
> DESIGN, SETTING, AND PATIENTS: This nationwide retrospective cohort study used the Korean National Health Insurance Service database. Adult ICU survivors between January 1, 2020, and December 31, 2022, were included. Patients who died within 1 year or had missing data were excluded. The final cohort consisted of 582,341 survivors.
> 
> INTERVENTIONS: None.
> 
> MEASUREMENTS AND MAIN RESULTS: The primary endpoint was the change in household income level (scale 0-20) between pre-ICU and post-ICU years, analyzed using generalized estimating equation models. The most salient finding was a profound financial polarization among survivors. While the overall cohort experienced a significant and progressive decline in income rank (1-yr ratio of means [RoM], 0.994; 2-yr RoM, 0.976; both p < 0.001), this average masked a severe impact on the highest income quartile (Q4), which exhibited a substantial 6.5% relative drop in mean rank (RoM, 0.935). In contrast, the lowest quartile (Q1) showed relative stability (RoM, 2.198) due to a "floor effect" and transitions into the social safety net. Descriptively, 160,682 survivors (27.6%) experienced income decline, 60,432 (10.4%) suffered catastrophic decline, and 12.3% of 326,125 previously employed survivors were no longer employed within 1 year.
> 
> CONCLUSIONS: Critical illness is associated with progressive socioeconomic deterioration characterized primarily by financial polarization. Even with universal health coverage, the economic burden hits previously high-earning households most severely, while lower income groups face potential asset depletion before transitioning to social safety nets. Policies integrating sustained financial protection and vocational rehabilitation into post-ICU survivorship care are essential.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：通过量化在全民健康覆盖环境下ICU幸存者家庭收入水平的变化和失业情况，评估重症疾病的社会经济后果。
> 
> 设计、地点与患者：本项全国性回顾性队列研究使用了韩国国民健康保险服务数据库。研究纳入了2020年1月1日至2022年12月31日期间的成年ICU幸存者。在1年内死亡或数据缺失的患者被排除。最终队列包含582,341名幸存者。
> 
> 干预措施：无。
> 
> 测量与主要结果：主要终点是ICU治疗前与治疗后年份之间家庭收入水平（0-20级）的变化，采用广义估计方程模型进行分析。最显著的发现是幸存者中出现了严重的财务两极分化。虽然整个队列的收入等级经历了显著且渐进性的下降（1年均值比[RoM]为0.994；2年RoM为0.976；p值均<0.001），但这一平均值掩盖了对最高收入四分位（Q4）人群的严重影响，其平均等级出现了6.5%的大幅相对下降（RoM为0.935）。相比之下，最低收入四分位（Q1）人群由于“地板效应”和向社会安全网的过渡而表现出相对稳定性（RoM为2.198）。描述性统计显示，160,682名幸存者（27.6%）经历了收入下降，60,432名（10.4%）遭受了灾难性的下降，并且在326,125名先前有工作的幸存者中，有12.3%的人在1年内失业。
> 
> 结论：重症疾病与渐进性的社会经济状况恶化相关，其主要特征是财务两极分化。即使在全民健康覆盖下，经济负担对既往高收入家庭的打击最为严重，而低收入群体在过渡到社会安全网之前面临潜在的资产耗尽。将持续的财务保护和职业康复整合到ICU后幸存者关怀中的政策至关重要。

## 4. 铜绿假单胞菌产生的氰化氢削弱了宿主的先天免疫反应

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42089632)
**期刊：** The Journal of infectious diseases
**PMID：** 42089632
**DOI：** 10.1093/infdis/jiag244

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Hydrogen cyanide generation by Pseudomonas aeruginosa blunts the host innate immune response.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: The biological mediator hydrogen cyanide (HCN) is produced by certain pathogenic bacteria. HCN exerts multiple effects in mammalian cells: at low concentrations it has cytoprotective regulatory effects, whereas at high concentrations it blocks mitochondrial electron transport by inhibiting cytochrome c oxidase, thereby halting cellular metabolism. Here, we evaluated whether bacterial HCN confers resistance to the host immune response. We used Pseudomonas aeruginosa as a model organism, since it is a clinically significant HCN-generating bacterial species and a common cause of nosocomial infections. METHODS: The study used bacterial mutants, macrophage co-cultures, cyanide quantification, phagocytosis and bioenergetics assays, and mouse infection models to assess HCN's role in immune evasion. RESULTS: Compared to wild type P. aeruginosa, genetically HCN-deficient bacteria were more susceptible to killing by immune cells in vitro and were cleared more rapidly in mouse models of systemic infection. Increased leukocyte killing was not due to increased phagocytosis. Pharmacological scavenging of HCN (by vitamin B12 or trihistidyl cobinamide) also enhanced leukocyte bacterial killing. CONCLUSIONS: These findings support the concept that HCN acts as a bacterial defense mechanism against the host's immune response. Targeting bacterial HCN (through inhibition of its biogenesis or by scavenging) could be a potential therapeutic strategy to improve the immune clearance of P. aeruginosa infections.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：生物介质氰化氢（HCN）由某些致病细菌产生。HCN在哺乳动物细胞中发挥多种作用：在低浓度下具有细胞保护调节作用，而在高浓度下则通过抑制细胞色素c氧化酶阻断线粒体电子传递，从而停止细胞代谢。在本研究中，我们评估了细菌产生的HCN是否赋予了对宿主免疫反应的抵抗力。我们使用铜绿假单胞菌作为模型生物，因为它是一种具有临床意义的产HCN细菌物种，也是医院获得性感染的常见原因。方法：该研究利用细菌突变体、巨噬细胞共培养、氰化物定量、吞噬作用和生物能量学分析以及小鼠感染模型，评估了HCN在免疫逃逸中的作用。结果：与野生型铜绿假单胞菌相比，基因缺失HCN的细菌在体外更容易被免疫细胞杀灭，并在全身感染的小鼠模型中被清除得更快。白细胞杀伤力的增加并非由于吞噬作用的增强。药理学清除HCN（通过维生素B12或三组氨酰钴胺酰胺）也增强了白细胞对细菌的杀伤效果。结论：这些发现支持了HCN作为细菌防御机制对抗宿主免疫反应的概念。靶向细菌HCN（通过抑制其生物合成或通过清除）可能是改善铜绿假单胞菌感染免疫清除的一种潜在治疗策略。

## 5. 非创伤性院外心脏骤停中院前主动脉复苏性血管内球囊止血术（REBOARREST）：一项国际、多中心、开放标签、实用性随机对照试验

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42087223)
**期刊：** Critical care (London, England)
**PMID：** 42087223
**DOI：** 10.1186/s13054-026-06057-y

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Prehospital resuscitative endovascular balloon occlusion of the aorta in non-traumatic out-of-hospital cardiac arrest (REBOARREST): an international, multicentre, open label, pragmatic, randomised, controlled trial.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Most patients with out-of-hospital cardiac arrest do not achieve sustained return of spontaneous circulation (ROSC). Resuscitative endovascular balloon occlusion of the aorta (REBOA) may increase blood pressure proximal to the ballon. If this technique is used during advanced life support (ALS), and occlusion is performed in the thoracic aorta, it may augment aortic pressure and coronary perfusion pressure. We investigated whether prehospital REBOA as an adjunct to ALS increased the rate of ROSC. METHODS: REBOARREST was a pragmatic, parallel-group, multicentre, randomised controlled trial conducted at 12 sites in Norway, Denmark, and Italy. Adult patients (18-80 years) with non-traumatic out-of-hospital cardiac arrest were randomly assigned (1:1) to either a control group that received ALS or to an intervention group that received ALS combined with REBOA as an adjunct. Fulfilment of eligibility criteria was determined by the physician on scene and sealed envelopes were used to allocate patients. The statistician that performed the analyses was blinded for group allocation. The primary outcome was sustained ROSC, defined as lasting ≥ 20 min, assessed in the intention-to-treat population. RESULTS: From June 7, 2021, to June 28, 2025, 200 patients were randomly assigned to the study groups. Due to lack of consent 21 patients dropped out of the trial, hence data from 179 patients are presented, 88 in the intervention group and 91 in the control group. Most patients were male (76%), with median age of 68 years (IQR 58-74). Median time from arrest to randomisation was 33 min (IQR 23-39) in the intervention group and 29 min (IQR 23-38) in the control group. Twenty-five of 88 patients (28%) in the intervention group and 24 of 91 patients (26%) in the control group achieved sustained ROSC (adjusted risk difference 1.8% [-11, 15, 95% CI], p = 0.78). Adverse events were registered in 19 patients. CONCLUSIONS: Among patients with non-traumatic out-of-hospital cardiac arrest, a strategy of prehospital deployment of REBOA as an adjunct to ALS was feasible but did not significantly improve rates of sustained ROSC compared to ALS alone. Deployment of prehospital REBOA is safe and manageable in a two-person team with low procedure time.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：大多数院外心脏骤停患者无法实现持续的自主循环恢复（ROSC）。主动脉复苏性血管内球囊止血术（REBOA）可以增加球囊近端的血压。如果在高级生命支持（ALS）过程中使用该技术，且在胸主动脉进行阻断，则可能增加主动脉压力和冠状动脉灌注压。我们研究了院前REBOA作为ALS的辅助手段是否能提高ROSC率。方法：REBOARREST是一项在挪威、丹麦和意大利的12个中心进行的实用性、平行对照、多中心随机对照试验。非创伤性院外心脏骤停的成年患者（18-80岁）被随机分配（1:1）到接受ALS的对照组或接受ALS结合REBOA辅助治疗的干预组。准入标准的符合情况由现场医生确定，并使用密封信封进行患者分配。进行分析的统计学家对分组情况保持盲态。主要结局指标是意向性治疗人群中评估的持续ROSC，定义为持续时间≥20分钟。结果：从2021年6月7日到2025年6月28日，共有200名患者被随机分配到研究组。由于缺乏知情同意，21名患者退出了试验，因此展示了179名患者的数据，其中干预组88名，对照组91名。大多数患者为男性（76%），中位年龄为68岁（IQR 58-74）。干预组从骤停到随机化的中位时间为33分钟（IQR 23-39），对照组为29分钟（IQR 23-38）。干预组88名患者中有25名（28%）实现了持续ROSC，对照组91名患者中有24名（26%）实现了持续ROSC（调整后的风险差异为1.8% [-11, 15, 95% CI]，p = 0.78）。共有19名患者登记了不良事件。结论：在非创伤性院外心脏骤停患者中，将院前部署REBOA作为ALS的辅助策略是可行的，但与单纯ALS相比，并未显著提高持续ROSC率。院前REBOA的部署是安全且可控的，可由两人团队在较短的操作时间内完成。

## 6. 严重急性肾损伤延迟策略下早期肾小球滤过动力学及尿素排泄与后续肾替代治疗的相关性研究

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42087193)
**期刊：** Critical care (London, England)
**PMID：** 42087193
**DOI：** 10.1186/s13054-026-06035-4

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** The association of early glomerular filtration kinetics and urinary urea excretion with subsequent renal replacement therapy under a delayed strategy in severe acute kidney injury.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: In severe acute kidney injury (AKI), delayed renal replacement therapy (RRT) strategies allow many KDIGO stage-3 patients to avoid dialysis, but excessive postponement in those who ultimately require RRT may worsen outcomes. Early physiologically grounded markers to identify patients likely to need RRT are lacking. We evaluated whether combining early glomerular filtration kinetics and timed urinary urea excretion could improve discrimination of subsequent RRT initiation under a delayed strategy. METHODS: TUBSAKI is a prospective bicentric ICU cohort including adults with KDIGO stage-3 AKI managed with a protocolized delayed RRT strategy. Blood and 24-hour urine samples were collected at diagnosis (D0) and day 1 (D1). Glomerular filtration dynamics were assessed using kinetic GFR (kGFR), and timed urinary urea excretion was assessed using UUEI. Discrimination for subsequent RRT was assessed using ROC curves and AUC. A combined logistic model (kGFR D0-D1 + UUEI D1) was internally validated by bootstrap, with sensitivity analyses adjusted for SOFA and KDIGO stage-3 oliguria. RESULTS: Among 110 patients, 31 (28%) required RRT. kGFR D0-D1 showed good discrimination (AUC 0.81 [0.72-0.89]), and UUEI D1 moderate discrimination (AUC 0.74 [0.63-0.82]). The combined model showed an AUC of 0.85 ([0.76-0.91]), optimism-corrected AUC 0.83, and acceptable calibration. Discrimination remained stable after adjustment for SOFA and oliguria. Incremental gain over kGFR alone was modest and not statistically significant. CONCLUSIONS: Early glomerular filtration kinetics and urinary urea excretion were associated with subsequent RRT initiation under a delayed strategy. The incremental clinical value of UUEI remained limited in this cohort, and external validation is required before clinical use.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：在严重急性肾损伤（AKI）中，延迟肾替代治疗（RRT）策略允许许多KDIGO 3期患者避免透析，但对于最终需要RRT的患者，过度推迟可能会恶化预后。目前缺乏早期基于生理学的标志物来识别可能需要RRT的患者。我们评估了结合早期肾小球滤过动力学和定时尿尿素排泄量是否能提高延迟策略下后续启动RRT的辨别能力。方法：TUBSAKI是一项前瞻性双中心重症监护室（ICU）队列研究，纳入了采用标准化延迟RRT策略管理的KDIGO 3期AKI成年患者。在诊断时（D0）和第1天（D1）采集血样和24小时尿样。使用动力学肾小球滤过率（kGFR）评估肾小球滤过动力学，并使用尿素排泄指数（UUEI）评估定时尿尿素排泄。使用受试者工作特征曲线（ROC）和曲线下面积（AUC）评估对后续RRT的辨别能力。通过自助法（bootstrap）对组合逻辑模型（kGFR D0-D1 + UUEI D1）进行了内部验证，并针对SOFA评分和KDIGO 3期少尿进行了敏感性分析调整。结果：在110名患者中，31名（28%）需要RRT。kGFR D0-D1显示出良好的辨别能力（AUC 0.81 [0.72-0.89]），UUEI D1显示出中等的辨别能力（AUC 0.74 [0.63-0.82]）。组合模型的AUC为0.85（[0.76-0.91]），乐观校正后的AUC为0.83，且校准度尚可。在调整SOFA和少尿后，辨别能力保持稳定。与单独使用kGFR相比，增量收益较小且无统计学意义。结论：早期肾小球滤过动力学和尿尿素排泄与延迟策略下后续启动RRT相关。在本队列中，UUEI的增量临床价值仍然有限，在临床应用前需要进行外部验证。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究通过前瞻性双中心队列，探索了利用动力学GFR（kGFR）和尿素排泄指数（UUEI）预测严重AKI患者在延迟RRT策略下是否最终需要启动透析。研究发现kGFR表现出良好的预测效能，而UUEI虽具相关性但其额外贡献有限，模型表现稳健且经SOFA评分校正后依然成立。该研究的创新性在于将生理学指标应用于延迟透析决策的精准化，但在多指标联合提升及外部验证方面仍有局限。

## 7. 呼吸道病毒感染期间鼻咽抽吸物优于其他上呼吸道采样方法在细胞免疫学中的应用价值

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42085560)
**期刊：** The Journal of infectious diseases
**PMID：** 42085560
**DOI：** 10.1093/infdis/jiag245

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Utility of nasopharyngeal aspirate over other upper airway sampling approaches for cellular immunology during respiratory viral infection.

> **英文摘要：**
> Early immune responses to respiratory viruses in the upper airways, including recruitment of innate immune cells like monocytes and dendritic cells (DCs), dictate disease development. Still, unlike soluble biomarkers, comparative evaluations of upper respiratory sampling methods for immune cell analysis are limited. We longitudinally collected matched nasopharyngeal aspirates (NPA), nostril swabs, nasal curettes, and blood from patients with influenza-like illness and controls. Among the methods tested, NPA yielded the highest numbers of viable immune cells, including monocytes and DCs, while causing similar sampling discomfort and blood contamination, making it superior for longitudinal collection of immune cells from the upper airways.

> **中文摘要：**
> 呼吸道病毒在上呼吸道的早期免疫反应，包括单核细胞和树突状细胞（DCs）等先天免疫细胞的招募，决定了疾病的发展。然而，与可溶性生物标志物不同，用于免疫细胞分析的上呼吸道采样方法的比较评估十分有限。我们纵向收集了流感样疾病患者及对照组的匹配鼻咽抽吸物（NPA）、鼻拭子、鼻刮片和血液。在测试的方法中，NPA 产生的活免疫细胞（包括单核细胞和树突状细胞）数量最高，同时引起的采样不适感和血液污染情况相似，这使其在从上呼吸道纵向收集免疫细胞方面具有优越性。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过纵向对比四种临床采样方法，评估了其在呼吸道病毒感染中获取上呼吸道免疫细胞的效能。研究结果明确指出，鼻咽抽吸物（NPA）在活免疫细胞产量上显著优于鼻拭子和鼻刮片，且不增加患者采样不适感。该发现为呼吸道病毒早期免疫反应的细胞水平研究提供了关键的采样基准，对于优化呼吸道传染病的精准免疫监测具有重要临床意义。
