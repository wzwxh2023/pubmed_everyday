## 1. 向癫痫患者及其照护者沟通癫痫猝死（SUDEP）：来自国际抗癫痫联盟（ILAE）癫痫猝死工作组的范围综述

**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41689717
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70145

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Communication regarding sudden unexpected death in epilepsy to people with epilepsy and their caregivers: A scoping review from the ILAE Sudden Unexpected Death in Epilepsy Task Force.

> **英文摘要：**
> Discussing sudden unexpected death in epilepsy (SUDEP) is difficult and sensitive for health care providers (HCPs), people with epilepsy (PwE), and caregivers. This scoping review examines the literature on SUDEP communication, focusing on need, timing, content, methods, facilitators, barriers, and outcomes. We performed a thematic analysis to identify themes that summarized the perceptions of the epilepsy community regarding key elements of SUDEP communication. We identified 66 articles that met inclusion criteria. These studies included more than 5300 HCPs and 40 000 PwE and caregivers. Caregivers and PwE felt that all PwE should receive SUDEP information. However, the majority of HCPs did not discuss SUDEP. The preferred source of information was an epilepsy specialist, but PwE also valued other HCPs with whom they have a trusted relationship. There was general agreement SUDEP should be discussed at diagnosis, but limited HCP time and training were barriers. Caregivers and PwE overwhelmingly wanted information about SUDEP. Greater education and training about SUDEP for HCP may facilitate communication. Fear of causing distress was the primary barrier to SUDEP communication. However, these concerns, although valid, were often exaggerated. Even in studies that found negative emotional responses to learning about SUDEP, participants regularly reported that the benefits of knowing outweighed the short-term consequences.

> **中文摘要：**
> 对于医疗保健提供者（HCP）、癫痫患者（PwE）及其照护者而言，讨论癫痫猝死（SUDEP）是一项困难且敏感的任务。本范围综述审查了关于 SUDEP 沟通的文献，重点关注需求、时机、内容、方法、促进因素、障碍和结果。我们进行了主题分析，以确定总结癫痫群体对 SUDEP 沟通关键要素看法的核心主题。我们筛选出符合纳入标准的 66 篇文章。这些研究涵盖了 5300 多名医疗保健提供者以及 40,000 名癫痫患者和照护者。照护者和癫痫患者认为，所有癫痫患者都应接收有关 SUDEP 的信息。然而，大多数医疗保健提供者并未讨论 SUDEP。首选的信息来源是癫痫专家，但癫痫患者也看重与其建立信任关系的其他医疗保健提供者。各方普遍认为应在确诊时讨论 SUDEP，但医疗保健提供者的时间和培训有限是主要障碍。照护者和癫痫患者极度渴望获得有关 SUDEP 的信息。加强针对医疗保健提供者的 SUDEP 教育和培训可能有助于促进沟通。害怕引起心理困扰是 SUDEP 沟通的主要障碍。然而，这些担忧虽然合理，但往往被夸大了。即使在发现了解 SUDEP 会产生负面情绪反应的研究中，参与者也经常报告说，知情带来的益处超过了短期后果。

## 2. 癫痫颞叶切除术后初期下降后的言语记忆功能动态模式：术后过程中的性别特异性差异

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41689421)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41689421
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70144

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Dynamic patterns of verbal memory function after an initial decline following temporal lobe resection against epilepsy: Sex-specific differences in the postoperative course.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: This study was performed to investigate the postoperative dynamics of verbal memory function after temporal lobe resection (TLR) in people with temporal lobe epilepsy (PwTLE), with a specific focus on the course following an initial verbal memory decline and the factors influencing later changes. METHODS: Verbal memory performance of 169 PwTLE was retrospectively analyzed at three time points: preoperatively (T1), 6 months postoperatively (T2), and 24 months postoperatively (T3). At the group level, repeated measures analysis of variance (RM-ANOVA) was used to evaluate differences in verbal memory across time points between patients who underwent resection in the language-dominant hemisphere and language-nondominant hemisphere. At the individual level, frequencies of significant verbal memory changes between time points were calculated. Subsequently, factors influencing late changes (T2-T3) after an initial decline (T1-T2) were analyzed for the language-dominant resected group. RESULTS: At the group level, RM-ANOVA revealed a significant interaction effect (p < .001) between time point and resected hemisphere, with significant declines observed in the language-dominant resected group between T1 and T2 (p < .001) and between T1 and T3 (p < .001). At the individual level, dynamic patterns of improvement, stability, and decline were observed across time points. In language-dominant resected PwTLE with an initial decline, RM-ANOVA showed a significant interaction between time point and sex (p < .001); at T3, females exhibited better recovery of verbal memory function than males (p = .04). SIGNIFICANCE: These findings underscore the importance of considering individual verbal memory trajectories following TLR, particularly after an initial decline. Notably, sex emerged as a significant factor influencing late postoperative recovery, with women showing greater verbal memory improvement than men. This suggests preliminary evidence for sex-dependent differences in cognitive recovery and highlights the need for personalized counseling in presurgical decision-making for PwTLE.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：本研究旨在探讨颞叶癫痫（PwTLE）患者在颞叶切除术（TLR）后言语记忆功能的术后动态变化，重点关注初期言语记忆下降后的演变过程以及影响随后变化的因素。方法：回顾性分析了169名PwTLE患者在三个时间点的言语记忆表现：术前（T1）、术后6个月（T2）和术后24个月（T3）。在群体层面，使用重复测量方差分析（RM-ANOVA）评估在言语优势半球和言语非优势半球接受切除术的患者之间，不同时间点言语记忆的差异。在个体层面，计算了不同时间点之间言语记忆发生显著变化的频率。随后，针对言语优势半球切除组，分析了初期下降（T1-T2）后影响后期变化（T2-T3）的因素。结果：在群体层面，RM-ANOVA显示时间点与切除半球之间存在显著的交互作用（p < .001），在言语优势半球切除组中观察到T1与T2之间（p < .001）以及T1与T3之间（p < .001）存在显著下降。在个体层面，在不同时间点观察到改善、稳定和下降的动态模式。在出现初期下降的言语优势半球切除的PwTLE患者中，RM-ANOVA显示时间点与性别之间存在显著的交互作用（p < .001）；在T3时，女性表现出比男性更好的言语记忆功能恢复（p = .04）。意义：这些发现强调了考虑TLR术后个体言语记忆轨迹的重要性，特别是在初期下降之后。值得注意的是，性别成为影响术后后期恢复的显著因素，女性比男性表现出更大的言语记忆改善。这为认知恢复中的性别依赖性差异提供了初步证据，并强调了在PwTLE患者术前决策中进行个性化咨询的必要性。

## 3. 不明原因癫痫婴儿在非诊断性短读长基因组测序后进行全面重新分析的诊断率。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41687048)
**期刊：** Neurology
**PMID：** 41687048
**DOI：** 10.1212/WNL.0000000000214645

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Diagnostic Yield of Comprehensive Reanalysis After Nondiagnostic Short-Read Genome Sequencing in Infants With Unexplained Epilepsy.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND AND OBJECTIVES: The highest incidence of epilepsy in childhood occurs in the first year of life. Infantile epilepsies are associated with substantial morbidity and mortality. Although most are presumed to have genetic etiologies, many infants with nonacquired epilepsy remain genetically unsolved after clinical genome sequencing. The yield of reanalysis after nondiagnostic genome sequencing in this population is unknown. We aimed to determine the diagnostic yield of comprehensive reanalysis after nondiagnostic genome sequencing in infants with unexplained epilepsy. METHODS: This cohort study included infants with unexplained epilepsy or complex febrile seizures who were recruited from 4 pediatric referral centers from September 2021 to March 2024 and had nondiagnostic clinical rapid genome sequencing. We performed comprehensive reanalysis of genome sequencing data from infants and available biological parents using multiple bioinformatics pipelines through July 2025 and clinically confirmed reanalysis findings. The primary outcome was diagnostic yield of genome sequencing reanalysis, defined as the percentage of infants who received genetic diagnoses from reanalysis. The secondary outcome was clinical utility of reanalysis findings. RESULTS: From an initial cohort of 312 infants with unexplained epilepsy who underwent clinical rapid genome sequencing, we performed comprehensive genome reanalysis in 176 infants with initially nondiagnostic results at a median age of 642 days, including 63 female patients (36%) and 30 (17%) with neonatal-onset seizures. The diagnostic yield of reanalysis was 5.1% (9/176, 95% CI 2.4%-9.5%), increasing the overall yield from 43.6% (136/312, 95% CI 38.0%-49.3%) to 46.5% (145/312, 95% CI 40.8%-52.2%). Of the new diagnoses, 6 involved variants not reported by clinical laboratories (2 single nucleotide variants, 2 structural variants, 1 tandem repeat expansion, 1 mosaic variant) and 3 involved previously reported variants of uncertain significance with new evidence. All diagnoses had clinical utility. DISCUSSION: Comprehensive reanalysis after nondiagnostic rapid genome sequencing has utility for infants with unexplained epilepsy. Our findings support implementation of reanalysis within 1-2 years after nondiagnostic genomic sequencing into routine clinical care of children with unexplained epilepsy and the expansion of clinically accredited genomic sequencing to include complex and noncoding variant detection.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景与目的：儿童期癫痫的发病率在出生后第一年最高。婴儿期癫痫与显著的发病率和死亡率相关。尽管大多数被认为具有遗传病因，但许多非获得性癫痫婴儿在临床基因组测序后仍未获得遗传学解释。在此类人群中，非诊断性基因组测序后进行重新分析的收益尚不清楚。本研究旨在确定不明原因癫痫婴儿在非诊断性基因组测序后进行全面重新分析的诊断率。方法：本队列研究纳入了2021年9月至2024年3月期间从4个儿科转诊中心招募的、具有非诊断性临床快速基因组测序结果的不明原因癫痫或复杂性热性惊厥婴儿。我们通过多种生物信息学管道，对截至2025年7月的婴儿及其生物学父母的基因组测序数据进行了全面重新分析，并对重新分析发现的结果进行了临床确认。主要结局指标是基因组测序重新分析的诊断率，定义为通过重新分析获得遗传学诊断的婴儿百分比。次要结局指标是重新分析结果的临床应用价值。结果：在最初接受临床快速基因组测序的312名不明原因癫痫婴儿队列中，我们对176名最初结果为非诊断性的婴儿进行了全面基因组重新分析，中位年龄为642天，其中包括63名女性患者（36%）和30名（17%）新生儿期起病的惊厥患者。重新分析的诊断率为5.1%（9/176，95% CI 2.4%-9.5%），使总诊断率从43.6%（136/312，95% CI 38.0%-49.3%）提高到46.5%（145/312，95% CI 40.8%-52.2%）。在新诊断中，有6例涉及临床实验室未报告的变异（2例单核苷酸变异、2例结构变异、1例串联重复扩增、1例嵌合变异），另有3例涉及此前报告的具有新证据的意义不明变异（VUS）。所有诊断均具有临床应用价值。讨论：非诊断性快速基因组测序后的全面重新分析对不明原因癫痫婴儿具有实用价值。我们的研究结果支持在非诊断性基因组测序后1-2年内，将重新分析纳入不明原因癫痫儿童的常规临床护理，并支持扩大临床认可的基因组测序范围，以涵盖复杂变异和非编码变异的检测。

该研究强调了对初次基因组测序阴性的婴儿癫痫病例进行定期重新分析的重要性。通过多维度的生物信息学手段，研究成功在原有基础上提升了5.1%的诊断率，特别是在识别结构变异、重复扩增及嵌合变异等复杂遗传因素方面表现突出。研究结果为临床诊疗路径的优化提供了有力证据，建议将重新分析作为标准护理流程，体现了精准医学在罕见遗传病诊断中的持续迭代价值。

## 4. 发作间期癫痫样放电与癫痫患儿的瞬时注意力丧失有关

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41685882)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41685882
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70112

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Interictal epileptiform discharges are involved in momentary lapses of attention in children with epilepsy.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: Attention impairments are common in children with epilepsy and widely impact their quality of life. Interictal epileptiform discharges (IEDs) may induce subtle dysfunctions of various cognitive processes, but data regarding the impact of IEDs on sustained attention remain limited. The objective of the present study was to evaluate the impact of IEDs on continuous undivided attention in children with epilepsy, controlling for the number of treatments, type of epilepsy, frequency of seizures in the past year, age at onset, and comorbid attention disorder.
> 
> METHODS: Using a computerized sustained attention test synchronized with the electroencephalogram in 61 children with diverse epilepsy syndromes, reaction time (RT), errors, attention stability over time, and the event-related potential (ERP) in Pz (related to attentional engagement) were collected. The cumulative impact of IEDs was evaluated using multivariate models controlling for epilepsy-related factors. The transient impact of IEDs was assessed by comparing responses in trials with and without IEDs.
> 
> RESULTS: IEDs were associated with attention fluctuations independently from other epilepsy-related factors. In terms of cumulative impact, a higher IED rate was associated with a poorer sustained attention performance over the entire task. In terms of transient impact, trials disrupted by IEDs were characterized by longer RT and a lower amplitude of the ERP extending over a long time window that included attentional processing (P300).
> 
> SIGNIFICANCE: These results suggest that IEDs may negatively impact sustained attention, independently of other epilepsy-related features. These findings support the hypothesis that IEDs could contribute to subtle attentional deficits and serve as potential biomarkers of abnormal brain function.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：注意力障碍在癫痫患儿中很常见，广泛影响其生活质量。发作间期癫痫样放电（IEDs）可能引起各种认知过程的微妙功能障碍，但关于 IEDs 对持续注意力的影响数据仍然有限。本研究的目的是评估 IEDs 对癫痫患儿持续不间断注意力的影响，并控制治疗数量、癫痫类型、过去一年的癫痫发作频率、发病年龄以及共患的注意力障碍等因素。
> 
> 方法：对 61 名患有多种癫痫综合征的儿童使用了与脑电图同步的计算机化持续注意力测试，收集了反应时间（RT）、错误、随时间变化的注意力稳定性以及 Pz 点的事件相关电位（ERP，与注意力参与有关）。使用控制癫痫相关因素的多变量模型评估 IEDs 的累积影响。通过比较有和没有 IEDs 试验中的反应来评估 IEDs 的瞬时影响。
> 
> 结果：IEDs 与注意力波动相关，且独立于其他癫痫相关因素。在累积影响方面，较高的 IEDs 发生率与整个任务中较差的持续注意力表现相关。在瞬时影响方面，受 IEDs 干扰的试验表现为更长的反应时间（RT）和更低幅度的 ERP（涵盖了包括注意力处理 P300 在内的长延时窗口）。
> 
> 意义：这些结果表明，IEDs 可能会对持续注意力产生负面影响，且独立于其他癫痫相关的特征。这些发现支持了 IEDs 可能导致微妙的注意力缺陷并作为大脑功能异常潜在生物标志物的假设。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过同步脑电图与计算机化持续注意力测试，深入探讨了发作间期癫痫样放电（IEDs）对癫痫患儿注意力的瞬时及累积影响。研究发现 IEDs 与反应时间延长及事件相关电位幅度降低显著相关，证明了其作为注意力波动独立驱动因素的作用。该结论强调了 IEDs 在评估癫痫患儿认知功能中的临床价值，并为其作为大脑功能异常的生物标志物提供了有力证据。

## 5. 迷走神经刺激的快速循环与癫痫患者睡眠相关呼吸障碍增加相关

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41685701)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41685701
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70147

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Fast cycle of vagus nerve stimulation is associated with increased sleep-related breathing disorder in patients with epilepsy.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: This work was undertaken to study the association between vagus nerve stimulation (VNS) parameters and the apnea-hypopnea index (AHI) measured by polysomnography in patients with drug-resistant epilepsy.
> 
> METHODS: Patients with epilepsy who underwent polysomnography with an active VNS device between 2018 and 2023 were retrospectively included. VNS output current and cycle speed, defined as the interval between two stimulation-ON (Stim-ON) periods, were correlated with AHI. Demographic, clinical, and polysomnography characteristics were compared between patients with low (≤1.5 mA) or high (>1.5 mA) output current, and between patients with slow VNS cycle (interval between two Stim-ON of >108 s) and fast cycle (interval between two Stim-ON of ≤108 s). Multiadjusted linear regression analysis was performed.
> 
> RESULTS: Twenty-two patients were included. Output current significantly correlated with AHI, but no difference was found between patients with low versus high output current. Patients with a fast VNS cycle had a significantly higher AHI than patients with a slow VNS cycle. After adjustment for age, number of antiseizure medications, body mass index, and gender, only the cycle speed remained significant, with no significant association with output current.
> 
> SIGNIFICANCE: Cycle speed, defined as a short interval between two stimulations, is associated with increased sleep-related respiratory events in patients with epilepsy treated with VNS. Increasing the duration between two stimulations may help clinicians to reduce VNS-induced sleep-related breathing disorder.

> **中文摘要：**
> 【目的】本研究旨在探讨难治性癫痫患者迷走神经刺激（VNS）参数与通过多导睡眠监测仪测得的呼吸暂停低通气指数（AHI）之间的关联。
> 【方法】回顾性纳入了2018年至2023年期间接受迷走神经刺激设备激活并进行多导睡眠监测的癫痫患者。将VNS输出电流和循环速度（定义为两次刺激开启[Stim-ON]周期之间的间隔）与AHI进行相关性分析。对比了低输出电流（≤1.5 mA）或高输出电流（>1.5 mA）患者之间，以及慢速VNS循环（两次Stim-ON间隔 >108秒）与快速循环（两次Stim-ON间隔 ≤108秒）患者之间的人口统计学、临床和多导睡眠监测特征。进行了多因素校正线性回归分析。
> 【结果】共纳入22名患者。输出电流与AHI显著相关，但在低输出电流与高输出电流组别之间未发现差异。与慢速VNS循环患者相比，快速VNS循环患者的AHI显著更高。在对年龄、抗癫痫药物数量、体质指数和性别进行调整后，仅循环速度仍具有显著性，且与输出电流无显著关联。
> 【意义】循环速度（定义为两次刺激之间的短间隔）与接受VNS治疗的癫痫患者睡眠相关呼吸事件增加有关。增加两次刺激之间的时间跨度可能有助于临床医生减少VNS诱发的睡眠相关呼吸障碍。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究深入探讨了迷走神经刺激（VNS）参数对难治性癫痫患者呼吸功能的负面影响，重点揭示了刺激循环速度而非单纯电流强度是诱发睡眠相关呼吸障碍的关键因素。通过多因素回归分析发现，较短的刺激间隔（快速循环）与AHI显著升高直接相关，这一发现为临床优化VNS参数配置提供了极具价值的证据。尽管受限于回顾性设计及较小的样本量（n=22），其结论对于在保证癫痫控制的同时减少VNS并发症具有重要的临床指导意义。
