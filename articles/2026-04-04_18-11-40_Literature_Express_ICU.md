## 2. 跨越34个国家的脑老化暴露组学研究

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41933172)
**期刊：** Nature medicine
**PMID：** 41933172
**DOI：** 10.1038/s41591-026-04302-z

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** The exposome of brain aging across 34 countries.

> **英文摘要：**
> The physical and social exposome affects human aging, and brain clocks may track its effects. However, most studies neglect multidomain exposures (physical, social and political) across diverse settings globally and their associations with brain aging. In this study, we characterized the associations between 73 country-level physical and social exposomal factors and multimodal brain age in 18,701 participants from 34 countries (healthy individuals and those with Alzheimer's disease, frontotemporal lobar degeneration or mild cognitive impairment). Exposome effects were assessed using generalized additive models and meta-analytic frameworks. Aggregated exposome models explained up to 15.5-fold more variance than individual exposures (delta Akaike information criterion (ΔAIC): 2,034-3,127). Physical exposome was primarily associated with accelerated structural brain aging (limbic, subcortical and cerebellar regions), whereas social exposome was more strongly associated with functional brain aging (frontotemporal and limbic networks). Exposome burden accounted for 3.3-9.1-fold higher risk of accelerated aging, exceeding effects of clinical diagnoses. Findings were out-of-sample validated in cross-sectional and longitudinal designs, remained consistent across clinical subgroups and persisted after adjustment for demographics, age correction bias, cognition, scanner type and data quality. The exposome accelerates brain aging in health and disease, underscoring the need to address physical, social and political inequities.

> **中文摘要：**
> 物理和社会暴露组影响人类衰老，而大脑时钟可以追踪其效应。然而，大多数研究忽视了全球不同背景下的多领域暴露（物理、社会和政治）及其与大脑老化的关联。在本研究中，我们刻画了来自34个国家的18,701名参与者（包括健康个体以及阿尔茨海默病、额颞叶变性或轻度认知障碍患者）中，73个国家级物理和社会暴露组因素与多模态脑年龄之间的关联。研究采用广义加性模型和元分析框架评估了暴露组效应。结果显示，聚合暴露组模型解释的变异量比单一暴露高出多达15.5倍（赤池信息量准则增量 (ΔAIC)：2,034-3,127）。物理暴露组主要与结构性脑老化加速（边缘系统、皮层下和小脑区域）相关，而社会暴露组与功能性脑老化（额颞和边缘网络）的关联更为强烈。暴露组负担导致衰老加速的风险增加了3.3至9.1倍，超过了临床诊断的影响。研究结果在横断面和纵向设计的样本外验证中得到了证实，在各临床亚组中保持一致，并在调整人口统计学、年龄校正偏差、认知、扫描仪类型和数据质量后依然存在。暴露组在健康和疾病状态下均会加速大脑老化，这强调了解决物理、社会和政治不平等问题的必要性。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究利用来自34个国家的超大规模跨国数据集，系统阐明了环境暴露组对大脑老化的深远影响。研究通过精细化的统计模型证明，物理和社会环境因素的累积效应（暴露组负担）在预测脑年龄加速方面甚至优于临床诊断。其核心创新点在于区分了物理暴露对脑结构与社会暴露对脑功能的差异化影响，为全球视角下的公共卫生干预和社会不平等治理提供了坚实的神经科学证据。

## 3. 非典型 IL-22 受体信号传导在白色念珠菌免疫监视过程中重塑口腔黏膜屏障

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41932922)
**期刊：** Nature communications
**PMID：** 41932922
**DOI：** 10.1038/s41467-026-71459-z

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Non-canonical IL‑22 receptor signaling remodels the oral mucosal barrier during Candida albicans immunosurveillance.

> **英文摘要：**
> Mucosal barrier integrity is vital for homeostasis with commensal organisms while preventing pathogen invasion. Here we show that fungal-induced immunosurveillance enhances resistance to fungal outgrowth and tissue invasion by remodeling the oral mucosal epithelial barrier in mouse models of adult and neonatal Candida albicans colonization. Epithelial subset expansion and tissue remodeling are dependent on interleukin-22 and signal transducer and activator of transcription 3 signaling, through a non-canonical receptor complex composed of glycoprotein 130 coupled with the interleukin-22 receptor subunit alpha-1 and the interleukin-10 receptor subunit beta. Epithelial proliferation enhanced antifungal host defenses through the upregulation of antimicrobial peptide expression. Immunosurveillance-induced epithelial remodeling is restricted to the oral mucosa, whereas barrier architecture is reset once fungal-specific immunity developed. Collectively, these findings identify fungal-induced transient mucosal remodeling as a critical determinant of resistance to mucosal fungal infection during early stages of microbial colonization.

> **中文摘要：**
> 黏膜屏障的完整性对于维持与共生生物的稳态以及防止病原体入侵至关重要。在此，我们通过成年和新生小鼠白色念珠菌定植模型证明，真菌诱导的免疫监视通过重塑口腔黏膜上皮屏障，增强了对真菌过度生长和组织入侵的抵抗力。上皮亚群的扩张和组织重塑依赖于白细胞介素-22（IL-22）和信号转导及转录激活因子 3（STAT3）信号传导，这一过程通过一种由糖蛋白 130（gp130）与白细胞介素-22 受体亚单位 alpha-1（IL-22RA1）及白细胞介素-10 受体亚单位 beta（IL-10RB）组成的非典型受体复合物实现。上皮细胞增殖通过上调抗菌肽的表达增强了宿主的抗真菌防御。免疫监视诱导的上皮重塑仅限于口腔黏膜，而一旦产生真菌特异性免疫，屏障结构就会恢复原状。总之，这些发现表明，真菌诱导的一过性黏膜重塑是微生物定植早期抵抗黏膜真菌感染的关键决定因素。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究揭示了一种由 IL-22/STAT3 通路介导的新机制，即通过由 gp130、IL-22RA1 和 IL-10RB 组成的非典型受体复合物驱动口腔黏膜重塑，从而抵抗白色念珠菌感染。研究强调了上皮屏障的动态防御特性，证明这种重塑是适应性免疫成熟前、真菌定植早期的关键一过性反应。该发现不仅明确了非典型 IL-22 受体构型在黏膜免疫中的核心作用，也为理解宿主-真菌相互作用及开发新型抗真菌疗法提供了重要的理论依据。

## 4. 非重症社区获得性肺炎住院患者经验性使用阿奇霉素的相关临床结局：一项目标试验模拟研究

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41931457)
**期刊：** Clinical infectious diseases : an official publication of the Infectious Diseases Society of America
**PMID：** 41931457
**DOI：** 10.1093/cid/ciag222

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Outcomes associated with empiric azithromycin use among patients hospitalized with non-severe community-acquired pneumonia: emulation of a target trial.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: There remains equipoise regarding the benefit of empiric azithromycin with beta-lactam therapy in patients hospitalized with non-severe CAP. METHODS: We performed a target trial emulation using a propensity-weighted cohort from 68 hospitals in Michigan. Adult patients hospitalized with non-severe CAP receiving beta-lactam antimicrobial therapy with or without azithromycin were included. CAP was defined by ICD-10 discharge diagnosis code of pneumonia and >2 signs/symptoms of pneumonia with radiographic findings. Patients with severe CAP, risk factors for multi-drug-resistant organisms, a macrolide allergy, or who received non-standard CAP treatment, doxycycline, an alternative macrolide, or a fluroquinolone were excluded. Time zero was encounter start. The primary outcome was time (days) to clinical stability. Secondary outcomes included composite 30-day mortality and rehospitalization, ICU transfer, and antibiotic duration. RESULTS: Of 66,657 patients hospitalized for CAP between September 2015 and July 2024, 28.5% (19,010) met inclusion criteria, of whom 93.8% (17,822/19,010) received empiric azithromycin. After IPTW, there was no difference in time to clinical stability between those receiving and not receiving empiric azithromycin (3 [IQR 3-4] vs 3 days [IQR 3-4], aHR 1.00 [0.96-1.05], p=.91). Empiric azithromycin was associated with lower composite 30-day mortality and rehospitalization (10.8% vs. 15.1%, aHR 0.73 [0.62-0.87], p=0.0004). There were no differences in ICU transfer (0.9% vs 1.4%; aHR 0.85 [0.48-1.49], p=.57), or total antibiotic duration (6 [IQR 5-8] vs. 7 [IQR 5-9] days, p=.23). CONCLUSION: Adding azithromycin to beta-lactam therapy in patients hospitalized with non-severe CAP was not associated with time to clinical stability but was associated with lower composite 30-day mortality and rehospitalization.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：对于因非重症社区获得性肺炎（CAP）住院的患者，在β-内酰胺类药物治疗的基础上联用经验性阿奇霉素的获益仍存在临床不确定性。方法：我们利用来自密歇根州68家医院的倾向加权队列进行了一项目标试验模拟研究。纳入因非重症CAP住院且接受β-内酰胺类抗菌药物治疗（联合或不联合阿奇霉素）的成年患者。CAP定义为ICD-10出院诊断码为肺炎，且具有超过2种肺炎体征/症状及影像学表现。排除了重症CAP、具有多重耐药菌风险因素、大环内酯类过敏或接受非标准CAP治疗、多西环素、其他大环内酯类或氟喹诺酮类药物治疗的患者。时间零点设定为入院开始。主要结局指标为达到临床稳定的时间（天）。次要结局指标包括30天死亡率与再入院的复合结局、ICU转入以及抗生素使用时长。结果：在2015年9月至2024年7月期间因CAP住院的66,657名患者中，28.5%（19,010人）符合纳入标准，其中93.8%（17,822/19,010）接受了经验性阿奇霉素治疗。经过逆概率处理加权（IPTW）后，接受与未接受经验性阿奇霉素治疗的患者在达到临床稳定的时间上没有差异（均为3天 [IQR 3-4]，调整后危险比 aHR 1.00 [0.96-1.05]，p=.91）。经验性联用阿奇霉素与较低的30天死亡率和再入院复合结局相关（10.8% vs. 15.1%，aHR 0.73 [0.62-0.87]，p=0.0004）。在ICU转入率（0.9% vs 1.4%；aHR 0.85 [0.48-1.49]，p=.57）或总抗生素疗程（6天 [IQR 5-8] vs. 7天 [IQR 5-9]，p=.23）方面没有差异。结论：对于非重症CAP住院患者，在β-内酰胺类治疗的基础上加用阿奇霉素与达到临床稳定的时间无关，但与较低的30天死亡率和再入院复合结局风险显著相关。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过目标试验模拟（Target Trial Emulation）这一严谨的因果推断方法，评价了非重症CAP住院患者经验性联用阿奇霉素的真实临床获益。研究结果表明，尽管联合方案未能加速临床稳定，但显著降低了30天死亡和再入院的风险，为现行指南中推荐的联合治疗方案提供了坚实的循证医学证据。该研究样本量大、随访时间长，虽然作为观察性研究存在潜在的混杂偏倚，但在临床决策中具有高度的参考价值。

## 5. 腱蛋白C衍生肽6（TCP6）促进墨西哥蝾螈部分肾切除后的肾脏从头再生

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41931365)
**期刊：** Journal of the American Society of Nephrology : JASN
**PMID：** 41931365
**DOI：** 10.1681/ASN.0000001066

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Tenascin C-Derived Peptide 6 Promotes De Novo Kidney Regeneration after Partial Nephrectomy in Axolotl.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Kidneys possess limited regenerative capacity. While renal tubules are capable of repair following injury, nephron loss resulting from renal mass ablation or disease is irreversible in mammals. In this study, we demonstrated de novo nephron regeneration following partial nephrectomy in axolotls, a model organism renowned for its remarkable regenerative ability. METHODS: Kidney mass ablation was carried out by employing unilateral one-quarter partial nephrectomy in axolotls, and the regeneration process was assessed at multiple time points (0, 1, 2, 4, 8, 12 and 16 weeks) post-surgery. RESULTS: We found that cells migrated into the wound sites and proliferated, beginning at 1 week post-nephrectomy and nearly restoring the cell mass of the contralateral kidney by 4 weeks. Cell proliferation peaked between weeks 2 and 4 at the resected site and was accompanied by fibroblast activation, SOX9 expression and induction of tenascin C. By 16 weeks after nephrectomy, new glomeruli had formed and renal tubules were de novo regenerated and functionally matured. Notably, tenascin C expression was transiently induced, peaking at 2 to 4 weeks post-surgery and rapidly declined as cell repopulation progressed. Knockdown of tenascin C expression reduced cell proliferation and impaired regenerative process. Conversely, administration of tenascin C-derived peptide 6 (TCP6) shifted tenascin C expression to an earlier stage and accelerated both new nephron formation and functional maturation. CONCLUSIONS: These studies demonstrate de novo nephron regeneration following partial nephrectomy in an animal model and identify TCP6 as an accelerator of kidney regeneration in vivo.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：肾脏的再生能力有限。虽然肾小管在损伤后能够修复，但在哺乳动物中，由肾组织消融或疾病导致的肾单位丢失是不可逆的。在这项研究中，我们在墨西哥蝾螈（一种以卓越再生能力闻名的模式生物）中证实了部分肾切除后的肾单位从头再生。方法：通过对墨西哥蝾螈进行单侧四分之一部分肾切除术实施肾组织消融，并在术后多个时间点（0、1、2、4、8、12和16周）评估再生过程。结果：我们发现细胞向伤口部位迁移并增殖，从肾切除术后1周开始，到第4周时几乎恢复了与对侧肾脏相当的细胞质量。切除部位的细胞增殖在第2周至第4周之间达到顶峰，并伴有成纤维细胞激活、SOX9表达和腱蛋白C（Tenascin C）的诱导。术后16周，新的肾小球已经形成，肾小管从头再生并达到功能成熟。值得注意的是，腱蛋白C的表达是瞬时诱导的，在术后2至4周达到顶峰，并随着细胞复群的进展而迅速下降。敲低腱蛋白C的表达会减少细胞增殖并损害再生过程。相反，给予腱蛋白C衍生肽6（TCP6）使腱蛋白C的表达阶段提前，并加速了新肾单位的形成和功能成熟。结论：这些研究证实了动物模型在部分肾切除后存在肾单位从头再生的现象，并确定TCP6是体内肾脏再生的加速剂。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究利用具有极强再生能力的墨西哥蝾螈模型，深入探讨了肾单位从头再生的分子机制，重点揭示了腱蛋白C在其中的核心作用。研究通过系统的术后时间点观察，证明了部分肾切除后可实现从细胞增殖到功能性肾单位形成的完整再生历程，并创新性地鉴定出衍生肽TCP6能够显著加速这一过程。该发现为攻克哺乳动物肾单位不可再生这一医学难题提供了重要的仿生学线索，具有极高的临床转化潜力，尽管从两栖动物到人类的跨物种机制转化仍面临挑战。
