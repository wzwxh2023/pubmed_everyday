## 1. 基于脑电图（EEG）微态衍生的动态网络生物标志物在颞叶癫痫侧向化及结构病因学中的应用

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41744907)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41744907
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70121

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** EEG microstate-derived dynamic network biomarkers for lateralization and structural etiology in temporal lobe epilepsy.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: Temporal lobe epilepsy (TLE) is the most common focal epilepsy but remains highly heterogeneous across hemispheric and structural etiology. This study aimed to characterize microstate-based network dynamics in TLE and evaluate their diagnostic value for seizure lateralization and structural etiology using machine learning. METHODS: Resting-state electroencephalography (EEG) recordings from 150 patients with unilateral TLE (71 right, 79 left) and 65 healthy controls (HCs) were analyzed. EEG signals were segmented into canonical microstates (A, B, C, D), and microstate-specific spatial, and temporal dynamic functional connectivity (dFC) variability metrics were extracted using phase lag index analysis. After two-step feature selection, the appropriate number of features were derived and input into Random Forest, XGBoost, and Support Vector Machine (SVM) classifiers to distinguish: TLE vs HCs, left vs right TLE, and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI)-negative (MRI-neg) vs hippocampal sclerosis (HS) TLE subtypes (TLE-HS). Model performance was evaluated on independent hold-out validation set using receiver operating characteristic analyses. RESULTS: Compared with HCs, patients with TLE exhibited increased duration and occurrence of microstate D and reduced expression microstate B, reflecting maladaptive attentional overactivation and visual suppression. Spatial variability was globally decreased, most prominently in left TLE. SVM achieved excellent performance for TLE detection (area under the curve [AUC] = .98) and lateralization (AUC = .97), whereas classification between MRI-neg TLE and TLE-HS was limited (AUC = .58). SIGNIFICANCE: EEG microstate-derived dFC metrics provide reliable, non-invasive biomarkers for identifying and lateralizing TLE using short duration resting-state EEG recordings. This framework advances understanding of TLE heterogeneity and supports the development of individualized electrophysiological tools for precision diagnosis.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：颞叶癫痫（TLE）是最常见的局灶性癫痫，但在半球分布和结构病因方面仍具有高度异质性。本研究旨在表征颞叶癫痫中基于微态的网络动态特征，并利用机器学习评估其在发作侧向化定位和结构病因诊断中的价值。方法：分析了150例单侧颞叶癫痫患者（右侧71例，左侧79例）和65例健康对照者（HCs）的静息态脑电图（EEG）记录。将EEG信号分割为典型的微态（A, B, C, D），并利用相位滞后指数分析提取了微态特有的空间和时间动态功能连接（dFC）变异性指标。经过两步特征筛选后，提取适当数量的特征并输入随机森林（Random Forest）、XGBoost和支持向量机（SVM）分类器，用以区分：颞叶癫痫与健康对照组、左侧与右侧颞叶癫痫，以及磁共振成像（MRI）阴性（MRI-neg）与海马硬化（HS）颞叶癫痫亚型（TLE-HS）。使用独立验证集的受试者工作特征曲线分析评估模型性能。结果：与健康对照组相比，颞叶癫痫患者表现出微态D的持续时间和发生率增加，以及微态B的表达减少，反映了适应不良的注意力过度激活和视觉抑制。空间变异性在全球范围内下降，在左侧颞叶癫痫中最为显著。支持向量机在颞叶癫痫检测（曲线下面积 [AUC] = 0.98）和侧向化定位（AUC = 0.97）方面取得了卓越表现，而MRI阴性颞叶癫痫与海马硬化颞叶癫痫之间的分类效果有限（AUC = 0.58）。结论：基于脑电图微态衍生的动态功能连接指标为通过短时间静息态脑电图记录识别和侧向化定位颞叶癫痫提供了可靠、非侵入性的生物标志物。该框架增进了对颞叶癫痫异质性的理解，并为开发用于精准诊断的个体化电生理工具提供了支持。

## 2. 乳酸受体 HCAR1 在新生儿缺氧缺血性癫痫发作中的作用

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41744475)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41744475
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70172

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Lactate receptor HCAR1 in neonatal hypoxic-ischemic seizures.

> **英文摘要：**
> Hydroxycarboxylic acid receptor 1 (HCAR1) is a G-protein-coupled lactate receptor expressed in the brain and plays a role in neuronal excitability and repair after injury. Hypoxic-ischemic encephalopathy (HIE) is the most common cause of brain injury and seizures in term neonates. The goal of this study was to describe HCAR1 expression and function in the neonatal brain and understand its role in HIE-associated seizures. HCAR1 expression was measured using quantitative reverse transcriptase polymerase chain reaction in postnatal day (p)10-50 mice. Neuronal properties and spontaneous excitatory postsynaptic currents (sEPSCs) were measured in hippocampal principal neurons from HCAR1 knockout and wild-type mice when exposed to lactate. p10 HCAR1 knockout and wild-type mice were exposed to hypoxia-ischemia (HI) and underwent electroencephalography to compare seizure burden. HCAR1 was expressed at p10 at similar levels to adults. Lactate decreased amplitudes and sEPSC frequency in wild-type but not HCAR1 knockout mice. After HI, HCAR1 knockout mice had higher seizure burden and behavioral seizure scores than wild-type mice. HCAR1 is expressed on neurons and plays a role in neuronal excitability and seizures in the neonatal brain.

> **中文摘要：**
> 羟基羧酸受体 1 (HCAR1) 是一种在大脑中表达的 G 蛋白偶联乳酸受体，在神经元兴奋性和损伤后的修复中发挥作用。缺氧缺血性脑病 (HIE) 是足月新生儿脑损伤和癫痫发作最常见的原因。本研究的目的是描述 HCAR1 在新生儿大脑中的表达和功能，并了解其在 HIE 相关癫痫发作中的作用。使用定量反转录聚合酶链反应测量了出生后第 10 天 (p10) 至 50 天小鼠的 HCAR1 表达。在暴露于乳酸环境时，测量了 HCAR1 敲除小鼠和野生型小鼠海马主要神经元的神经元特性和自发兴奋性突触后电流 (sEPSCs)。将 p10 HCAR1 敲除小鼠和野生型小鼠暴露于缺氧缺血 (HI) 环境中，并接受脑电图监测以比较癫痫负担。结果显示，HCAR1 在 p10 时的表达水平与成年期相似。乳酸能降低野生型小鼠的 sEPSC 幅度和频率，但在 HCAR1 敲除小鼠中无此效应。HI 诱导后，HCAR1 敲除小鼠的癫痫负担和行为癫痫评分均显著高于野生型小鼠。HCAR1 在神经元上表达，并在新生儿大脑的神经元兴奋性和癫痫发作调控中发挥重要作用。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究揭示了乳酸受体 HCAR1 在新生儿缺氧缺血性脑病（HIE）诱发的癫痫发作中具有关键的神经保护作用。通过基因敲除小鼠模型，研究者证实了 HCAR1 的缺失会显著加剧缺氧缺血后的癫痫负担，并明确了乳酸通过 HCAR1 抑制神经元过度兴奋的电生理机制。该研究不仅深化了对代谢受体在早期脑发育中功能的理解，也为开发针对 HIE 相关癫痫的代谢调节疗法提供了新的潜在靶点。其创新性在于将乳酸代谢信号与新生儿神经元兴奋性直接联系起来，但临床转化潜力仍需在人体研究中进一步评估。

## 3. 海马切除范围分级与颞叶癫痫手术的神经心理结局相关

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41744392)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41744392
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70162

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Graded extent of hippocampal resection is related to neuropsychological outcomes in temporal lobe epilepsy surgery.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: Surgical resection for epilepsy seeks to maximize seizure freedom while minimizing new neurocognitive impairments. Tailored resections guided by anatomoelectroclinical (AEC) hypotheses offer the possibility of sparing parts of the hippocampus. The relationship between the extent of hippocampal resection and postoperative neurocognitive outcomes in this context has not been studied and has important implications for clinical practice. We test this relationship in a series of left and right tailored anterior temporal lobectomy (ATL) surgeries. METHODS: We conducted a retrospective analysis of 34 adult patients with drug-resistant temporal lobe epilepsy (18 left, 16 right) who underwent tailored ATL based on individualized AEC hypotheses at the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center. All patients completed standardized pre- and postoperative neuropsychological testing, and 85.3% underwent preoperative stereoelectroencephalography to guide resection. Surgical extent was tailored through a multidisciplinary process integrating AEC correlations and intraoperative electrophysiology. Preoperative and postoperative hippocampal volumes were measured and correlated with changes in verbal and visual memory, as well as language performance. RESULTS: Greater extent of resection of the left hippocampus was significantly associated with worse postoperative outcomes in both verbal and visual recall. Extent of resection of the right hippocampus was not related to reductions in performance across any domain, with some indication of improvements in performance after right ATL surgery at the group level. Seizure outcomes (66.6% Engel I at 2 years) were consistent with the existing literature and did not vary with hippocampal resection extent. SIGNIFICANCE: These findings highlight the critical role of the left hippocampus in supporting both verbal and visual memory and underscore the importance of preserving hippocampal tissue during left ATL when feasible. Our results support the utility of AEC-guided tailored resections as a strategy to balance seizure control with cognitive preservation.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：癫痫手术切除旨在最大程度实现无发作状态，同时尽量减少新的神经认知功能损伤。由解剖-电-临床（AEC）假设指导的个体化切除术为保留部分海马提供了可能性。在此背景下，海马切除范围与术后神经认知结局之间的关系尚未得到研究，且对临床实践具有重要意义。我们在一系列左侧和右侧个体化颞前叶切除术（ATL）中验证了这种关系。方法：我们对匹兹堡大学医学中心 34 例接受基于个体化 AEC 假设的定制化 ATL 的成人耐药性颞叶癫痫患者（18 例左侧，16 例右侧）进行了回顾性分析。所有患者均完成了标准化的术前和术后神经心理学测试，85.3% 的患者接受了术前立体定向脑电图以指导切除。切除范围通过整合 AEC 相关性和术中电生理学的多学科流程进行定制。测量术前和术后海马体积，并将其与言语和视觉记忆以及语言表现的变化进行相关性分析。结果：左侧海马切除范围越大，术后言语和视觉回忆的结局越差，两者显著相关。右侧海马切除范围与任何领域的表现下降均无关，群体水平上右侧 ATL 手术后表现有改善迹象。癫痫预后（2 年时 66.6% 达到 Engel I 级）与现有文献一致，且不随海马切除范围而改变。意义：这些发现强调了左侧海马在支持言语和视觉记忆方面的关键作用，并强调了在可行的情况下左侧 ATL 手术中保留海马组织的重要性。我们的结果支持将 AEC 指导的个体化切除作为平衡癫痫控制与认知保护的一种策略。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过对34例颞叶癫痫患者的回顾性量化分析，探讨了基于解剖-电-临床（AEC）假设的个体化海马切除范围与神经心理预后的关系。研究核心发现左侧海马切除程度是术后言语和视觉记忆损伤的敏感预测因素，而右侧切除对认知影响较小，且缩小切除范围并未牺牲癫痫控制率。该研究的创新性在于将AEC引导的精准手术理念与海马体积定量分析相结合，为“功能保护性癫痫手术”提供了坚实的循证医学支持。局限性主要在于单中心回顾性设计及样本量相对有限，未来需多中心前瞻性研究以进一步确立最优切除边界。

## 4. 使用高分辨率薄膜皮层阵列绘制术中发作间期癫痫样放电图谱

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41744320)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41744320
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70135

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Mapping intraoperative interictal epileptiform discharges using high-resolution, thin-film cortical arrays.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: Interictal epileptiform discharges (IEDs) are transients observed on the electroencephalogram (EEG) of patients with epilepsy. IEDs have traditionally been recorded from scalp or intracranial EEG macrocontacts, which coarsely sample neural activity. Here, we investigated the use of flexible, high-resolution microelectrocorticographic (μECoG) arrays for measuring IEDs with greater spatiotemporal precision to test whether there exist microscale patterns of IED activity that may be missed on standard intracranial EEG. METHODS: We used liquid crystal polymer thin-film μECoG arrays with both high resolution (.76-1.72-mm spacing, 200-μm diameter) and large cortical coverage (144-1596 mm) to record from seven patients undergoing surgical treatment of epilepsy. We identified IEDs by a combination of expert review and automated detection. We quantified the spatial extent of IEDs, mapped patterns of repeated IED activity, and quantified IED propagation direction using multilinear fit models. We also compared IED detection rates and propagation measurements between μECoG arrays and simulated macroarrays (10-mm spacing, 2.3-mm diameter). RESULTS: We demonstrated successful use of μECoG arrays to map intraoperative microscale patterns of IEDs. The majority of patients (5/7) exhibited elevated IED activity that was highly localized (subcentimeter localization). Across all patients, 40% of detected IEDs were observed within a 4-mm radius of cortex. μECoG arrays also mapped the direction of IED propagation. An average of 39% (range = 4.2%-96.5%, SD = ±36.8%) of the IED events captured by the μECoG arrays were not detectable by simulated macrocontacts. SIGNIFICANCE: These intraoperative data demonstrate that μECoG arrays can map the microscale spatiotemporal activity of IEDs. These patterns of IEDs may be poorly captured by standard, macroscale recording devices. Our findings support the use of high-resolution, large area coverage μECoG arrays for the presurgical and intraoperative mapping of epileptic cortex.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：发作间期癫痫样放电（IEDs）是在癫痫患者脑电图（EEG）上观察到的瞬态现象。传统上，IEDs 是通过头皮或颅内 EEG 宏电极记录的，这些电极对神经活动的采样较为粗略。本研究中，我们探讨了使用柔性高分辨率微皮层电图（μECoG）阵列以更高的时空精度测量 IEDs 的可行性，旨在测试是否存在标准颅内 EEG 可能遗漏的微尺度 IED 活动模式。方法：我们采用具有高分辨率（0.76-1.72 mm 间距，200 μm 直径）和大皮层覆盖范围（144-1596 mm²）的液晶聚合物薄膜 μECoG 阵列，对 7 名接受手术治疗的癫痫患者进行了记录。通过专家评审和自动化检测相结合的方法识别 IEDs。我们量化了 IEDs 的空间范围，绘制了重复 IED 活动的模式，并使用多元线性拟合模型量化了 IED 的传播方向。我们还比较了 μECoG 阵列与模拟宏阵列（10 mm 间距，2.3 mm 直径）之间的 IED 检测率和传播测量值。结果：我们展示了成功应用 μECoG 阵列绘制术中 IED 微尺度模式的过程。大多数患者（5/7）表现出高度局限（亚厘米级定位）的增强 IED 活动。在所有患者中，40% 的检出 IEDs 观察于皮层 4 mm 半径范围内。μECoG 阵列还成功绘制了 IED 的传播方向。μECoG 阵列捕获的 IED 事件中，平均有 39%（范围 4.2%-96.5%，标准差 ±36.8%）无法被模拟宏电极检测到。意义：这些术中数据表明，μECoG 阵列能够绘制 IED 的微尺度时空活动。这些 IED 模式可能难以被标准的宏观记录设备捕获。我们的研究结果支持在癫痫皮层的术前和术中制图中使用高分辨率、大面积覆盖的 μECoG 阵列。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过应用先进的高分辨率薄膜 μECoG 阵列技术，深入探讨了癫痫术中微尺度发作间期癫痫样放电（IEDs）的制图。其核心创新在于揭示了传统宏电极技术所忽视的大量微小、局限且具有方向性的电生理活动，数据表明近四成 IED 事件在常规监测中会被遗漏。该研究方法严谨，将微观电生理细节与大面积皮层覆盖相结合，为精准癫痫外科手术提供了更细致的病灶定位方案。其局限性在于样本量较小（7例），但其临床价值在于提出了高分辨率电生理成像在术中导航中的必要性。
