## 3. 定义癫痫持续状态的终点：一项范围综述及标准化框架

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41773460)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41773460
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70169

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Defining the end point of status epilepticus: A scoping review and framework for standardization.

> **英文摘要：**
> Status epilepticus (SE) is a life-threatening neurological emergency with consensus-driven definitions for onset but no standardized criteria for its end point. This gap creates uncertainty in research and clinical practice. We conducted a scoping review to evaluate how end points have been defined in SE research and to identify key areas of variability. Comprehensive searches of MEDLINE, Embase, and CENTRAL (Cochrane Central Registry of Controlled Trials; 1980-2025) yielded 3940 citations (1674 unique). After screening, 207 studies met the inclusion criteria and were charted using scoping review methodology (Preferred Reporting Items of Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses, extension for scoping reviews). Data were extracted on terminology, electroencephalography (EEG) use, temporal thresholds, and definitions of therapeutic success across retrospective and prospective designs. Five domains of heterogeneity were identified: (1) semantic terminology (e.g., "resolved," "controlled," "terminated," "cessation"); (2) EEG confirmation of the SE end point (reported in 54% of studies, with variable criteria); (3) whether time was included in defining the end point; (4) how quickly seizures were judged to have stopped, based on clinical signs, EEG findings, or both; and (5) how long seizure freedom had to be maintained to count as a successful end point. No consistent global or time-based patterns were identified, and overlapping terminology further limited comparison across studies. Despite established consensus definitions for SE onset, the end point remains variably defined, undermining methodological rigor and limiting cross-study synthesis. A unified, consensus-driven framework is urgently needed to standardize SE end points through the use of standard terminology and methodologies, thereby strengthening clinical trial design and facilitating regulatory evaluation of novel therapies.

> **中文摘要：**
> 癫痫持续状态（SE）是一种危及生命的神经系统急症，尽管其发作定义已有共识驱动的规范，但对其终点尚未建立标准化标准。这一空白导致了研究和临床实践中的不确定性。我们进行了一项范围综述，以评估 SE 研究中终点的定义方式，并识别变异性的关键领域。通过对 MEDLINE、Embase 和 CENTRAL（科克伦对照试验中心注册库；1980-2025年）的全面检索，共获得 3940 条引文（其中 1674 条为唯一引文）。经筛选，共有 207 项研究符合纳入标准，并采用范围综述方法学（系统评价和元分析首选报告项目范围综述扩展版）进行了图表化分析。我们提取了回顾性和前瞻性设计中关于术语、脑电图（EEG）使用、时间阈值以及治疗成功定义的各项数据。研究识别出五个异质性领域：（1）语义术语（例如，“消退”、“受控”、“终止”、“停止”）；（2）SE 终点的 EEG 确认（54% 的研究有报告，且标准不一）；（3）定义终点时是否包含时间因素；（4）根据临床体征、EEG 表现或两者，判断癫痫发作停止的速度；以及（5）为了计作成功的终点，癫痫无发作状态必须维持多久。研究未发现一致的全球或基于时间的模式，且重叠的术语进一步限制了研究间的比较。尽管 SE 的发作已有公认的共识定义，但其终点定义仍然存在差异，这削弱了方法论的严谨性，并限制了跨研究的综合分析。迫切需要一个统一的、由共识驱动的框架，通过使用标准术语和方法来规范 SE 终点，从而加强临床试验设计，并促进监管机构对创新疗法的评估。

## 4. 局灶性耐药性癫痫患儿脑网络扰动与迷走神经刺激反应的相关性

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41771009)
**期刊：** Neurology
**PMID：** 41771009
**DOI：** 10.1212/WNL.0000000000214710

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Association of Brain Network Perturbations With Response to Vagus Nerve Stimulation in Children With Drug-Resistant Focal Epilepsy.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND AND OBJECTIVES: Vagus nerve stimulation (VNS) is the most common neuromodulation technique used to treat drug-resistant epilepsy (DRE) in children. Despite this, approximately half of those implanted do not realize a benefit and there are currently no means to preoperatively identify responders. Recent neuroimaging work has suggested that intrinsic differences in brain connectivity may explain some heterogeneity in VNS responsiveness. In the current work, we sought to study whether preimplantation functional network perturbations in relation to interictal epileptiform discharges (IEDs) are associated with VNS response in children with focal DRE.
> 
> METHODS: We retrospectively studied resting-state magnetoencephalography in children with focal DRE (n = 65), recorded before VNS implantation. Beamforming was used to reconstruct source-level estimations of neural activity within a parcellation of 52 cortical brain regions. Static functional connectivity was estimated using amplitude envelope correlation, followed by general linear modeling and network-based statistics to identify spatial networks associated with VNS response (>50% seizure reduction at 6 months). Perturbations in brain connectivity were estimated by inferring dynamic cortical microstates from amplitude envelopes and extracting event-related microstate probability time courses surrounding IEDs. Differences in microstate dynamics after IEDs were assessed using t tests at each time point, comparing responders and nonresponders, followed by temporal cluster-based correction for multiple comparisons.
> 
> RESULTS: A total of 44 children were included in the final analysis (mean age 15 years, 57% male, 52% responders). No clinical variables, including IED topographies, were associated with VNS response. Significant static networks were identified in alpha-band connectivity relating to both VNS response (anterior-dominant, t = 4.52) and nonresponse (posterior-dominant, t = -4.98). From the dynamic microstate analysis, one microstate related to a frontotemporal network showed significantly greater perturbation in nonresponders compared with responders (temporal cluster p < 0.05), in the 500 milliseconds after IEDs.
> 
> DISCUSSION: Our results provide evidence that connectivity of an intrinsic, anterior-dominant network is associated with response to VNS. Responders to VNS are characterized by stronger baseline connectivity of this network and greater resilience of this network to IED-related disruption.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景与目标：迷走神经刺激（VNS）是治疗儿童耐药性癫痫（DRE）最常用的神经调节技术。尽管如此，约有一半的植入患者未能获益，且目前尚无手段在术前识别有效者。最近的神经影像学研究表明，脑连接的内在差异可能解释了VNS反应的部分异质性。在当前的工作中，我们试图研究在局灶性DRE儿童中，与发作间期痫样放电（IEDs）相关的植入前功能网络扰动是否与VNS反应相关。
> 
> 方法：我们回顾性研究了局灶性DRE儿童（n = 65）在VNS植入前记录的静息态脑磁图。使用波束成形技术重建了52个皮层脑区划分内的神经活动源级估计。使用振幅包络相关性评估静态功能连接，随后采用广义线性模型和基于网络的统计学方法来识别与VNS反应（6个月时发作减少 >50%）相关的空间网络。通过从振幅包络中推断动态皮层微状态，并提取IED周围事件相关的微状态概率时间进程，来评估脑连接的扰动。使用每个时间点的t检验比较有效者和无效者，并随后进行基于时间聚类的多重比较校正，以评估IED后微状态动力学的差异。
> 
> 结果：共有44名儿童被纳入最终分析（平均年龄15岁，57%为男性，52%为有效者）。包括IED拓扑结构在内的临床变量均与VNS反应无关。在α频段连接中识别出与VNS反应（前部占优势，t = 4.52）和无反应（后部占优势，t = -4.98）相关的显著静态网络。从动态微状态分析来看，在IED后500毫秒内，一个与额颞网络相关的微状态在无效者中表现出比有效者显著更大的扰动（时间聚类p < 0.05）。
> 
> 讨论：我们的结果提供了证据，表明内在的前部占优势网络的连接性与VNS的反应相关。VNS有效者的特征是该网络具有更强的基线连接，且该网络对IED相关的干扰具有更强的韧性。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究利用脑磁图（MEG）技术深入探讨了局灶性耐药性癫痫患儿对迷走神经刺激（VNS）反应的神经生物学预测因子。研究创新性地结合了静态功能连接与基于发作间期痫样放电（IED）的动态微状态分析，发现前部占优势脑网络的基线强度及其对痫样放电干扰的“韧性”是预测VNS疗效的关键指标。虽然该研究为回顾性设计且样本量有限，但其结果为临床开发术前筛选生物标志物提供了重要证据，揭示了内源性脑网络稳定性在神经调节预后中的决定性作用。
