> **英文摘要：**

MEASUREMENTS AND MAIN RESULTS: A prospective analysis enrolled 151 patients (93 sepsis, 58 septic shock) and 20 controls. ## 2. 高碳酸血症性酸中毒和非呼吸性酸中毒与接受机械通气的脓毒症患者住院死亡率的相关性：一项回顾性多中心队列研究

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41784455)
**期刊：** Critical care medicine
**PMID：** 41784455
**DOI：** 10.1097/CCM.0000000000007086

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Association of Hypercapnic and Nonrespiratory Acidemia With Hospital Mortality in Mechanically Ventilated Patients With Sepsis: A Retrospective Multicenter Cohort Study.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVES: The mortality among patients admitted with sepsis remains high and varies depending on the site of infection. The impact of hypercapnia and acidemia on clinical outcomes in mechanically ventilated patients with sepsis is not well understood.
> 
> DESIGN: Multicenter, binational, retrospective study assessed the association of compensated hypercapnia, hypercapnic acidemia, and nonrespiratory acidemia, in mechanically ventilated patients with mortality in sepsis.
> 
> SETTING: Data were extracted from the "Australian and New Zealand Intensive Care Society Centre for Outcome and Resource Evaluation adult patient" database over a 17-year period (from January 2006 to December 2022) from 201 ICUs.
> 
> PATIENTS: Patients were classified into four mutually exclusive groups based on a combination of arterial pH and arterial Co2 recorded during the first 24 hours of ICU stay: normocapnia with normal pH, fully compensated hypercapnia, hypercapnic acidemia, and nonrespiratory acidemia. Logistic regression and Cox proportional hazards regression were used to examine the association of compensated hypercapnia, hypercapnic, and nonrespiratory academia to hospital mortality.
> 
> INTERVENTIONS: None.
> 
> MEASUREMENTS AND MAIN RESULTS: Fifty-two thousand four hundred five patients were included. Overall compensated hypercapnia (odds ratio [OR], 1.39; 95% CI, 1.24-1.55; p < 0.001), hypercapnic acidemia (OR, 1.68; 95% CI, 1.57-1.80; p < 0.001), and nonrespiratory acidemia (OR, 1.75; 95% CI, 1.61-1.90; p < 0.001) was associated with increased risk of hospital mortality as compared with patients with normocapnia and normal pH. The risk of increased hospital mortality associated with hypercapnic and nonrespiratory acidemia persisted in all prespecified diagnostic subgroups when compared with patients who had normal pH and normocapnia. Compensated hypercapnia was associated with increased mortality risk in neurologic and unspecified subgroups of sepsis.
> 
> CONCLUSIONS: Hypercapnic acidemia and nonrespiratory acidemia within the first 24 hours of ICU admission are associated with increased risk of hospital mortality in mechanically ventilated patients with sepsis. This association remains consistent in all diagnostic subgroups of sepsis.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：脓毒症入院患者的死亡率仍然很高，且因感染部位而异。高碳酸血症和酸中毒对接受机械通气的脓毒症患者临床结局的影响尚不完全清楚。 
> 
> 设计：一项跨国、多中心、回顾性研究，旨在评估代偿性高碳酸血症、高碳酸血症性酸中毒和非呼吸性酸中毒与接受机械通气的脓毒症患者死亡率之间的关联。
> 
> 设置：数据提取自“澳大利亚和新西兰重症监护协会结局与资源评估中心成人患者”数据库，涵盖了从2006年1月至2022年12月的17年间，来自201个ICU的数据。
> 
> 患者：根据入住ICU后前24小时内记录的动脉血pH值和动脉二氧化碳（CO2）的组合，将患者分为四个互斥组：正常碳酸血症且pH值正常组、完全代偿性高碳酸血症组、高碳酸血症性酸中毒组和非呼吸性酸中毒组。采用逻辑回归和Cox比例风险回归模型来考察代偿性高碳酸血症、高碳酸血症性酸中毒和非呼吸性酸中毒与住院死亡率的关联。
> 
> 干预措施：无。
> 
> 测量指标与主要结果：共纳入52,405名患者。与正常碳酸血症且pH正常的患者相比，整体代偿性高碳酸血症（比值比 [OR], 1.39; 95% CI, 1.24-1.55; p < 0.001）、高碳酸血症性酸中毒（OR, 1.68; 95% CI, 1.57-1.80; p < 0.001）和非呼吸性酸中毒（OR, 1.75; 95% CI, 1.61-1.90; p < 0.001）均与住院死亡风险增加相关。与pH值和碳酸血症正常的患者相比，在所有预设的诊断亚组中，高碳酸血症性酸中毒和非呼吸性酸中毒相关的住院死亡风险增加依然存在。代偿性高碳酸血症与神经系统及未分类脓毒症亚组的死亡风险增加相关。
> 
> 结论：入住ICU后前24小时内出现的高碳酸血症性酸中毒和非呼吸性酸中毒，与接受机械通气的脓毒症患者住院死亡风险增加相关。这种关联在脓毒症的所有诊断亚组中均保持一致。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

这项大规模回顾性多中心研究通过跨越17年的超5万例样本，系统揭示了机械通气脓毒症患者早期酸碱失衡对预后的预测价值。研究的关键发现是：无论是高碳酸血症性酸中毒、非呼吸性酸中毒，甚至是代偿后的高碳酸血症，均与死亡率显著增加独立相关。该研究的创新性在于其在不同感染来源的亚组中均证实了这种一致的关联，具有极高的临床参考价值。其局限性主要源于回顾性设计，无法排除治疗干预（如保护性通气策略）导致的“允许性高碳酸血症”对结果的潜在干扰。

> **英文摘要：**
> The use of antibiotics in the hospice setting is complicated by differing patient goals, elevated side effects, and limited information regarding effectiveness. Here, we describe a case and apply the four-quadrant approach (4QA). This includes analyzing the case through the lenses of (1) medical indications related to improving function and reducing symptoms; (2) patient preferences, which vary among patients from complete avoidance of antibiotics to a therapeutic dosage to treat infection; (3) quality-of-life factors that include managing infectious symptoms versus antibiotic side effects; and (4) contextual features, which include antimicrobial resistance concerns and cost considerations. Antibiotics may be beneficial in some cases and should not be denied to patients enrolled in hospice care. More robust evidence-based information about outcomes is needed to inform discussions at the time of enrollment. Applying the 4QA to a case can help determine the best approach for each individual patient.

> **中文摘要：**

## 5. XKR8介导的脂质不对称性破坏协调中性粒细胞细胞外陷阱形成并抑制真菌感染

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41781710)
**期刊：** Nature immunology
**PMID：** 41781710
**DOI：** 10.1038/s41590-026-02456-z

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Lipid asymmetry disruption by XKR8 orchestrates neutrophil extracellular trap formation and inhibits fungal infection.

> **英文摘要：**
> Neutrophil extracellular traps (NETs) constitute a vital antimicrobial defense mechanism of neutrophils, contributing to various physio-pathological processes; however, the role of plasma membrane asymmetry in this process remains unknown. Here we identify Xk-related protein 8 (XKR8), a plasma membrane phospholipid scramblase, as a pivotal regulator of NETs formation. Upon NETs induction, XKR8 is cleaved by caspase-3, thereby disrupting plasma membrane lipid asymmetry via phospholipid scrambling. Mutation of the caspase-3 cleavage site in XKR8 impairs NET formation. Inhibition of calcium signals before lipid scrambling abrogates NET formation, whereas blockade after scrambling does not. Cleaved XKR8 reorients plasma membrane lipids, altering membrane lipid tension and promoting Ca signals through mechanosensitive channels. XKR8-deficient mice exhibit compromised NET formation and impaired control of Candida albicans pulmonary infection, showing that XKR8 is indispensable for neutrophil-driven immune responses in vivo. These findings define caspase-3-XKR8plasma membrane phospholipid scrambling as a central mechanism controlling NET formation and underscore its critical role in neutrophil-dependent antifungal immunity.

> **中文摘要：**
> 中性粒细胞细胞外陷阱（NETs）构成了中性粒细胞至关重要的抗微生物防御机制，参与了多种生理病理过程；然而，质膜不对称性在这一过程中的作用仍不清楚。在这里，我们鉴定出 Xk 相关蛋白 8（XKR8），一种质膜磷脂翻转酶，是 NETs 形成的关键调节因子。在 NETs 诱导后，XKR8 被 caspase-3 切割，从而通过磷脂翻转破坏质膜脂质不对称性。XKR8 中 caspase-3 切割位点的突变会削弱 NET 的形成。在脂质翻转前抑制钙信号会取消 NET 的形成，而在翻转后阻断则不会。被切割的 XKR8 重新定向质膜脂质，改变膜脂质张力，并通过机械敏感通道促进钙信号。XKR8 缺陷的小鼠表现出 NET 形成受损以及对白色念珠菌（Candida albicans）肺部感染的控制能力下降，这表明 XKR8 对于体内中性粒细胞驱动的免疫反应是不可或缺的。这些发现定义了 caspase-3-XKR8 质膜磷脂翻转是控制 NET 形成的核心机制，并强调了其在中性粒细胞依赖性抗真菌免疫中的关键作用。

## 6. PARP7 保护肺上皮屏障免受多种环境威胁

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** PARP7 protects the lung epithelial barrier from diverse environmental threats.

> **英文摘要：**
> Poly-ADP-ribose polymerase (PARP) family proteins are involved in a wide range of cellular processes. Several PARPs are targeted by inhibitors as treatments for cancer based on their biochemical functions; however, the physiological functions of most PARPs and the potential adverse effects of PARP inhibition are unknown. Here, we show that PARP7 is important for lung physiology. Loss of PARP7 in mice increases susceptibility to chemically induced diffuse alveolar hemorrhaging (DAH) and pristane-induced lupus. Single-nucleus RNA-seq reveals that PARP7 is selectively expressed in alveolar type I cells and PARP7 loss increases immune cell infiltration within the lung, indicating a loss of epithelial barrier integrity. Further, PARP7 inhibition in human bronchial epithelial cells in air-liquid interface culture leads to increased barrier permeability after cigarette smoke challenge or bacterial infection. Mechanistically, we show that PARP7 target, aryl hydrocarbon receptor (AHR), mediates diverse cellular responses to cigarette smoke challenge, including loss of tight junction protein Occludin and increased expression of xenobiotic metabolizing genes and proinflammatory genes. Together, our study uncovers PARP7 as a key player in maintaining the epithelial barrier integrity within the lung, which may have important implications for pulmonary diseases and for guiding PARP7 inhibitor use in the clinic.

> **中文摘要：**
> 聚腺苷二磷酸核糖聚合酶 (PARP) 家族蛋白参与广泛的细胞过程。鉴于其生化功能，数种 PARP 已成为癌症治疗的抑制靶点；然而，大多数 PARP 的生理功能以及 PARP 抑制可能带来的潜在副作用尚不清楚。在此，我们证明了 PARP7 对肺部生理具有重要意义。小鼠中 PARP7 的缺失增加了其对化学诱导的弥漫性肺泡出血 (DAH) 和降植烷诱导的狼疮的易感性。单核 RNA 测序显示 PARP7 在 I 型肺泡细胞中选择性表达，且 PARP7 的缺失会增加肺内的免疫细胞浸润，表明上皮屏障完整性的丧失。此外，在气液界面培养的人支气管上皮细胞中，抑制 PARP7 会导致在香烟烟雾挑战或细菌感染后屏障通透性增加。在机制上，我们表明 PARP7 的靶标——芳香烃受体 (AHR) 介导了对香烟烟雾挑战的多样化细胞反应，包括紧密连接蛋白 Occludin 的丢失以及异源物质代谢基因和促炎基因表达的增加。总之，我们的研究揭示了 PARP7 是维持肺部上皮屏障完整性的关键因子，这可能对肺部疾病产生重要影响，并为临床指导 PARP7 抑制剂的使用提供参考。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过结合单核转录组测序、小鼠疾病模型及人原代上皮细胞气液界面培养，系统阐明了 PARP7 在维持肺部上皮屏障完整性中的关键生理作用。研究揭示了 PARP7 缺失通过芳香烃受体 (AHR) 通路削弱肺部抵御环境压力（如香烟烟雾、细菌感染）的能力，并首次将其与弥漫性肺泡出血风险联系起来。该成果不仅填补了 PARP 家族成员非肿瘤生理功能的认知空白，也为正在临床开发的 PARP7 抑制剂提供了至关重要的肺部安全性预警和科学指导。

## 7. 根霉属特异性糖淀粉酶作为诊断毛霉病新型生物标志物的鉴定与验证

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41779678)
**期刊：** The Journal of infectious diseases
**PMID：** 41779678
**DOI：** 10.1093/infdis/jiag126

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Identification and validation of Rhizopus-specific glucoamylase as a novel biomarker for diagnosing mucormycosis.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Mucormycosis is a life-threatening invasive fungal infection. Current diagnosis relies on insensitive techniques with prolonged turnaround times, underscoring the need for rapid non-invasive diagnostic tools. The study aimed to identify the antigens of Rhizopus arrhizus that can facilitate serodiagnosis of mucormycosis and assess their diagnostic potential in patients. METHODS: Two-stage systematic study combining antigen discovery and clinical validation. Using immunoproteomics, we identified immunoreactive protein spots in R. arrhizus secretory extract using pooled sera from six mucormycosis patients. We performed LC-MS/MS for characterizing the most reactive spot. Next, we developed an indirect ELISA using purified protein and evaluated its diagnostic performance in 92 mucormycosis cases and 41 controls (other mold infections [n=22], diabetes mellitus alone [n=19]). Participants were randomly divided 1:1 into derivation and validation cohorts. ROC curve analysis identified optimal cutoff values. RESULTS: Immunoproteomics identified three immunoreactive protein spots (70, 45, and 30 kDa) in crude extract. The most reactive 70 kDa spot was identified as Rhizopus-specific glucoamylase (RSG) by LC-MS/MS and confirmed with specific anti-Rhizopus glucoamylase antibody. In the derivation cohort (46 cases, 11 controls), anti-RSG IgG demonstrated area under ROC curve of 0.933. In the validation cohort, anti-RSG IgG achieved 76.1% sensitivity (95% CI: 61.2-87.4%) and 100% specificity (95% CI: 71.5-100%). Besides, the assay detected antibodies in 85.1% of culture-negative but microscopy-positive cases and in 87.5% of mixed fungal infection cases. CONCLUSIONS: RSG is a potential biomarker for diagnosing mucormycosis. Anti-RSG IgG shows strong diagnostic promise and merits prospective multicenter clinical validation.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：毛霉病是一种危及生命的侵袭性真菌感染。目前的诊断依赖于灵敏度低且周转时间长的技术，这凸显了对快速非侵入性诊断工具的需求。本研究旨在鉴定少根根霉（Rhizopus arrhizus）中可促进毛霉病血清学诊断的抗原，并评估其在患者中的诊断潜力。方法：这是一项结合了抗原发现和临床验证的两阶段系统研究。利用免疫蛋白质组学技术，我们使用6名毛霉病患者的混合血清鉴定了少根根霉分泌提取物中的免疫反应性蛋白点。我们采用LC-MS/MS对反应性最强的蛋白点进行了表征。随后，我们开发了基于纯化蛋白的间接ELISA方法，并评估了其在92例毛霉病病例和41例对照（其他霉菌感染 [n=22]，单纯糖尿病 [n=19]）中的诊断效能。参与者按1:1的比例随机分配到推导队列和验证队列中。通过ROC曲线分析确定了最佳截断值。结果：免疫蛋白质组学在粗提取物中鉴定了三个免疫反应性蛋白点（70、45和30 kDa）。反应性最强的70 kDa蛋白点经LC-MS/MS鉴定为根霉属特异性糖淀粉酶（RSG），并得到特异性抗根霉糖淀粉酶抗体的证实。在推导队列（46例病例，11例对照）中，抗RSG IgG的ROC曲线下面积为0.933。在验证队列中，抗RSG IgG达到了76.1%的敏感性（95% CI: 61.2-87.4%）和100%的特异性（95% CI: 71.5-100%）。此外，该检测在85.1%的培养阴性但镜检阳性病例以及87.5%的混合真菌感染病例中检测到了抗体。结论：RSG是诊断毛霉病的潜在生物标志物。抗RSG IgG显示出强大的诊断前景，值得进行前瞻性多中心临床验证。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过先进的免疫蛋白质组学和LC-MS/MS技术，成功从少根根霉中鉴定出一种新型生物标志物——根霉属特异性糖淀粉酶（RSG）。研究设计严谨，涵盖了从抗原发现到两阶段临床验证（推导与验证队列）的完整流程。结果显示抗RSG IgG检测具有极高的特异性（100%）和良好的敏感性，特别是在解决培养阴性及混合感染的诊断难题方面表现出显著优势，为毛霉病的早期非侵入性诊断提供了极具潜力的新手段。
