> **英文摘要：**
> Antiretroviral therapy (ART) interruption typically leads to rapid HIV-1 viral rebound in people with HIV-1. To develop an HIV-1 cure, insight into immunological mechanisms capable of preventing HIV-1 viral rebound is urgently needed. **英文原标题：** FUT1- and GAL3ST2-mediated cellular glycan remodeling broadly restricts sialic acid-dependent viral infections.

> **英文摘要：**
> Sialic acid is an abundant terminal glycan found on the surface of almost all mammalian cells and is utilized by many viruses as an attachment factor and/or bona fide receptor. Altering canonical cellular glycosylation can affect the ability of viruses to initiate infections; however, no systematic analysis of the effects of altering expression of glycan "capping" enzymes on viral infection rates has been previously performed. We therefore designed an arrayed CRISPR activation screen that targeted 54 glycosyltransferases known to perform terminal glycan modifications and then examined the susceptibility of 12 different viruses that rely on sialic acid receptors on the resultant cell lines. We identified two glycosyltransferases, fucosyltransferase 1 (FUT1) and galactose-3-O-sulfotransferase 2 (GAL3ST2), as capable of inhibiting a broad range of viruses when upregulated, including influenza viruses, respiroviruses, paramyxoviruses, enteroviruses, and coronaviruses. Furthermore, through adeno-associated virus delivery, we confirmed the inhibitory effects of FUT1 and GAL3ST2 on influenza viruses in various cell models, including primary differentiated human airway cells. ## 4. 铜绿假单胞菌菌血症的疗程——一项基于BALANCE随机对照试验的事后亚组分析

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41774760)
**期刊：** Clinical infectious diseases : an official publication of the Infectious Diseases Society of America
**PMID：** 41774760
**DOI：** 10.1093/cid/ciag144

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Duration of therapy for Pseudomonas aeruginosa bacteremia - a post hoc subgroup analysis from the BALANCE randomized controlled trial.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Despite the clinical significance of P. aeruginosa bacteremia, the optimal duration of antibiotic therapy is unclear. We aimed to explore the association of antibiotic duration on mortality in hospitalized patients with P. aeruginosa bacteremia.
> 
> METHODS: This study was a post hoc analysis of all patients with P. aeruginosa bacteremia included in the BALANCE randomized controlled trial comparing 7 versus 14 days of antibiotic therapy for bacteremia. The primary结局 was 90-day mortality. Unadjusted risk differences [RDs] with 95% confidence intervals [CIs] for outcomes were calculated for patients randomized to 7 versus 14 days of treatment. Multivariable logistic regression was used to evaluate independent predictors of mortality.
> 
> RESULTS: Among 157 patients with P. aeruginosa bacteremia, 74 were randomized to 7 days antibiotic treatment, and 83 to 14 days. Patients in the 7-day group were more likely to be men and acquire infection in hospital. Crude mortality at 90 days in the intention-to-treat population was 22/74 (29.7%) in the 7-day group versus 17/83 (20.5%) in the 14-day group (risk difference 9.2, 95% confidence interval [CI] -4.8 to 23.2). Age was associated with 90-day mortality (odds ratio [OR] 1.06, 95% CI 1.02-1.09), but randomization group (7 or 14 days) was not (OR 1.47, 95% CI 0.66-3.27).
> 
> CONCLUSIONS: Among patients with P. aeruginosa bacteremia included in an international trial testing duration of antibiotic treatment, no significant difference in mortality was demonstrated between 7 vs 14 days of antibiotic therapy; however, a limited sample size precludes a conclusion of non-inferiority, benefit or harm.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：尽管铜绿假单胞菌菌血症具有重要的临床意义，但其最佳抗生素治疗疗程尚不明确。我们旨在探讨抗生素疗程与铜绿假单胞菌菌血症住院患者死亡率之间的关联。
> 
> 方法：本研究是对BALANCE随机对照试验中纳入的所有铜绿假单胞菌菌血症患者进行的事后分析，该试验比较了菌血症7天与14天的抗生素治疗。主要结局是90天死亡率。计算了随机分配至7天与14天治疗患者的未调整风险差及其95%置信区间。采用多变量逻辑回归评估死亡率的独立预测因素。
> 
> 结果：在157名铜绿假单胞菌菌血症患者中，74人被随机分配至7天抗生素治疗组，83人被分配至14天组。7天组的患者更可能为男性且为医院获得性感染。在意向治疗人群中，7天组的90天粗死亡率为22/74（29.7%），而14天组为17/83（20.5%）（风险差9.2，95%置信区间-4.8至23.2）。年龄与90天死亡率相关（比值比1.06，95%置信区间1.02-1.09），但随机分组（7天或14天）与之无关（比值比1.47，95%置信区间0.66-3.27）。
> 
> 结论：在一项测试抗生素治疗疗程的国际试验所纳入的铜绿假单胞菌菌血症患者中，7天与14天抗生素治疗在死亡率方面未显示出显著差异；然而，有限的样本量使我们无法得出关于非劣效性、获益或危害的结论。

## 5. 凋亡小体在肾脏疾病中的作用：炎症、纤维化与修复的调控者

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Apoptotic Bodies in Kidney Disease: Orchestrators of Inflammation, Fibrosis, and Repair.

> **英文摘要：**
> Apoptotic bodies are membrane-bound vesicles generated during the terminal stages of programmed cell death and traditionally viewed as inert cellular debris. Emerging evidence, however, positions apoptotic bodies as dynamic mediators of intercellular communication with critical roles in renal physiology and pathology. In the healthy kidney, efficient efferocytosis of apoptotic bodies maintains tissue homeostasis by ensuring immunologically silent clearance of apoptotic remnants. In acute kidney injury (AKI), extensive tubular epithelial apoptosis generates a high burden of apoptotic bodies that can amplify inflammation, endothelial dysfunction, and adaptive immune activation when clearance is impaired, yet promote resolution and epithelial repair when efficiently removed. In chronic kidney disease (CKD), persistent low-grade apoptosis combined with defective efferocytosis leads to progressive accumulation of apoptotic bodies in the interstitium, where their bioactive cargo-including DAMPs, cytokines, growth factors, and profibrotic microRNAs-drives fibroblast activation, extracellular matrix expansion, and fibrosis. In the vasculature, apoptotic bodies derived from vascular smooth muscle cells act as nucleation sites for calcium-phosphate crystal deposition, linking apoptosis to the development of medial vascular calcification in CKD. Together, these findings highlight apoptotic bodies as active regulators of injury, inflammation, fibrosis, regeneration, and vascular pathology. Understanding the determinants of their pathogenic versus reparative effects could yield new biomarkers and therapeutic strategies, including modulation of efferocytosis, targeting apoptotic body-derived signaling pathways, and engineering apoptotic bodies-based delivery systems.

> **中文摘要：**
> 凋亡小体是在程序性细胞死亡终末阶段产生的膜包被囊泡，传统上被视为惰性的细胞碎片。然而，新出现的证据将凋亡小体定位为细胞间通讯的动态介质，在肾脏生理和病理中起着关键作用。在健康肾脏中，凋亡小体的高效胞葬作用通过确保凋亡残余物的免疫静默清除来维持组织稳态。在急性肾损伤中，广泛的肾小管上皮细胞凋亡产生大量凋亡小体，当清除受损时，它们会放大炎症、内皮功能障碍和适应性免疫激活；而当被有效清除时，则促进炎症消退和上皮修复。在慢性肾脏病中，持续的低水平凋亡合并胞葬功能缺陷导致凋亡小体在间质中逐渐积累，其生物活性内容物（包括损伤相关分子模式、细胞因子、生长因子和促纤维化微小RNA）驱动成纤维细胞活化、细胞外基质扩张和纤维化。在脉管系统中，源自血管平滑肌细胞的凋亡小体可作为磷酸钙晶体沉积的成核位点，从而将凋亡与慢性肾脏病中膜性血管钙化的发生联系起来。总之，这些发现强调了凋亡小体是损伤、炎症、纤维化、再生和血管病理的主动调节者。理解其致病性与修复性效应的决定因素，可能催生新的生物标志物和治疗策略，包括调节胞葬作用、靶向凋亡小体来源的信号通路以及设计基于凋亡小体的递送系统。

Here, we report that selected flavonoids-puerarin (PUE), naringenin, and kaempferol-prolong nerve block from S1SCBs 4- to 25-fold. > Introduction Continuous renal replacement therapy (CRRT) is a high-stakes modality central to paediatric critical care, yet maintaining provider competency is challenging in many paediatric intensive care units due to low case volumes and limited access to structured training. > 引言 连续性肾脏替代治疗（CRRT）是儿科重症监护中的一项高风险核心治疗方式，然而，由于病例数量少且难以获得结构化培训，在许多儿科重症监护室中维持医护人员的胜任力颇具挑战。传统的高保真模拟虽然有效，但资源密集，限制了其可扩展性和全球可及性。基于屏幕的模拟提供了一种补充性的低成本方法，以弥补这些教育差距。方法 我们开发了儿科CRRT模拟器（https://crrt-sim.netlify.app/），这是一个免费的、基于浏览器的平台，包含21个交互式案例，旨在模拟真实的CRRT决策过程。该模拟器采用模块化临床决策树和自适应学习原则，案例进展映射至修订版布鲁姆教育目标分类学。我们对2025年7月9日至10月6日期间访问该平台的用户汇总、匿名的Google Firebase分析数据进行了分析。指标包括独立用户数、会话频率、地理分布以及案例层面的临床决策。国家按世界银行收入类别进行分类。结果 在90天期间，来自92个国家（包括25个中低收入国家）的2，578名独立用户产生了3，761次会话。用户活动最高的国家/地区是美国（50.5%）、沙特阿拉伯（7.8%）、加拿大（7.1%）、印度（4.9%）和巴西（3.7%）。44%的用户至少返回进行了一次额外会话。案例层面的分析揭示了显著的实践差异：在一个脓毒症相关急性肾损伤案例（n = 1651）中，抗凝剂选择分别为枸橼酸盐673/1651（40.8%）、肝素589/1651（35.7%）、无抗凝246/1651（14.9%）和依前列醇84/1651（5.1%）（χ² = 214.8， p < 0.001）。在一个涉及幼儿的血流动力学安全场景（n = 650）中，尽管体外循环容量高，仍有446例（68.6%）选择了生理盐水预冲，而非血液[165/650（25.4%）]或白蛋白[39/650（6.0%）]（χ² = 487.7， p < 0.001）。结论 基于屏幕的CRRT模拟是一种可扩展的方法，可用于支持儿科重症监护培训并为未来的课程开发提供信息。
