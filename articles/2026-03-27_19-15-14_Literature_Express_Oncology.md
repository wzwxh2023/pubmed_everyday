> **英文摘要：**
> Defects in nucleotide metabolism and imbalances in deoxynucleotide triphosphate (dNTP) pools are associated with several human diseases, including cancer and mitochondrial disorders. In non-replicative cells, while DNA synthesis is reduced, a continuous supply of nucleotides is essential to sustain mitochondrial DNA (mtDNA) replication and repair. Human all-α dCTP pyrophosphatase 1 (DCTPP1), a nucleotido hydrolase with high specificity for dCTP, plays a critical role in maintaining nucleotide homeostasis, however its participation in mtDNA stability remains unexplored. Hence, reduced DCTPP1 levels restored dCTP availability and increased mtDNA copy number. These findings suggest that DCTPP1 plays a critical role in regulating mitochondrial dNTP pools and that down-regulation of the enzyme may serve as a compensatory mechanism in disorders marked by secondary dCTP depletion. 本研究通过对两个大型独立儿科队列进行高灵敏度误差校正测序，明确了镰状细胞病（SCD）患儿中克隆性造血（CH）的患病率显著升高，从而解决了此前研究中的争议。研究严谨地采用了年龄、性别和种族匹配的对照组，证明了这种早期诱变现象与疾病本身相关，而非由常用的羟基脲治疗引起。这一发现对于理解慢性血液系统应激如何驱动早期克隆演化具有重要意义，同时也为 SCD 患儿的长期健康监测提供了新的科学依据。

## 77. 通过破坏增强子依赖性表观遗传绝缘实现 BCL11A 的沉默

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41191525)
**期刊：** Blood
**PMID：** 41191525
**DOI：** 10.1182/blood.2025030211

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Silencing of BCL11A by disrupting enhancer-dependent epigenetic insulation.

> **英文摘要：**
> The transcription factor BCL11A is a genetically and clinically validated regulator of the fetal-to-adult hemoglobin switch in human erythroid cells. CRISPR editing of an intronic enhancer within the BCL11A gene reactivates fetal hemoglobin (HbF) in adult erythroid cells, serving as the first CRISPR-based therapy for β-hemoglobinopathies. However, the molecular basis for the remarkable efficacy of CRISPR-mediated enhancer ablation remains elusive. Here, we describe a new genome architecture, an enhancer-dependent chromatin rosette, that is essential for epigenetic insulation and the developmentally regulated, hematopoietic lineage-specific expression of BCL11A. CRISPR-mediated disruption of the BCL11A erythroid enhancer impairs transcription of enhancer-driven RNAs and NIPBL-dependent cohesin loading, leading to the destabilization of the rosette structure, loss of chromatin insulation, and epigenetic silencing of BCL11A. Moreover, targeted depletion of enhancer RNAs using antisense oligonucleotides silences BCL11A by disrupting epigenetic insulation, causing HbF reactivation in adult erythroid cells. These findings uncover an essential role for enhancer-driven epigenetic insulation in transcriptional control, presenting a new strategy for the therapeutic targeting of BCL11A.

> **中文摘要：**
> 转录因子 BCL11A 是人类红系细胞中胎儿型至成人型血红蛋白转换的遗传学和临床验证调节因子。通过 CRISPR 编辑 BCL11A 基因内的内含子增强子可使成人红系细胞中的胎儿血红蛋白 (HbF) 重新激活，这已成为首个基于 CRISPR 的 β-血红蛋白病疗法。然而，CRISPR 介导的增强子消融产生显著疗效的分子基础仍不清楚。在此，我们描述了一种新的基因组架构，即增强子依赖性染色质花环（chromatin rosette），它对于表观遗传绝缘以及 BCL11A 的发育调节、造血谱系特异性表达至关重要。CRISPR 介导的 BCL11A 红系增强子破坏会损害增强子驱动的 RNA 转录和 NIPBL 依赖性黏连蛋白（cohesin）加载，导致花环结构不稳定、染色质绝缘丧失以及 BCL11A 的表观遗传沉默。此外，利用反义寡核苷酸靶向消耗增强子 RNA，可通过破坏表观遗传绝缘来沉默 BCL11A，从而引起成人红系细胞中的 HbF 重新激活。这些发现揭示了增强子驱动的表观遗传绝缘在转录控制中的关键作用，并为靶向 BCL11A 的治疗提供了一种新策略。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究深入探讨了 BCL11A 表达调控的分子机制，首次发现并定义了“增强子依赖性染色质花环”这一关键基因组结构。研究明确了该结构在维持表观遗传绝缘中的核心作用，并证明了破坏此绝缘状态（无论是通过 CRISPR 基因编辑增强子还是利用 ASO 靶向增强子 RNA）均能有效沉默 BCL11A 并诱导 HbF 再激活。这一发现不仅为现有的 CRISPR 疗法提供了理论支撑，还开辟了通过靶向增强子 RNA 治疗 β-血红蛋白病的新路径，具有极高的学术价值和临床应用前景。
