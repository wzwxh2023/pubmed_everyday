## 1. 在颞叶癫痫手术中磁共振成像与颅内脑电图异常的多模态整合研究

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41427675)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41427675
**DOI：** 10.1111/epi.70042

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Multimodal integration of magnetic resonance imaging and intracranial electroencephalographic abnormalities in temporal lobe epilepsy surgery.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: Precise localization of epileptogenic tissue is critical for successful surgery in drug-resistant temporal lobe epilepsy (TLE) but is challenging in those requiring intracranial electroencephalography (iEEG). A range of modalities are used for localization, including magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) and EEG, which are typically integrated qualitatively by the clinical team.
> 
> METHODS: This study quantitatively performed retrospective analysis of three modalities in 40 individuals with TLE who underwent subsequent resective surgery: preoperative diffusion-weighted MRI, T1-weighted MRI, and iEEG. Brain abnormalities in gray matter (GM) volume, superficial white matter (SWM) mean diffusivity, and interictal iEEG band power were derived by comparison to 97 MRI controls and 247 subjects with iEEG. We hypothesized that combined abnormalities in GM and SWM could differentiate postsurgical outcomes and adding iEEG abnormalities would improve outcome differentiation.
> 
> RESULTS: MRI (union of GM and SWM) abnormalities were primarily concentrated in the ipsilateral hippocampus and inferior temporal regions. Resection of these abnormal regions effectively differentiated seizure-free outcomes (area under the curve [AUC] = .76, area under the precision-recall curve [AUPRC] = .78, p < .01), corroborating previous results from larger TLE cohorts. Adding iEEG abnormalities improved outcome differentiation (AUC = .92, AUPRC = .89, p < .01; z = 2.01, p < .05). MRI abnormalities were more likely to colocalize with iEEG implantation sites (z = 6.26, p < .01) and iEEG abnormalities (z = 4.34, p < .01) in individuals with favorable outcomes (International League Against Epilepsy [ILAE] class 1 and 2), but not in those with less favorable outcomes (ILAE class 3+).
> 
> SIGNIFICANCE: Combining quantitative MRI-derived GM and SWM abnormalities with interictal iEEG data improves localization of epileptogenic tissue and postsurgical outcome differentiation. Multimodal approaches may offer added value for surgical planning in complex situations.

> **中文摘要：**
> 研究目的：在药物难治性颞叶癫痫（TLE）中，精确定位致痫组织对于手术成功至关重要，但在需要进行颅内脑电图（iEEG）监测的患者中，这一过程充满挑战。定位通常依赖多种成像与生理监测手段，包括磁共振成像（MRI）和脑电图（EEG），而这些信号往往由临床团队进行定性整合。
> 
> 研究方法：本研究对40名接受后续切除手术的TLE患者进行了三种模态的定量回顾性分析：术前弥散加权MRI、T1加权MRI以及iEEG。通过与97名MRI对照者及247名iEEG受试者对比，提取了灰质（GM）体积、浅层白质（SWM）平均弥散率及发作间期iEEG频带功率的异常值。研究假设为：GM与SWM的联合异常可区分术后结局，而加入iEEG的异常信息则可进一步提高区分能力。
> 
> 研究结果：MRI（GM与SWM联合）异常主要集中在同侧海马及颞下区。切除这些异常区域能够有效区分无发作复发的患者（曲线下面积［AUC］=0.76，精确率-召回曲线下面积［AUPRC］=0.78，p<0.01），与以往更大TLE队列的结果一致。加入iEEG异常后，结局区分能力进一步提升（AUC=0.92，AUPRC=0.89，p<0.01；z=2.01，p<0.05）。在术后预后良好的患者（国际抗癫痫联盟［ILAE］1–2级）中，MRI异常更倾向于与iEEG电极植入位点（z=6.26，p<0.01）及iEEG异常（z=4.34，p<0.01）共定位，而在预后较差者（ILAE≥3级）中则未观察到类似趋势。
> 
> 研究意义：将MRI定量分析所得灰质与浅层白质异常与发作间期iEEG数据相结合，可改进致痫灶的定位及术后结局区分能力。在复杂病例中，多模态分析可能为外科手术规划提供额外价值。

## 2. 抗癫痫药物与特发性广泛性癫痫中发作间期癫痫样放电的相关性：基于家庭视频脑电图研究的回顾性探索性分析

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41427654)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41427654
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70072

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Correlations between antiseizure medications and interictal epileptiform discharges in the idiopathic generalized epilepsies: A retrospective exploratory analysis of home video-electroencephalographic studies.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: This study was undertaken to investigate the efficacy of antiseizure medications in reducing interictal epileptic discharges (IEDs) in patients with idiopathic generalized epilepsies (IGEs) and to explore the relationship between IED measures and clinical events. We hypothesize that valproate and ethosuximide are superior to levetiracetam, which in turn is superior to lamotrigine in reducing IED activity.
> 
> METHODS: We performed a retrospective analysis of 577 video-electroencephalographic studies in individuals with IGEs over a median recording duration of 5 days. Clinical variables analyzed for each study included the following: antiseizure medication regimen, seizure occurrence, mean, median, and maximum IED durations, IED rate (events per hour), and IED burden (percentage of the recording containing IEDs). All IED outcomes were measured twice: for all detected IEDs and for IEDs ≥3 s.
> 
> RESULTS: Linear regression analysis of the monotherapy regimens found that carbamazepine was associated with greater IED rates (all: β = 20.0 events/h, p < .001; ≥3 s: β = 2.4 events/h, p < .001). Similarly, regression analysis of the dual therapy regimens revealed that valproate and lamotrigine were associated with reduced IED rates and burdens in the overall cohort (rate: β = -18.0 events/h, p = .028; burden: β = -.62%, p = .031). Additionally, participants with detected seizures exhibited higher IED durations, rates, and burdens than those without seizures, in both the overall and ≥3-s cohorts (all p < .001). These findings do not conclusively support or reject the hypothesis.
> 
> SIGNIFICANCE: Carbamazepine is associated with increased interictal event rate, whereas the valproate and lamotrigine combination is associated with reduced interictal event rate and burden. Our findings also demonstrate that increased IED outcomes are associated with seizure occurrence.

> **中文摘要：**
> 研究目的：本研究旨在探讨抗癫痫药物在减少特发性广泛性癫痫（IGEs）患者发作间期癫痫样放电（IED）方面的疗效，并探索IED指标与临床事件之间的关系。我们假设，丙戊酸和乙琥胺在降低IED活动方面优于左乙拉西坦，而左乙拉西坦又优于拉莫三嗪。
> 
> 研究方法：我们对577例IGEs患者的录像脑电图研究进行了回顾性分析，中位记录时长为5天。每项研究分析的临床变量包括抗癫痫药物方案、癫痫发作发生情况、IED的平均值、中位数及最长持续时间、IED频率（次/小时）以及IED负荷（含IED的记录时长百分比）。所有IED结局均进行两次测量：一次针对所有检测到的IED，另一次针对持续时间≥3秒的IED。
> 
> 研究结果：单药治疗方案的线性回归分析发现，卡马西平与较高的IED频率相关（所有IED：β=20.0次/小时，p<0.001；≥3秒IED：β=2.4次/小时，p<0.001）。类似地，双药治疗方案的回归分析显示，丙戊酸与拉莫三嗪的联合用药与整体队列中IED频率和负荷的降低相关（频率：β=-18.0次/小时，p=0.028；负荷：β=-0.62%，p=0.031）。此外，检测到癫痫发作的受试者在IED持续时间、频率和负荷方面均显著高于无发作者，无论是在整体队列还是≥3秒IED组中（所有p<0.001）。这些结果未能明确支持或否定原假设。
> 
> 研究意义：卡马西平与发作间期事件频率增加相关，而丙戊酸与拉莫三嗪的联合则与发作间期事件频率及负荷降低相关。我们的研究还表明，较高的IED指标与癫痫发作的发生有关。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究利用视频脑电图大样本数据，探讨抗癫痫药物与发作间期癫痫样放电之间的关系，具有较强的临床意义。结果显示，卡马西平可能增加IED活动，而丙戊酸与拉莫三嗪联合治疗可能降低IED频率与负荷。研究进一步提示IED指标与癫痫发作风险密切相关，为个体化药物选择提供参考。然而，该研究为回顾性分析，因而无法完全排除混杂因素，结论仍需前瞻性验证。

## 3. 支持记忆编码的网络枢纽：对癫痫外科认知功能保留的启示

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41427594)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41427594
**DOI：** 10.1111/epi.18705

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Network hubs supporting memory encoding: Implications for cognitive preservation in epilepsy surgery.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: Memory impairments are common in neurological disorders, especially drug-resistant epilepsy, where cognitive deficits such as accelerated long-term forgetting may occur before surgery due to interictal/ictal discharges, antiseizure medications, and disease progression. Postsurgical memory decline affects 40%-60% of patients. Understanding the neural mechanisms of memory encoding is essential for identifying critical network hubs and guiding surgical interventions that preserve cognitive function.
> 
> METHODS: We analyzed intracranial electroencephalographic recordings from epilepsy patients (n = 15) performing a verbal memory encoding task to identify key structures involved in successful memory formation. Functional connectivity was computed using multiple features across several frequency bands. Temporal dynamics of connectivity were examined to characterize pre-encoding and encoding phases.
> 
> RESULTS: The middle temporal gyrus (MTG) consistently emerged as a verbal memory hub across features and frequency bands. The inferior frontal gyrus (IFG) was identified as a beta-band-specific hub via phase synchrony. Temporal analyses revealed that MTG exhibited stable and widespread connectivity differences across all encoding phases, suggesting a role in pre-encoding network priming. In contrast, IFG displayed a transient decrease in connectivity and spatial extent during early encoding, indicating a more focal and dynamic contribution.
> 
> SIGNIFICANCE: These findings suggest that successful memory encoding relies on widespread, frequency-specific connectivity patterns centered on MTG, complemented by transient, localized IFG activity. Identifying such hubs may inform surgical planning by highlighting regions critical for cognitive outcomes, ultimately supporting strategies to preserve memory in epilepsy surgery.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：记忆障碍在神经系统疾病中十分常见，尤其是在难治性癫痫中。此类患者的认知缺陷（如加速性长期遗忘）可于术前因发作间期或发作期放电、抗癫痫药物及疾病进展而出现。术后记忆下降影响约40%至60%的患者。理解记忆编码的神经机制对于识别关键网络枢纽并指导保护认知功能的外科干预至关重要。
> 
> 方法：我们分析了15例癫痫患者在执行言语记忆编码任务时的颅内脑电记录，以确定成功记忆形成所涉及的关键结构。通过多个频段的多种特征计算功能连接性，并通过时间动力学分析来描述编码前期与编码期的连接特征。
> 
> 结果：中颞回（MTG）在不同特征和频段中均稳定地表现为言语记忆的枢纽。下额回（IFG）通过相位同步性被鉴定为β波段特异性枢纽。时间动态分析显示，MTG在所有编码阶段均表现出稳定且广泛的连接性差异，提示其在编码前期网络预激活中的作用。相比之下，IFG在早期编码阶段表现出连接性与空间范围的瞬时下降，表明其具有更局部且动态的作用。
> 
> 意义：这些发现表明，成功的记忆编码依赖于以MTG为中心的广泛且频段特异性的连接模式，并辅之以瞬时且局部化的IFG活动。识别此类枢纽结构有助于外科手术规划，突出影响认知结局的关键脑区，从而支持在癫痫外科中保护记忆功能的策略。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究旨在揭示癫痫患者记忆编码的关键神经网络枢纽，以指导术中认知功能的保留。作者基于颅内脑电记录分析了多个频段和时间动态的功能连接，系统揭示了中颞回为核心记忆枢纽，而下额回在β波段中发挥瞬时动态作用。研究创新地结合了频率特异性与时间分辨性分析，为理解术前和术中认知保护提供新思路。然而，样本量有限及任务范式的单一性可能影响结果推广性。

## 4. 头皮阴性内侧颞叶发作间期癫痫样放电改变大规模脑网络：一项高密度脑电与颅内脑电同步研究

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41427497)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41427497
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70061

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Scalp-negative medial temporal interictal epileptic discharges alter large-scale brain networks: A simultaneous high-density electroencephalographic and intracranial electroencephalographic study.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: Interictal epileptiform discharges (IEDs) observed on scalp electroencephalography (EEG) serve as a diagnostic hallmark of epilepsy. However, only a small fraction of IEDs recorded by intracranial EEG (iEEG) are detectable on the scalp; the vast majority remain invisible on scalp recordings. Nevertheless, epilepsy is associated with important brain network alterations and cognitive symptoms between seizures. Our study aims to investigate the impact of these scalp-negative IEDs on the whole-brain network, particularly those produced in medial temporal regions, given their consistent involvement across patients, their potential implications in cognitive deficit, and their relevance in both epilepsy and Alzheimer disease. To achieve this, we leveraged simultaneous iEEG and high-density EEG (hdEEG) recordings.
> 
> METHODS: We analyzed IEDs occurring solely in the medial temporal regions, not visible on the scalp, in nine patients with drug-resistant epilepsy during simultaneous iEEG-hdEEG recordings. These scalp-negative IEDs were compared to epochs without any epileptic activity recorded in either modality. Spectral and network characteristics were investigated at the region of interest (ROI) for both modalities in different canonical frequency bands.
> 
> RESULTS: ROI time courses reconstructed with hdEEG during IEDs exhibited sharp wave characteristics (cluster-based corrected time-frequency analysis), confirming that some information time-locked to iEEG IEDs reached the scalp. Our findings revealed a significant frequency-dependent, large-scale increase in power and network features during scalp-negative IEDs compared to epochs without IEDs, at the whole-brain level and within specific ROIs, including the ipsilateral hippocampus (p < .05, false discovery rate corrected).
> 
> SIGNIFICANCE: Despite being localized in medial temporal regions, these IEDs have a widespread impact on functional brain networks, potentially contributing to cognitive impairment in epilepsy. This study lays the groundwork for examining their real-time effects on cognition, including in neurodegenerative conditions such as Alzheimer disease, where these IEDs can be present and difficult to detect.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：在头皮脑电图（EEG）上观察到的发作间期癫痫样放电（IED）是癫痫的重要诊断标志。然而，仅有一小部分通过颅内脑电（iEEG）记录的 IED 可在头皮上检测到；绝大多数在头皮记录中不可见。尽管如此，癫痫在发作间期仍伴随重要的脑网络改变和认知症状。本研究旨在探讨这些头皮阴性 IED 对全脑网络的影响，尤其是来源于内侧颞叶区域的 IED，因为这些区域在患者间具有一致的参与，可能与认知缺陷相关，并与癫痫及阿尔茨海默病均有联系。为实现这一目的，我们采用了同时进行的 iEEG 与高密度 EEG（hdEEG）记录。
> 
> 方法：我们分析了九例药物难治性癫痫患者在同步 iEEG-hdEEG 记录期间仅发生于内侧颞叶区域、且在头皮上不可见的 IED。这些头皮阴性 IED 与在任一记录方式下均无癫痫活动的时间段进行比较。针对这两种记录方式，在不同经典频段下，分别在感兴趣区（ROI）内分析了频谱特征和网络特性。
> 
> 结果：利用 hdEEG 在 IED 期间重建的 ROI 时间序列显示出锐波特征（基于聚类校正的时频分析），证实了一些与 iEEG IED 时间锁定的信息确实传导至头皮。我们的研究发现，在头皮阴性 IED 期间，与无 IED 的时间段相比，功率和网络特征在全脑及特定 ROI（包括同侧海马区）均出现显著的、依赖于频率的大尺度增强（p < .05，假发现率校正）。
> 
> 意义：尽管这些 IED 局限于内侧颞叶区域，但它们对功能性脑网络具有广泛影响，可能促进癫痫相关的认知障碍。本研究为进一步探讨其对认知的实时影响奠定了基础，特别是在阿尔茨海默病等可存在但难以检测此类 IED 的神经退行性疾病中。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究利用同步高密度脑电与颅内脑电技术，揭示了即便在头皮不可检测的内侧颞叶间歇性癫痫样放电，也能引起大范围的功能性脑网络变化。研究在方法上结合多模态信号分析与时频校正，提供了对潜隐性癫痫网络活动的量化证据。结果提示，这类不可见的 IED 或为癫痫相关认知障碍的潜在机制之一。创新之处在于首次直接证实头皮阴性放电的广泛网络效应，但样本量较小及患者群体局限于难治性癫痫者，可能影响结果的外推性。

## 5. 儿童发热感染相关癫痫综合征中的双侧壳核对称征：诊断价值及临床意义

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41427492)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41427492
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70064

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** The symmetrical claustrum sign in pediatric febrile infection-related epilepsy syndrome: Diagnostic value and clinical implications.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: This study aimed to assess the diagnostic value of symmetrical claustrum sign (SCS) in children with febrile infection-related epilepsy syndrome (FIRES), and describe the clinical features, treatment responses, and prognosis of children with FIRES accompanied by symmetrical claustrum lesions (FIRES-C).
> 
> METHODS: A retrospective cohort study conducted at Beijing Children's Hospital from January 2015 to April 2024 included children with "fever and convulsions" who met the inclusion criteria. The diagnostic efficacy of SCS was calculated by analyzing the sensitivity and specificity.
> 
> RESULTS: The study enrolled 1105 patients, including 129 with FIRES, 35 of whom were diagnosed with FIRES-C (median onset age: 8.3 years; 18 male). In the cohort of patients with status epilepticus (n = 347), the sensitivity of SCS was 27.1% (95% confidence interval [CI], 19.8%-35.9%), and the specificity was 95.4% (95% CI, 91.8%-97.4%). The median time to develop bilateral claustrum lesions was 15.8 days (interquartile range [IQR] 11-19). The results of the magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) scans revealed that 7 patients (20%) had isolated claustrum lesions, whereas 28 patients (80%) had extra-claustrum lesions. After a median follow-up of 42 months, 14 of the 29 patients (48.3%) who survived exhibited moderate to severe deficits (modified Rankin scale [mRS] ≥3) and 2 patients died. When compared to FIRES patients without claustrum lesions (n = 94), those with FIRES-C (n = 35) demonstrated a tendency toward milder disease severity, characterized by higher Glasgow Coma Scale (GCS) scores and lower incidence of non-midazolam anesthetic use and respiratory support requirements (p < .001). However, no significant differences in prognosis were observed between the two groups (p = .322).
> 
> SIGNIFICANCE: SCS exhibit high specificity but limited sensitivity in the diagnosis of FIRES in pediatric patients. SCS may serve as a biomarker to assist in the diagnostic process and is typically associated with a less severe clinical progression.

> **中文摘要：**
> 研究目的：本研究旨在评估儿童发热感染相关癫痫综合征（FIRES）中双侧壳核对称征（SCS）的诊断价值，并描述伴有双侧壳核病变的FIRES（FIRES-C）患儿的临床特征、治疗反应及预后。
> 
> 研究方法：本研究为回顾性队列研究，在2015年1月至2024年4月于北京儿童医院开展，纳入符合纳入标准的“发热伴惊厥”儿童。通过分析灵敏度和特异性计算SCS的诊断效能。
> 
> 研究结果：共有1105名患者入组，其中129例为FIRES，35例被诊断为FIRES-C（发病中位年龄8.3岁；男性18例）。在347例癫痫持续状态患者中，SCS的灵敏度为27.1%（95%置信区间[CI]，19.8%-35.9%），特异性为95.4%（95% CI，91.8%-97.4%）。双侧壳核病变出现的中位时间为15.8天（四分位距[IQR] 11-19）。磁共振成像（MRI）结果显示，7例（20%）为单纯壳核病变，28例（80%）伴有壳核外病变。经过中位42个月的随访，29名存活患儿中有14例（48.3%）出现中度至重度功能缺损（改良Rankin量表mRS ≥3），另有2例死亡。与无壳核病变的FIRES患儿（n=94）相比，FIRES-C患儿（n=35）病情相对较轻，表现为较高的格拉斯哥昏迷评分（GCS）、较少使用非咪达唑仑类麻醉药及较低的呼吸支持需求（p<.001）。然而，两组之间的预后差异无统计学意义（p=.322）。
> 
> 研究意义：SCS在儿童FIRES诊断中具有较高特异性但灵敏度有限。SCS可作为辅助诊断的生物标志，并常与较轻的临床病程相关。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究聚焦于儿童发热感染相关癫痫综合征中双侧壳核对称征的诊断价值，是影像学与临床表现结合的代表性研究。研究以大型回顾性队列为基础，系统评估了SCS的灵敏度与特异性，发现其高特异性可为早期诊断提供参考。结果表明，FIRES伴壳核病变患儿病情似较轻，但预后差异不显著。研究创新在于提出SCS作为潜在影像生物标志的价值，但受限于回顾性设计及样本异质性，仍需前瞻性验证。

## 6. 通过手术确诊的颞叶脑膨出所获得的磁共振成像新见解支持“牵拉”作为可能的癫痫发生机制。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41424127)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41424127
**DOI：** 10.1002/epi.70062

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Novel magnetic resonance imaging insights from surgically confirmed temporal lobe encephaloceles support tethering as a potential mechanism of epileptogenesis.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: Preoperative identification of temporal encephaloceles is challenging, and their epileptogenic mechanisms remain unclear. Patients with refractory temporal lobe epilepsy in the setting of encephalocele often benefit from surgical resection, at times requiring stereoelectroencephalography. This study investigates two novel magnetic resonance imaging (MRI)-based metrics in Talairach space, frontotemporal aperture (FTA) and opercular angle (OA), to detect the presence of encephalocele.
> 
> METHODS: We present a retrospective analysis of 31 patients with refractory temporal lobe epilepsy and temporal encephalocele, treated by resection at Cleveland Clinic Epilepsy Center from 2016 to 2024. All cases were surgically confirmed to have presence of an ipsilateral encephalocele. Preoperative MRI measured two novel indices, the FTA and OA, according to a standardized method based on the Talairach referential system. Statistical comparisons were made between the epileptogenic (ipsilateral) side harboring an encephalocele and the contralateral (nonoperative) side. Data collection included clinical and imaging features, and surgery type. Seizure outcomes were assessed by International League Against Epilepsy (ILAE) class at last follow-up.
> 
> RESULTS: Increased FTA and OA differences were observed between the ipsilateral (operated) side harboring an encephalocele compared to the contralateral (nonoperated) side, suggesting asymmetric ipsilateral Sylvian fissure expansion and tethered-related effects; similar analysis in a limited cohort of 14 patients with unilateral mesial temporal sclerosis did not show the same effect. In a subgroup of 20 encephalocele patients with ≥1-year follow-up, 50% achieved seizure freedom (ILAE class 1) and 80% had favorable (ILAE class 1-3) outcomes. In six patients with bilateral encephaloceles, five had contralateral lesions with larger volumes than those residing on the ipsilateral surgical side.
> 
> SIGNIFICANCE: Careful review of preoperative imaging using two novel MRI indices may improve diagnosis of temporal encephaloceles. Our findings affirm the possibility that epileptogenicity of encephaloceles may be partly ascribed to temporal lobe tethering. Their early identification can refine surgical decision-making for patients with intractable, focal temporal lobe epilepsy.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：术前识别颞叶脑膨出具有挑战性，其癫痫发生机制仍不明确。在脑膨出背景下的难治性颞叶癫痫患者通常可从外科切除中获益，有时需借助立体脑电图检查。本研究旨在基于Talairach空间提出并探讨两项新的磁共振成像（MRI）指标——额颞开口角（frontotemporal aperture, FTA）和盖部角（opercular angle, OA），用于检测脑膨出的存在。
> 
> 方法：我们对2016年至2024年间在克利夫兰诊所癫痫中心接受切除术的31例难治性颞叶癫痫合并颞叶脑膨出患者进行了回顾性分析。所有病例术中均确证存在同侧脑膨出。术前MRI依照基于Talairach参考系统的标准化方法测量两项新指标FTA与OA。统计比较在具有脑膨出的致癫痫（同侧）半球与对侧（未手术）半球之间进行。数据采集内容包括临床与影像学特征以及手术类型。癫痫控制效果按国际抗癫痫联盟（ILAE）分级在末次随访时评估。
> 
> 结果：在存在脑膨出的同侧（手术侧）与对侧（未手术侧）之间观察到FTA与OA差异显著增大，提示同侧外侧裂的不对称扩大及与“牵拉效应”相关的结构改变；而在14例单侧内侧颞叶硬化的对照患者中未见相同结果。在20例有≥1年随访的脑膨出患者亚组中，50%实现了癫痫完全缓解（ILAE 1级），80%取得了良好结局（ILAE 1–3级）。在6例双侧脑膨出患者中，5例对侧病灶体积大于手术侧。
> 
> 结论：应用两项新型MRI指标对术前影像进行仔细审查可提高颞叶脑膨出的诊断准确性。研究结果支持脑膨出的癫痫致病性部分可能归因于颞叶的牵拉作用。早期识别此类病变有助于优化难治性局灶性颞叶癫痫患者的外科决策。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过建立基于Talairach空间的新型MRI定量指标（FTA与OA），探讨了颞叶脑膨出的结构异常与癫痫发生机制之间的联系。结果显示脑膨出同侧FTA与OA显著增大，提示可能存在“牵拉相关”癫痫病理机制。研究样本由手术确证病例组成，结果具较高临床可信度。虽然样本量有限，且缺乏纵向机制验证，但该工作在影像学定量评估和癫痫病理推断方面具有重要创新性。
