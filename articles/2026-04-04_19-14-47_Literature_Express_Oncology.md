## 1. 豆状蛋白（Legumain）通过抑制结节病中mTORC1/STAT1介导的M1型巨噬细胞极化来限制肉芽肿形成

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Legumain Restrains Granuloma Formation by Inhibiting mTORC1/STAT1-Mediated M1 Macrophage Polarization in Sarcoidosis.

> **英文摘要：**
> Sarcoidosis is a systemic granulomatous disease that has limited treatment options. Emerging evidence suggests that macrophages are essential for sarcoid granuloma initiation. Legumain (LGMN), a cysteine protease, regulates macrophage polarization in various cancers. However, its involvement in sarcoid granuloma formation remains elusive. Herein, LGMN is upregulated in macrophages within sarcoid-like granulomas. Genetic deletion of Lgmn exacerbates granulomatous inflammation in a Propionibacterium acnes (P. acnes)-induced mouse model, accompanied by increased M1 macrophage polarization. Mechanistically, LGMN binds to integrin αvβ3 on the macrophage surface and restrains M1 polarization by inhibiting the mechanistic target of rapamycin complex 1 (mTORC1)/signal transducer and activator of transcription 1 (STAT1) pathway. Furthermore, intratracheal administration of lipid nanoparticles carrying Lgmn plasmid DNA effectively alleviates granuloma formation induced by P. acnes or trehalose 6,6'-dimycolate, concomitant with decreased mTORC1/STAT1 activation and M1 polarization. These findings reveal the pivotal role of LGMN in restraining sarcoid granulomatous inflammation through suppression of mTORC1/STAT1-driven M1 macrophage polarization. Therefore, LGMN supplementation may be a promising therapeutic strategy for sarcoidosis.

## 2. 腹腔镜递送 MnO-P-ICG 补片用于肝细胞癌的光动力治疗和自然杀伤细胞驱动的免疫治疗

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Laparoscopic Delivery of a MnO-P-ICG Patch for Photodynamic Therapy and NK Cell-Driven Immunotherapy in Hepatocellular Carcinoma.

> **英文摘要：**
> Recurrence and metastasis are the primary causes of mortality in hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC), primarily driven by an immunosuppressive tumor microenvironment. This study developed a multifunctional flexible patch (MnO-P-ICG NFs) based on electrospun SiO gel fibers whose surface is modified with a layer of "nest-like" MnO. The patch enables the co-delivery of indocyanine green (ICG) and pachymaran directly to the tumor site via a laparoscopic system. Under near-infrared light irradiation, ICG serves as a photosensitizer for photodynamic therapy (PDT), effectively ablating tumors and inducing immunogenic cell death (ICD), thereby activating antitumor immunity. Meanwhile, pachymaran, with its immunomodulatory effects, preferential activates natural killer (NK) cells to exert antitumor effects. Additionally, PDT further enhances this immune response by activating the cGAS-STING pathway. In mouse models, implantation of the patch significantly inhibited primary liver tumor progression and ascites formation. In a malignant liver tumor model, MnO-P-ICG NFs prevented local recurrence in 80% of treated mice. Notably, both the depletion of NK cells and the blockade of the cGAS-STING pathway compromised the therapeutic efficacy of MnO-P-ICG NFs. Clinically, tumors with higher NK cell infiltration were associated with improved patient outcomes. In conclusion, this versatile patch provides a promising therapeutic strategy for patients with advanced HCC.

> 复发和转移是导致肝细胞癌（HCC）死亡的主要原因，这主要由免疫抑制的肿瘤微环境所驱动。本研究开发了一种基于静电纺丝二氧化硅（SiO）凝胶纤维的多功能柔性补片（MnO-P-ICG NFs），其表面修饰有一层“巢状”二氧化锰（MnO）。该补片能够通过腹腔镜系统将吲哚菁绿（ICG）和茯苓多糖（pachymaran）直接递送到肿瘤部位。在近红外光照射下，ICG 作为光动力疗法（PDT）的光敏剂，能有效消融肿瘤并诱导免疫原性细胞死亡（ICD），从而激活抗肿瘤免疫。同时，茯苓多糖凭借其免疫调节作用，优先激活自然杀伤（NK）细胞以发挥抗肿瘤效应。此外，PDT 通过激活 cGAS-STING 通路进一步增强了这种免疫反应。在小鼠模型中，植入该补片显著抑制了原发性肝肿瘤的进展和腹水的形成。在恶性肝肿瘤模型中，MnO-P-ICG NFs 防止了 80% 治疗小鼠的局部复发。值得注意的是，NK 细胞的耗竭和 cGAS-STING 通路的阻断均损害了 MnO-P-ICG NFs 的治疗疗效。临床上，具有较高 NK 细胞浸润的肿瘤与患者预后的改善相关。总之，这种多功能补片为晚期 HCC 患者提供了一种极具前景的治疗策略。

## 3. 氨解毒通过重塑肝脏微环境抑制肝转移

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41933927)
**期刊：** Advanced science (Weinheim, Baden-Wurttemberg, Germany)
**PMID：** 41933927
**DOI：** 10.1002/advs.202521098

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Ammonia Detoxification Inhibits Liver Metastasis by Reshaping Hepatic Microenvironment.

> **英文摘要：**
> Liver metastases represent an urgent unmet medical need in the care of cancer due to a lack of effective therapies. This study reveals that pathological ammonia accumulation, resulting from dysregulation of ammonia removal processes, promotes the formation of a hepatic microenvironment favorable for metastatic colonization. Metabolomic and transcriptomic analyses suggest that tumoral metabolic reprogramming due to cancer cell-intrinsic hyperactivation of the de novo pyrimidine biosynthesis pathway impairs the urea cycle and causes pathological ammonia accumulation. Utilizing tumor microenvironmental profiling and single-cell RNA sequencing, subsequent analyses further reveal that ammonia accumulation remodels the hepatic stromal-immune landscape by inducing hepatic stellate cell differentiation into metastasis-associated fibroblasts (MAFs), suppressing Ifi27l2a monocyte-macrophages, and expanding pro-metastatic low-density neutrophils (LDNs). Strikingly, the ammonia detoxifying agent L-ornithine-L-aspartate (LOLA) potently promotes ammonia removal, thereby reshaping the hepatic microenvironment with decrease of MAFs and LDNs and increase of Ifi27l2a monocyte-macrophages, and reducing metastatic burden in liver metastasis mouse models. These results suggest that ammonia, a metabolic toxin, is required for the formation of the hepatic microenvironment favoring liver metastatic colonization, and highlight LOLA as a promising therapeutic strategy for treatment of liver metastasis by detoxifying pathological ammonia accumulation.

> **中文摘要：**
> 由于缺乏有效的治疗方法，肝转移是癌症护理中一个迫切需要解决的、尚未被满足的医疗需求。本研究揭示了由于氨清除过程失调导致的病理性氨蓄积，会促进有利于转移定植的肝脏微环境的形成。代谢组学和转录组学分析表明，由于癌细胞内在的从头嘧啶生物合成途径过度激活导致的肿瘤代谢重编程，损害了尿素循环并引起病理性氨蓄积。利用肿瘤微环境谱分析和单细胞RNA测序，随后的分析进一步揭示，氨蓄积通过诱导肝星状细胞分化为转移相关成纤维细胞（MAFs）、抑制Ifi27l2a单核巨噬细胞以及增加促转移的低密度中性粒细胞（LDNs），重塑了肝脏基质-免疫格局。值得注意的是，氨解毒剂L-鸟氨酸-L-天冬氨酸（LOLA）能强效促进氨的清除，从而通过减少MAFs和LDNs、增加Ifi27l2a单核巨噬细胞来重塑肝脏微环境，并显著减轻肝转移小鼠模型的转移负荷。这些结果表明，氨作为一种代谢毒素，是形成有利于肝转移定植的肝脏微环境所必需的，并强调了LOLA作为一种通过解除病理性氨蓄积来治疗肝转移的有前景的治疗策略。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究深入探讨了氨代谢在肝转移中的病理生理意义，揭示了癌细胞通过从头嘧啶合成途径干扰尿素循环，导致局部氨蓄积并驱动肝脏微环境重塑的分子机制。研究利用单细胞测序等先进技术，精细阐明了氨如何诱导肝星状细胞活化及影响免疫细胞构成，从而构建促转移前生态位。最重要的是，研究证实了临床现有药物LOLA在抑制肝转移中的潜力，为通过代谢干预重塑肿瘤微环境提供了坚实的实验证据和潜在的临床转化方向。

## 4. 用于量化三阴性乳腺癌中BET溴结构域抑制剂耐药性及筛选再敏化药物的单细胞计算框架

**期刊：** Advanced science (Weinheim, Baden-Wurttemberg, Germany)
**PMID：** 41933924
**DOI：** 10.1002/advs.202513246

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Single-Cell Computational Frameworks for Quantifying BET Bromodomain Inhibitor Resistance and Screening Re-Sensitizer Drugs in Triple-Negative Breast Cancer.

> **英文摘要：**
> Triple-negative breast cancer (TNBC) is an aggressive subtype characterized by rapid proliferation and a great propensity for metastasis. Therapeutic options for TNBC remain limited due to the absence of targetable hormone receptors. While BET bromodomain inhibitors (BBDIs) exhibit promising anticancer potential, the emergence of drug resistance presents a major challenge. Here, through leveraging single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) data across continuous states of BBDI treatment in TNBC, this study conducts an extensive investigation into BBDI resistance, and develops two computational frameworks, FR20 and D-FR20, to quantify BBDI resistance at single-cell resolution and to screen potential BBDI re-sensitizer drugs, respectively. The accuracy and scalability of FR20 are confirmed through rigorous evaluation in nine independent datasets. In addition, cellular dynamic changes and ferroptosis inhibition are revealed in the evolution of BBDI resistance. Experimental validation demonstrates that GPX4 overexpression significantly reduces drug sensitivity in TNBC cells. Furthermore, in vitro and in vivo experiments validate the ability of the small molecule filgotinib, identified by D-FR20, to re-sensitize BBDI and effectively eliminate resistant TNBC cells. Collectively, this study provides two computational frameworks for predicting BBDI resistance and candidate re-sensitizer, as well as demonstrates the roles of ferroptosis in BBDI resistance, offering a promising avenue for TNBC treatment.

> **中文摘要：**
> 三阴性乳腺癌（TNBC）是一种具有高度侵袭性的亚型，其特点是增殖迅速且极易发生转移。由于缺乏可靶向的激素受体，TNBC 的治疗选择仍然有限。虽然 BET 溴结构域抑制剂（BBDIs）表现出显著的抗癌潜力，但耐药性的出现提出了重大挑战。在本研究中，通过利用 TNBC 在 BBDI 治疗持续状态下的单细胞 RNA 测序（scRNA-seq）数据，对 BBDI 耐药性进行了广泛研究，并开发了两个计算框架 FR20 和 D-FR20，分别用于在单细胞分辨率下量化 BBDI 耐药性以及筛选潜在的 BBDI 再敏化药物。通过在九个独立数据集中的严格评估，证实了 FR20 的准确性和可扩展性。此外，研究揭示了 BBDI 耐药性演化过程中的细胞动态变化和铁死亡抑制现象。实验验证表明，GPX4 过表达显著降低了 TNBC 细胞的药物敏感性。此外，体外和体内实验证实了由 D-FR20 识别的小分子非戈替尼（filgotinib）具有使 BBDI 重新敏化并有效清除耐药 TNBC 细胞的能力。总之，本研究提供了两个用于预测 BBDI 耐药性和候选再敏化剂的计算框架，并论证了铁死亡在 BBDI 耐药中的作用，为 TNBC 治疗提供了一条极具前景的途径。

**英文原标题：** Leptomeningeal metastatic cancer cells induce a permissive choroid plexus vasculature through extracellular-vesicle-derived 5-HIAA signaling.

> **英文摘要：**
> The choroid plexus acts as a crucial barrier in leptomeningeal metastasis (LM), yet the mechanisms through which cancer cells modulate supportive premetastasis niches remain unclear. Here, we show that remodeling of the metabolic microenvironment modifies the structure and function of choroid plexus vessels, which destroy vascular integrity to facilitate metastatic colonization. An abnormal increase in the metabolite 5-hydroxyindoleacetic acid (5-HIAA) in the cerebrospinal fluid and choroid plexus indicates a predisposition. Before initially invading the choroid plexus through cancer cells, 5-HIAA-containing extracellular vesicles specifically target vessels, resulting in vascular distortion, abnormal hemodynamics, compromised permeability and facilitation of cancer cell traversal. Mechanistically, aryl hydrocarbon receptor pathway activation by 5-HIAA is linked to choroid plexus vascular remodeling, characterized by aberrant expression of mechanoreceptors and tight junction proteins. Hence, our findings underscore the key role of 5-HIAA in supportive niche formation and highlight metabolic determinants that govern choroidal plasticity to effectively prevent metastatic colonization in LM initiation.

> 脉络丛在脑膜转移（LM）中起着关键屏障作用，但癌细胞调节支持性转移前生态位的机制尚不清楚。在此，我们展示了代谢微环境的重塑改变了脉络丛血管的结构和功能，从而破坏血管完整性以促进转移定植。脑脊液和脉络丛中代谢物 5-羟基吲哚乙酸（5-HIAA）的异常升高预示着转移倾向。在癌细胞最初入侵脉络丛之前，含有 5-HIAA 的细胞外囊泡特异性靶向血管，导致血管扭曲、血流动力学异常、通透性受损并促进癌细胞穿越。机制上，5-HIAA 激活芳香烃受体通路与脉络丛血管重塑相关，其特征是机械受体和紧密连接蛋白的异常表达。因此，我们的研究结果强调了 5-HIAA 在支持性生态位形成中的关键作用，并突出了决定脉络丛可塑性的代谢因素，有助于有效预防脑膜转移启动阶段的转移定植。
