## 1. 突触结合蛋白 1 和突触结合蛋白 7 促进 MR1 介导的抗原呈递

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Synaptotagmin 1 and Synaptotagmin 7 promote MR1-mediated presentation of antigens.

> **英文摘要：**
> (Mtb) is an intracellular pathogen that can be sensed by T cells, which are essential for the control of infection. In comparison to viral infections, Mtb antigens are relatively limited and hence, challenging to sample. Specialized antigen presentation pathways enable the presentation of such scarce antigens to CD8 T cells, which are, thus, uniquely poised to survey intracellular environments. A subset of CD8 T cells prevalent in the airways, known as mucosal-associated invariant T (MAIT) cells, can be activated through the presentation of Mtb antigens via the major histocompatibility complex class I-related protein 1 (MR1) molecule. Prior work demonstrates that endosomal calcium signaling is critical for MR1-mediated presentation of Mtb-derived antigens. Here, we show that the calcium-sensing trafficking proteins Synaptotagmin (Syt) 1 and Syt7 specifically promote MAIT cell activation in response to Mtb-infected cells. In bronchial epithelial cells, Syt1 and Syt7 localize to late endo-lysosomes and MR1 vesicles. Loss of Syt1 and Syt7 results in enlarged MR1 vesicles and an increased number of MR1 vesicles in close proximity to Mtb-containing vacuoles during infection. This study identifies a specialized pathway in which Syt1 and Syt7 facilitate the translocation of MR1 from Mtb-containing vacuoles, potentially to the cell surface for antigen presentation.

## 2. 2025年德尔菲专家小组关于重症监护中脑微透析的共识声明

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41975464)
**期刊：** Critical care (London, England)
**PMID：** 41975464
**DOI：** 10.1186/s13054-026-05993-z

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Consensus statement from the 2025 Delphi panel on cerebral microdialysis in critical care.

> **英文摘要：**
> PURPOSE: Secondary brain injury is a common cause of poor outcome after trauma, subarachnoid hemorrhage, and intracerebral hemorrhage, and optimizing treatment requires real-time insight into cerebral metabolism. Cerebral microdialysis (CMD) uniquely provides key related information, yet consensus on its use has not been updated since publication of the consensus statement from the 2014 International Microdialysis Forum. We aimed to assess expert consensus on the use of CMD in critical care and provide contemporary guidance to standardize practice and advance clinical implementation.
> 
> METHODS: We conducted a 3-round modified Delphi study with international experts in CMD and neurocritical care. Consensus was defined as ≥ 75% agreement among non-abstaining respondents, with a minimum of 30 non-abstaining respondents required per statement. Statements not reaching consensus were iteratively revised based on panelist feedback.
> 
> RESULTS: Forty of 67 invited experts (60%) from 9 countries participated. Sixty of 62 individual items achieved consensus (97%) across 9 domains: indications and patient selection, technical and procedural considerations, detecting deterioration and secondary injury, metabolic interpretation, treatment algorithms, glucose management, sampling frequency, core reporting items, and barriers to clinical implementation.
> 
> CONCLUSION: This consensus statement provides updated, evidence-informed recommendations for the use of CMD in critical care. The panel reaffirmed many core recommendations from the 2014 consensus while making targeted advances: cautious extension of guidance to intracerebral hemorrhage, comprehensive reporting guidance addressing frequently omitted elements (19 items vs. 6 in 2014), and identification of 10 key barriers to routine clinical adoption.

> **中文摘要：**
> 【目的】继发性脑损伤是创伤、蛛网膜下腔出血和脑出血后预后不良的常见原因，而优化治疗需要实时了解脑代谢情况。脑微透析（CMD）能独特地提供关键的相关信息，但自2014年国际微透析论坛发布共识声明以来，关于其使用的共识尚未更新。本研究旨在评估专家对重症监护中使用CMD的共识，并提供当代的指导建议，以标准化操作实践并推进临床实施。
> 【方法】我们对脑微透析和神经重症监护领域的国际专家进行了一项为期三轮的改良德尔菲研究。共识被定义为非弃权受访者中≥75%的一致性，且每项声明至少需要30名非弃权受访者参与。未达成共识的声明根据专家小组成员的反馈进行了迭代修改。
> 【结果】来自9个国家的67位受邀专家中有40位（60%）参与了研究。在9个领域共62个单项中，有60个项目（97%）达成了共识，这些领域包括：适应症和患者选择、技术和操作考虑、检测恶化和继发性损伤、代谢解释、治疗方案、血糖管理、采样频率、核心报告项目以及临床实施的障碍。
> 【结论】本共识声明为重症监护中使用CMD提供了更新的、基于证据的建议。专家小组重申了2014年共识中的许多核心建议，同时取得了针对性的进展：将指导范围谨慎地扩展到脑出血领域，提供了涵盖经常遗漏元素的综合报告指导（由2014年的6项增加至19项），并确定了常规临床应用的10个关键障碍。

## 3. 微血管内皮B类I型清道夫受体通过抑制T细胞心脏趋向性来防止射血分数保留型心力衰竭。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41975084)
**期刊：** EMBO molecular medicine
**PMID：** 41975084
**DOI：** 10.1038/s44321-026-00405-9

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Microvascular endothelial scavenger receptor class B type I protects against heart failure with preserved ejection fraction by inhibiting T-cell cardiotropism.

> **英文摘要：**
> Cardiac microvascular endothelial cells (CMECs) dysfunction is a well-recognized mediator of heart failure with preserved ejection fraction (HFpEF), but the underlying mechanism remains unclear. Here we find that scavenger receptor class B type I (SR-B1) is predominantly expressed in CMECs and decreased significantly in HFpEF. Endothelial-specific SR-B1 deficiency exacerbates cardiac pathological remodeling and diastolic dysfunction in HFpEF, which can be prevented by endothelial SR-B1 reconstitution through adeno-associated virus serotype 1 (AAV1)-mediated delivery in endothelial-specific SR-B1-deficient mice. Single-cardiac-endothelial-cell transcriptomics and lineage-tracing system reveal that inflammatory CMECs subcluster activation is responsible for the deteriorating HFpEF progression induced by endothelial SR-B1 loss, rather than endothelial-to-mesenchymal transition. Mechanistically, SR-B1 loss drives increased CXCL10 secretion, which orchestrates CMECs activation and CXCR3-positive T-cell cardiotropism to promote diastolic dysfunction-a process associated with endothelial IRF1 activation. Most importantly, the SR-B1-CXCL10-CXCR3 axis is activated in human HFpEF cardiac tissue, and the elevated CXCL10 level in plasma is independently associated with a higher HFpEF prevalence. This study uncovers that activation of the SR-B1-CXCL10-CXCR3 axis in CMECs aggravates HFpEF pathogenesis through the accumulation of CXCR3-positive T-cells in hearts.

> **中文摘要：**
> 心脏微血管内皮细胞（CMECs）功能障碍是射血分数保留型心力衰竭（HFpEF）公认的介导因素，但其潜在机制尚不清楚。在此，我们发现B类I型清道夫受体（SR-B1）主要在CMECs中表达，并在HFpEF中显著下降。在内皮特异性SR-B1缺陷小鼠中，内皮特异性的SR-B1缺失会加剧HFpEF中的心脏病理重塑和舒张功能障碍，而通过腺相关病毒血清1型（AAV1）介导的递送重建内皮SR-B1可以防止这种情况。单心脏内皮细胞转录组学和谱系示踪系统揭示，炎症性CMECs亚群的活化是导致内皮SR-B1缺失所诱导的HFpEF进展恶化的原因，而非内皮向间质转化。从机制上讲，SR-B1的缺失驱动CXCL10分泌增加，后者协同调控CMECs活化和CXCR3阳性T细胞的心脏趋向性，从而促进舒张功能障碍——这一过程与内皮IRF1活化相关。最重要的是，SR-B1-CXCL10-CXCR3轴在人类HFpEF心脏组织中被激活，并且血浆中CXCL10水平的升高与更高的HFpEF患病率独立相关。本研究揭示了CMECs中SR-B1-CXCL10-CXCR3轴的激活通过心脏中CXCR3阳性T细胞的积聚加剧了HFpEF的发病机制。

## 4. 妊娠早期感染SARS-CoV-2导致母胎界面发生严重的免疫失调，尽管在胎盘组织中检测到的病毒有限。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41974725)
**期刊：** Nature communications
**PMID：** 41974725
**DOI：** 10.1038/s41467-026-71770-9

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** SARS-CoV-2 infection during the first trimester leads to profound immune dysregulation at the maternal-fetal interface despite limited virus detection in placental tissues.

> **英文摘要：**
> The endemic nature of SARS-CoV-2 underscores the imperative to elucidate its effects on pregnancy, particularly the potential for vertical transmission and its influence on the early maternal-fetal interface. Here, we performed an extensive cohort study involving 761 pregnant women who voluntarily terminated their pregnancies during the first trimester. Analyses conducted using RT‒qPCR, fluorescence in situ hybridisation, and indirect immunofluorescence showed low detection levels of SARS-CoV-2 infection within villous and decidual tissues. Single-cell RNA sequencing analysis revealed an absence of cell populations demonstrating significant coexpression of ACE2 and TMPRSS2, potentially providing a mechanistic explanation for the rare incidence of viral infection within placental tissues. The maternal systemic inflammatory response was activated, and the levels of IL-31, IL-5, and GRO-α were significantly elevated during acute infection. Furthermore, increased IgG titres were negatively correlated with TNF-β concentrations, suggesting a protective immunomodulatory function of IgG antibodies. Conversely, both bulk and single-cell transcriptomic analyses revealed pronounced, cell type-specific antiviral and immune responses within the placental microenvironment. A pervasive interferon-stimulated gene signature was identified, accompanied by the emergence of M2-like macrophages with diminished antigen presentation capacity during convalescence. Importantly, maternal SARS-CoV-2 infection disrupted intercellular communication networks, notably impairing the activity of the WNT and TGF-β signalling pathways in trophoblasts, thereby altering their differentiation trajectories. In summary, within this large cohort of first-trimester samples, we identified infrequent instances of SARS-CoV-2 infection in both villous and decidual tissues. Nonetheless, infection substantially perturbed the placental immune milieu and trophoblast dynamics, which may adversely affect pregnancy outcomes.

> **中文摘要：**
> SARS-CoV-2的流行特性强调了阐明其对妊娠影响的必要性，特别是垂直传播的可能性及其对早期母胎界面的影响。在此，我们进行了一项涉及761名在妊娠早期自愿终止妊娠的孕妇的大规模队列研究。通过RT-qPCR、荧光原位杂交和间接免疫荧光进行的分析显示，在绒毛和蜕膜组织中检测到的SARS-CoV-2感染水平较低。单细胞RNA测序分析显示，缺乏表现出ACE2和TMPRSS2显著共表达的细胞群体，这可能为胎盘组织内罕见的病毒感染提供了机制解释。母体全身炎症反应被激活，在急性感染期间IL-31、IL-5和GRO-α的水平显著升高。此外，增加的IgG滴度与TNF-β浓度呈负相关，提示IgG抗体具有保护性的免疫调节功能。相反，批量和单细胞转录组分析均显示，在胎盘微环境中存在明显的、细胞类型特异性的抗病毒和免疫反应。研究鉴定出一种普遍的干扰素刺激基因特征，并伴随康复期抗原递呈能力减弱的M2样巨噬细胞的出现。重要的是，母体SARS-CoV-2感染扰乱了细胞间通讯网络，特别是损害了滋养细胞中WNT和TGF-β信号通路的活性，从而改变了它们的分化轨迹。总之，在这一大规模妊娠早期样本队列中，我们在绒毛和蜕膜组织中仅发现了罕见的SARS-CoV-2感染实例。尽管如此，感染仍显著干扰了胎盘免疫环境和滋养细胞动力学，这可能会对妊娠结局产生不利影响。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究通过对761例妊娠早期样本的大规模队列研究，深入探讨了SARS-CoV-2对母胎界面的间接影响。研究发现虽然胎盘直接感染率极低（与ACE2/TMPRSS2共表达缺失有关），但母体感染会引发显著的全身炎症及胎盘局部的免疫重构，包括干扰素特征基因的广泛激活和滋养细胞分化通路的受损。这项工作的重要价值在于揭示了病毒如何通过改变免疫微环境而非直接感染来干扰早期妊娠，为理解新冠病毒相关的潜在妊娠风险提供了严谨的分子生物学证据。

## 5. 宿主-微生物组原型区分人类下呼吸道的感染与病原体携带状态。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41974724)
**期刊：** Nature communications
**PMID：** 41974724
**DOI：** 10.1038/s41467-026-71863-5

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Host-microbiome archetypes differentiate infection from pathogen carriage in the human lower airway.

> **英文摘要：**
> Distinguishing lower respiratory tract infection (LRTI) from incidental pathogen carriage (IPC) is clinically challenging. The immunologic and microbial factors defining the states of LRTI and IPC are poorly understood. Here, we perform host-microbe metatranscriptomic profiling of tracheal aspirates from 326 mechanically ventilated children with clinically adjudicated LRTI (n = 207), IPC (n = 70), or non-infectious respiratory failure (n = 49). In the airway microbiome, LRTI shows reduced alpha diversity and taxonomic richness, while IPC displays greater bacterial abundance, enrichment in respiratory anaerobes, and increased metabolic activity. At the host level, patients with LRTI exhibit a distinct lower airway transcriptional signature of innate and adaptive immune activation compared to those with IPC, who have similar transcriptional profiles to uninfected controls. Mediation analyses suggest the airway microbiome influences the host response to pathogens. An integrated host-microbe metatranscriptomic classifier accurately discriminates LRTI from IPC and controls (AUC = 0.89, 95% confidence interval (CI) 0.85-0.92). The single gene FABP4, encoding a macrophage-associated lipid chaperone and recently described pneumonia biomarker, performs similarly when combined with alpha diversity; FABP4 protein alone achieves an AUC = 0.88 (95% CI 0.82-0.93). Together, our findings reveal distinct ecological and immunologic archetypes defining LRTI and IPC, and support data-driven, biology-informed LRTI diagnostics incorporating host and microbial features.

> **中文摘要：**
> 在临床上，区分下呼吸道感染(LRTI)与偶然性病原体携带(IPC)极具挑战性。定义LRTI和IPC状态的免疫学及微生物学因素尚不明确。本研究中，我们对326名机械通气儿童的气管抽吸物进行了宿主-微生物宏转录组学分析，这些患儿经临床裁定为LRTI (n=207)、IPC (n=70)或非感染性呼吸衰竭(n=49)。在气道微生物组方面，LRTI表现为α多样性和分类丰富度降低，而IPC则显示出更高的细菌丰度、呼吸道厌氧菌富集以及更强的代谢活性。在宿主层面，与IPC患者相比，LRTI患者表现出独特的下呼吸道先天性与适应性免疫激活转录特征，而IPC患者的转录谱则与未感染的对照组相似。中介分析表明，气道微生物组影响宿主对病原体的反应。一个整合的宿主-微生物宏转录组学分类器能准确区分LRTI、IPC及对照组(AUC = 0.89, 95%置信区间(CI) 0.85-0.92)。编码巨噬细胞相关脂质伴侣蛋白且近期被报道为肺炎生物标志物的单基因FABP4，在与α多样性结合时表现相似；单独使用FABP4蛋白即可达到AUC = 0.88 (95% CI 0.82-0.93)。总之，我们的研究揭示了定义LRTI和IPC的独特生态学和免疫学原型，并为结合宿主与微生物特征的、数据驱动的、基于生物学的LRTI诊断方法提供了支持。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究旨在解决临床上难以区分下呼吸道感染与病原体携带的难题。通过对机械通气儿童进行宿主-微生物宏转录组学分析，研究揭示了两种状态在气道微生物组生态和宿主免疫转录方面的根本差异。其核心发现是，下呼吸道感染不仅是病原体的存在，更是宿主免疫激活与微生物组失调的综合结果，并提出整合分类器及FABP4蛋白可作为有效的鉴别诊断工具。这项工作为开发更精准的、基于生物学机制的下呼吸道感染诊断策略奠定了坚实基础。
