## 1. IL-9和Blimp-1在过敏性哮喘中保护2型固有淋巴细胞的转录特性

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42032301)
**期刊：** Nature immunology
**PMID：** 42032301
**DOI：** 10.1038/s41590-026-02509-3

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** IL-9 and Blimp-1 protect the transcriptional identity of group 2 innate lymphocytes in allergic asthma.

> **英文摘要：**
> Allergic asthma is driven by type 2 immune responses, including type 2 innate lymphoid cells (ILC2s). Although ILC2s are activated by the tissue alarmins interleukin (IL)-33 and IL-25, these signals do not intrinsically enforce type 2 identity and the mechanisms that maintain type 2 cytokine expression remain unclear. Here we show that allergen-induced IL-33 and IL-25 rapidly induce IL-9, which in turn upregulates the transcriptional repressor Blimp-1 in ILC2s. Blimp-1 sustains type 2 immunity by directly repressing type 1 inflammatory programs, including expression of interferon-γ and tumor necrosis factor. Deletion of Blimp-1 in ILC2s increased type 1 cytokine production and reduced IL-5 and IL-13 expression, eosinophil recruitment and mucus production in the lung. In contrast, IL-9 expression was enhanced in the absence of Blimp-1, leading to increased mast cell recruitment. Together, these findings identify Blimp-1 as a key regulator of ILC2 transcriptional fidelity that stabilizes type 2 inflammation while constraining divergent inflammatory programs during allergic responses.

> **中文摘要：**
> 过敏性哮喘由2型免疫反应驱动，包括2型固有淋巴细胞（ILC2s）。尽管ILC2s被组织警报素白细胞介素(IL)-33和IL-25激活，但这些信号本身并不强制执行2型特性，且维持2型细胞因子表达的机制仍不清楚。本研究表明，变应原诱导的IL-33和IL-25可迅速诱导IL-9，进而上调ILC2s中的转录阻遏因子Blimp-1。Blimp-1通过直接抑制1型炎症程序（包括干扰素-γ和肿瘤坏死因子的表达）来维持2型免疫。ILC2s中Blimp-1的缺失增加了1型细胞因子的产生，并减少了肺部IL-5和IL-13的表达、嗜酸性粒细胞的招募及粘液的产生。相比之下，在缺乏Blimp-1的情况下，IL-9的表达增强，导致肥大细胞招募增加。总之，这些发现表明Blimp-1是ILC2转录保真度的关键调节因子，它在过敏反应期间稳定2型炎症，同时限制差异性炎症程序。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究深入探讨了过敏性哮喘中ILC2s维持其转录特性的分子机制。研究通过揭示“IL-33/IL-25—IL-9—Blimp-1”这一新型轴线，阐明了转录阻遏因子Blimp-1如何作为核心开关，通过抑制1型炎症程序来保卫2型免疫应答的稳定性与独特性。这一发现不仅在理论上完善了固有淋巴细胞功能的调控网络，也为过敏性气道疾病的精准转录干预提供了潜在靶点。

## 2. 卷积神经网络以诊断级准确性量化结核分枝杆菌的抗生素耐药性并预测治疗反应。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42031767)
**期刊：** Nature communications
**PMID：** 42031767
**DOI：** 10.1038/s41467-026-72225-x

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Convolutional neural networks quantify antibiotic resistance in Mycobacterium tuberculosis with diagnostic grade accuracy and predict treatment response.

> **英文摘要：**
> There is considerable interest in training machine learning (ML) models on genomic data that achieve clinical grade diagnostic accuracy. Many successful ML models have been trained and validated on binary tasks because predicting biomedically relevant continuous variables is difficult to optimize. In this work, we present convolutional neural networks (CNNs) that predict minimum inhibitory concentrations (MICs) for eight antibiotics from Mycobacterium tuberculosis complex (MTBC) gene sequences. By including evolutionary information, protein biochemical properties, and data augmentation for rare variants, we build models that predict 89% of MICs within one drug concentration doubling. Although trained on ≤ 52% of the World Health Organization's (WHO) MTBC drug resistance mutation catalog data, the CNNs accurately predict the effects of 97% of the catalog's graded mutations. In a cohort of 373 patients with rifampicin-susceptible M. tuberculosis infections, higher CNN-predicted rifampicin MICs are associated with unfavorable treatment outcomes, providing additional evidence that subtle differences in MIC below the resistance threshold are clinically relevant. These results demonstrate the value of encoding multiple dimensions of biological data in machine learning of M. tuberculosis drug resistance phenotypes and that domain knowledge-inspired machine learning models can be both interpretable and reach clinical grade accuracy.

> **中文摘要：**
> 将机器学习（ML）模型应用于基因组数据以达到临床级诊断准确性，引起了广泛关注。许多成功的机器学习模型已在二元任务上得到训练和验证，因为预测生物医学相关的连续变量难以优化。在这项工作中，我们提出了卷积神经网络（CNNs），该网络能从结核分枝杆菌复合体（MTBC）的基因序列中预测八种抗生素的最低抑菌浓度（MICs）。通过整合进化信息、蛋白质生化特性以及对罕见变异的数据增强，我们构建的模型能够预测89%的MIC值，其误差在一个药物浓度倍增范围内。尽管训练数据仅占世界卫生组织（WHO）MTBC耐药突变目录数据的≤52%，但该CNN模型能准确预测目录中97%的分级突变效应。在一个包含373名利福平敏感结核分枝杆菌感染患者的队列中，CNN预测的较高利福平MIC值与不良治疗结局相关，这为低于耐药阈值的MIC微小差异具有临床相关性提供了额外证据。这些结果证明了在结核分枝杆菌耐药表型的机器学习中，编码多维度生物学数据的价值，并表明受领域知识启发的机器学习模型既能保证可解释性，又能达到临床级的准确性。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究的核心目标是开发一种卷积神经网络（CNN）模型，利用结核分枝杆菌的基因序列数据，精确预测其对多种抗生素的最低抑菌浓度（MIC）。研究团队通过整合进化信息与蛋白质生化特性，并对罕见变异进行数据增强，显著提升了模型的预测性能。关键发现表明，该模型不仅能以高精度量化耐药水平，还能揭示低于传统耐药阈值的MIC微小差异对患者治疗结局的临床意义。这项工作的创新性在于，它成功地将领域知识与深度学习相结合，解决了预测连续型表型（MIC）的难题，使模型兼具临床级准确度与生物学可解释性。

## 3. 保守非编码序列-9通过调控NFATc1介导的B细胞IL-10表达来控制炎症反应。

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42030383)
**期刊：** Science advances
**PMID：** 42030383
**DOI：** 10.1126/sciadv.aec7779

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Conserved noncoding sequence-9 regulates NFATc1-mediated IL-10 expression in B cells to control inflammatory responses.

> **英文摘要：**
> Interleukin-10 (IL-10) production by B cells plays a critical role in regulating inflammatory responses, yet the mechanisms controlling its expression remain poorly understood. We identified a conserved noncoding sequence (CNS-9) as an essential regulatory element for IL-10 expression in mouse B cells. Comprehensive genomic analyses revealed that CNS-9 functions as an enhancer bound by the transcription factor NFATc1, which facilitates chromatin looping between CNS-9 and the IL-10 promoter to drive transcription. Flow cytometry analyses identified B1a cells as the predominant source of B cell-derived IL-10, with this production critically dependent on NFATc1-mediated CNS-9 regulation. In a mouse model of LPS-induced sepsis, deletion of CNS-9, B cell-specific NFATc1, or both resulted in reduced IL-10 production, exacerbated inflammatory responses, and decreased survival. Furthermore, we demonstrated that the human homolog, CNS-12, functions similarly through NFATc1-dependent mechanisms. These findings establish a conserved regulatory pathway controlling IL-10 expression in B cells with notable implications for inflammatory disease pathogenesis and potential therapeutic interventions.

> **中文摘要：**
> B细胞产生的白细胞介素-10（IL-10）在调节炎症反应中起着关键作用，但其表达的调控机制仍不完全清楚。我们鉴定出一个保守非编码序列（CNS-9），它是小鼠B细胞中IL-10表达所必需的关键调控元件。全面的基因组学分析显示，CNS-9作为一个增强子，与转录因子NFATc1结合，促进CNS-9与IL-10启动子之间的染色质环化，从而驱动转录。流式细胞术分析确定了B1a细胞是B细胞源性IL-10的主要来源，且其产生严重依赖于NFATc1介导的CNS-9调控。在LPS诱导的脓毒症小鼠模型中，敲除CNS-9、B细胞特异性的NFATc1，或两者同时敲除，均导致IL-10产生减少、炎症反应加剧及存活率下降。此外，我们证明了其人类同源序列CNS-12通过NFATc1依赖的机制发挥类似功能。这些发现揭示了一条调控B细胞IL-10表达的保守调控通路，对炎症性疾病的发病机制和潜在的治疗干预具有重要意义。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究精准揭示了B细胞中抗炎因子IL-10表达的关键调控机制。研究团队创新性地鉴定出一段保守非编码序列CNS-9作为核心增强子，并阐明了其通过与转录因子NFATc1结合、促进染色质环化来启动IL-10转录的分子通路。通过在LPS诱导的脓毒症小鼠模型中进行验证，该研究有力地证实了此通路在控制体内炎症反应中的关键生理功能。这一发现不仅深化了对B细胞免疫调节功能的理解，也为炎症性疾病的治疗提供了新的潜在靶点。

## 4. 平衡液体与0.9%生理盐水在儿童感染性休克治疗中的应用比较

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42028918)
**期刊：** The New England journal of medicine
**PMID：** 42028918
**DOI：** 10.1056/NEJMoa2601969

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Balanced Fluid or 0.9% Saline in Children Treated for Septic Shock.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Whether treatment with balanced crystalloid fluid leads to better outcomes than 0.9% saline in children treated for septic shock is debated.
> 
> METHODS: In this pragmatic clinical trial conducted at 47 emergency departments in five countries, patients (2 months to <18 years of age) with suspected septic shock and abnormal perfusion were randomly assigned to receive fluid resuscitation with either balanced fluid or 0.9% saline for up to 48 hours. The primary outcome was a major adverse kidney event (a composite of death, new renal-replacement therapy, or persistent kidney dysfunction) at 30 days after enrollment or hospital discharge, whichever occurred first.
> 
> RESULTS: Of 9041 enrolled patients, 277 (6.1%) in the balanced-fluid group and 282 (6.2%) in the 0.9%-saline group withdrew from the trial, leaving 4235 and 4247 patients, respectively, for analysis. A primary-outcome event occurred in 137 patients (3.4%) in the balanced-fluid group and in 124 (3.0%) in the 0.9%-saline group (difference, 0.4 percentage points; 95% confidence interval [CI], -0.5 to 1.3; risk ratio, 1.10; 95% CI, 0.88 to 1.40; P = 0.85). The median number of hospital-free days during 28 days after enrollment was 23 (interquartile range, 19 to 25) in both groups. Hyperchloremia occurred in 868 patients (31.4%) in the balanced-fluid group and in 1383 (49.0%) in the 0.9%-saline group; hypernatremia in 52 (1.8%) and 89 (3.1%), respectively; and hyperlactatemia in 260 (19.8%) and 228 (16.7%). No differences in other safety outcomes or adverse events were seen.
> 
> CONCLUSIONS: Among children treated for septic shock, no significant difference was seen in the incidence of death, new renal-replacement therapy, or persistent kidney dysfunction when fluid resuscitation was administered with balanced fluid as compared with 0.9% saline. (Funded by Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development and others; PRoMPT BOLUS ClinicalTrials.gov number, NCT04102371.).

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：对于接受感染性休克治疗的儿童，使用平衡晶体液是否比使用0.9%生理盐水能带来更好的结局，目前尚存争议。
> 
> 方法：这项实用性临床试验在五个国家的47个急诊科进行，将疑似感染性休克且灌注异常的患者（年龄2个月至<18岁）随机分组，分别接受平衡液体或0.9%生理盐水进行液体复苏，持续时间最长48小时。主要结局是入组后30天或出院时（以先发生者为准）发生的主要肾脏不良事件（死亡、新的肾脏替代治疗或持续性肾功能障碍的复合结局）。
> 
> 结果：在9041名入组患者中，平衡液体组有277名（6.1%）退出试验，0.9%生理盐水组有282名（6.2%）退出试验，最终分别有4235名和4247名患者纳入分析。平衡液体组有137名患者（3.4%）发生主要结局事件，0.9%生理盐水组有124名（3.0%）（差异为0.4个百分点；95%置信区间[CI]，-0.5至1.3；风险比，1.10；95% CI，0.88至1.40；P = 0.85）。两组在入组后28天内的无住院天数中位数均为23天（四分位距，19至25）。平衡液体组有868名患者（31.4%）出现高氯血症，0.9%生理盐水组有1383名（49.0%）；高钠血症分别为52名（1.8%）和89名（3.1%）；高乳酸血症分别为260名（19.8%）和228名（16.7%）。在其他安全性结局或不良事件方面未见差异。
> 
> 结论：在接受感染性休克治疗的儿童中，与使用0.9%生理盐水进行液体复苏相比，使用平衡液体在死亡、新的肾脏替代治疗或持续性肾功能障碍的发生率方面未见显著差异。（由尤尼斯·肯尼迪·施莱弗国家儿童健康与人类发展研究所等资助；PRoMPT BOLUS ClinicalTrials.gov 编号，NCT04102371。）

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究是一项大规模、多国参与的实用性随机临床试验，旨在解决儿科感染性休克治疗中平衡液与生理盐水选择的关键争议。研究方法严谨，通过比较主要肾脏不良事件，直接回应了临床关切。其核心发现是，两种液体在改善主要临床结局方面无显著差异，这一结论为临床实践提供了高级别证据，挑战了平衡液在肾脏保护方面更优的传统观点。尽管未显示主要终点的优越性，但研究揭示了两种液体在电解质紊乱方面的不同影响，为临床决策提供了更全面的考量。这项研究的局限性在于其“实用性”设计可能引入无法完全控制的混杂变量。
