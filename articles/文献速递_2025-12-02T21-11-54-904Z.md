## 2. 产前暴露于寨卡病毒与幼儿期癫痫相关住院风险

**原文链接：** [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41324929](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41324929)
**期刊：** JAMA pediatrics
**PMID：** 41324929
**DOI：** 10.1001/jamapediatrics.2025.4935

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Prenatal Exposure to Zika Virus and Risk of Epilepsy-Related Hospitalization During Early Childhood.

**英文摘要原文：**
IMPORTANCE: Epilepsy is a major clinical concern in children with congenital Zika syndrome (CZS). However, whether in utero exposure to Zika virus (ZIKV) without development of CZS is associated with increased epilepsy risk compared with unexposed children remains unclear.

OBJECTIVE: To compare the risk of epilepsy-related hospitalizations during the first 4 years of life among children exposed to ZIKV during pregnancy (with and without CZS) vs an unexposed group.

DESIGN, SETTING, AND PARTICIPANTS: This was a population-based cohort study conducted in Brazil among live-born singleton children with 22 or more weeks' gestation born from January 2015 to November 2018. Data analysis was conducted from December 2024 to March 2025.

EXPOSURE: In utero exposure to ZIKV by maternal notification during pregnancy.

MAIN OUTCOMES AND MEASURES: The primary outcome was the time from birth to the first epilepsy-related hospitalization. Hazard ratios (HRs) and 95% CIs were estimated using a cause-specific Cox regression model, adjusting for maternal education level, maternal self-reported race and ethnicity, maternal age, year of child birth, and adequacy of the number of antenatal appointments. All-cause deaths were also considered as competing events.

RESULTS: Among 10 828 887 children (5 275 628 [48.7%] female; mean [SD] gestational age, 38.5 [2.0] weeks), 2780 (0.03%) had CZS and 8361 (0.08%) were exposed to ZIKV during pregnancy without developing CZS. After adjusting for confounders, CZS was associated with an increased risk of epilepsy-related hospital admission (adjusted HR [aHR], 34.22 [95% CI, 29.16-40.16]). Age-specific aHRs peaked at age 7 to 18 months (aHR [95% CI], 33.72 [24.70-46.04] for 0-6 months, 44.58 [35.89-55.36] for 7-18 months, and 20.62 [14.31-29.72] for 19-48 months). Children with CZS who were microcephalic, normocephalic, or macrocephalic showed similar associations with epilepsy-related admissions. Children exposed to ZIKV without CZS did not show increased risk compared with unexposed peers (aHR, 0.66 [95% CI, 0.34-1.27]).

CONCLUSIONS AND RELEVANCE: In this population-based cohort study, CZS was associated with elevated risk of epilepsy-related hospitalization in early childhood in Brazil. In contrast, children exposed to ZIKV during pregnancy without CZS did not appear to have higher risk of epilepsy-related admission compared with unexposed children.

**中文摘要译文：**
重要性：癫痫是先天性寨卡综合征（CZS）儿童的主要临床关注点。然而，与未暴露儿童相比，子宫内暴露于寨卡病毒（ZIKV）但未发展为CZS是否与癫痫风险增加相关仍不清楚。

目的：比较妊娠期间暴露于ZIKV的儿童（伴有和不伴有CZS）与未暴露组在生命前4年内癫痫相关住院的风险。

设计、设置和参与者：这是一项基于人群的队列研究，在巴西进行，研究对象为2015年1月至2018年11月期间出生的孕周≥22周的单胎活产儿童。数据分析于2024年12月至2025年3月进行。

暴露：通过妊娠期间母亲报告确认的子宫内ZIKV暴露。

主要结局和指标：主要结局是从出生到首次癫痫相关住院的时间。使用特定原因Cox回归模型估计风险比（HRs）和95%置信区间（CIs），调整了母亲教育水平、母亲自我报告的种族和民族、母亲年龄、儿童出生年份以及产前就诊次数的充分性。全因死亡也被视为竞争事件。

结果：在10,828,887名儿童中（5,275,628名[48.7%]女性；平均[SD]孕周38.5[2.0]周），2780名（0.03%）患有CZS，8361名（0.08%）在妊娠期间暴露于ZIKV但未发展为CZS。调整混杂因素后，CZS与癫痫相关住院风险增加相关（调整后HR[aHR]，34.22[95% CI，29.16-40.16]）。年龄特异性aHR在7至18个月时达到峰值（aHR[95% CI]，0-6个月为33.72[24.70-46.04]，7-18个月为44.58[35.89-55.36]，19-48个月为20.62[14.31-29.72]）。患有CZS的小头畸形、正常头围或大头畸形儿童与癫痫相关住院的关联相似。暴露于ZIKV但未发展为CZS的儿童与未暴露同龄人相比未显示风险增加（aHR，0.66[95% CI，0.34-1.27]）。

结论和相关性：在这项基于人群的队列研究中，CZS与巴西幼儿期癫痫相关住院风险升高相关。相比之下，妊娠期间暴露于ZIKV但未发展为CZS的儿童与未暴露儿童相比似乎没有更高的癫痫相关住院风险。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过大规模人群队列设计，首次系统评估了产前寨卡病毒暴露与幼儿癫痫住院风险的关联。研究创新性地区分了先天性寨卡综合征（CZS）患者与仅暴露未发病儿童，发现CZS患儿癫痫住院风险显著增高34倍，且风险在7-18月龄达到峰值，而单纯暴露未发病儿童风险未见增加。这一发现具有重要临床价值，为寨卡病毒暴露儿童的癫痫风险分层管理提供了循证依据，同时缓解了仅暴露未发病家庭的焦虑。研究局限性在于依赖住院数据可能低估门诊癫痫病例，且未评估癫痫亚型与严重程度。
