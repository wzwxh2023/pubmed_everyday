## 1. 立体定向MRI引导下激光消融治疗结节性硬化症伴难治性癫痫儿童的临床结局

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41367165)
**期刊：** Epilepsia
**PMID：** 41367165
**DOI：** 10.1111/epi.70036

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Clinical outcomes following stereotactic MRI-guided laser ablation in children with tuberous sclerosis complex and intractable epilepsy.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVE: In children with tuberous sclerosis complex (TSC) and drug-resistant epilepsy (DRE), magnetic resonance imaging-guided stereotactic laser ablation (SLA) therapy offers less-invasive treatment compared to craniotomy and resection. Our study seeks to further expand on the long-term outcomes in patients with TSC-related DRE who have undergone SLA.
> 
> METHODS: Patients with TSC and DRE treated with SLA, between July 1, 2016 and January 1, 2022, at our institution were identified retrospectively. Reduction in number of anti-seizure medications (ASMs), developmental improvement as reported by the patients' families, seizure frequency measured in surgically targeted seizure type and total seizures per day, and Engel classification were evaluated at 6 months, 1 year, 2 years, and thereafter at the most recent follow-up visit if available.
> 
> RESULTS: Forty patients (ages 11 months to 23 years, median age 3.6 years) with TSC underwent SLA. Total follow-up was on average 2.9 years post-surgery. Significant (>50%) reduction in total seizure frequency occurred in 80% of patients (n = 32), with 63% of patients reporting a >90% reduction in seizure frequency (n = 25) at last follow-up. Fifty-eight percent had complete freedom from their surgically targeted seizure type (n = 23). At last follow up, perceived developmental gains occurred in 63% (n = 25), reduction in ASM occurred in 40% (n = 16), and Engel class was III or less in 93% of patients (n = 37).
> 
> SIGNIFICANCE: SLA was safe and effective for most patients, even the very young. A majority of patients achieved improvement in seizure frequency, including the surgically targeted seizure type, but less than half were able to reduce medications. Reported developmental improvement was related to Engel outcomes. Future studies utilizing neuropsychological testing in the full cohort will be required to offer objective insights into post-operative development profiles.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：在患有结节性硬化症综合征（TSC）及药物难治性癫痫（DRE）的儿童中，磁共振成像引导的立体定向激光消融（SLA）疗法相比开颅切除是一种创伤更小的治疗方式。本研究旨在进一步探讨接受SLA治疗的TSC相关DRE患者的长期结局。
> 
> 方法：回顾性纳入2016年7月1日至2022年1月1日期间在本机构接受SLA治疗的TSC合并DRE患者。于术后6个月、1年、2年及最近一次随访（若可得）时，评估抗癫痫药物（ASM）数量减少、由患者家属报告的发展改善、手术靶向发作类型及每日总癫痫发作频率变化，以及Engel分类。
> 
> 结果：共纳入40例TSC患者（年龄11个月至23岁，中位年龄3.6岁）接受SLA治疗。术后平均随访2.9年。80%的患者（n=32）总发作频率显著减少（超过50%）；在最后一次随访时，63%的患者（n=25）发作频率减少超过90%。58%的患者（n=23）达到了手术靶向发作类型的完全控制。最后随访时，63%的患者（n=25）报告有发展性改善，40%（n=16）减少了ASM使用，93%的患者（n=37）的Engel分级为III级或以下。
> 
> 结论：SLA治疗对大多数患者（包括非常年幼者）而言安全且有效。大部分患者的癫痫发作频率得到改善，包括手术靶向的发作类型，但减少药物的比例不足一半。报告中的发展改善与Engel结局相关。未来需在完整队列中利用神经心理学测试，以客观评估术后发展特征。

## 2. 阿尔茨海默病早期临床阶段睡眠纺锤体活动与认知下降的关联研究

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41364885)
**期刊：** Neurology
**PMID：** 41364885
**DOI：** 10.1212/WNL.0000000000214459

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Association of Sleep Spindle Activity With Cognitive Decline in Early Clinical Stages of Alzheimer Disease.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND AND OBJECTIVES: Sleep architecture, including spindles and slow oscillations, is disrupted in Alzheimer disease (AD). How changes in these sleep elements relate to cognitive decline is less clear. Our objectives were to examine changes in sleep macroarchitecture and microarchitecture in early clinical stages of AD compared with older adult controls (CTLs) and to investigate their associations with longitudinal cognitive change.
> 
> METHODS: This was both a cross-sectional and longitudinal study performed at Mass General Brigham Hospitals, where participants with early AD or CTLs underwent overnight ambulatory scalp EEG and longitudinal cognitive testing. We compared sleep microarchitectural features extracted from the EEG, including spindle activity, across the brain topography and between groups. We then performed longitudinal analyses using mixed-effects models to test the association of these sleep features with changes in cognition on the Montreal Cognitive Assessment (MoCA), collected annually for up to 7 years.
> 
> RESULTS: AD (n = 47, mean age 74.1 years, 66% female) and CTL (n = 43, mean age 72.6 years, 56% female) groups spent a similar proportion of sleep time in each stage of sleep. Sleep efficiency, however, was lower in the AD group (mean: CTL 75.1% vs AD 70.9%;  = 0.034). We found a significant reduction in spindle range power (11-16 Hz) in patients with AD compared with CTLs, particularly in the temporal regions (mean normalized power at EEG channels T3/T4: CTL 3.13 ± 1.13 vs AD 2.48 ± 1.01;  = 0.005). In participants with longitudinal MoCA scores (AD = 26, CTL = 25), reduced temporal lobe spindle density (β = 0.61, 95% CI 0.35-0.87; false discovery rate [FDR]-adjusted  < 0.001) and temporal lobe spindle power (β = 0.56, 95% CI 0.22-0.88; FDR-adjusted  = 0.005) were each associated with a faster rate of cognitive decline.
> 
> DISCUSSION: Temporal lobe sleep spindle activity is reduced in early clinical stages of AD and is associated with a faster rate of cognitive decline. Our results underscore the importance of including temporal lobe measurements when assessing sleep neurophysiology in AD, which is not standard in polysomnography. Future work examining the relationship between AD biomarkers and reduced spindle activity is needed to elucidate the potential mechanisms underlying these findings.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景与研究目的：阿尔茨海默病（AD）患者的睡眠结构，包括纺锤波和慢振荡，往往受到破坏。然而，这些睡眠要素的变化如何与认知衰退相关尚不清楚。本研究的目的在于比较AD早期临床阶段与老年健康对照者（CTLs）在睡眠宏观结构及微观结构上的差异，并探讨这些变化与认知随时间变化的关联。
> 
> 方法：这是一项在麻省总医院布莱根医疗系统开展的横断面与纵向结合研究。早期AD患者与健康对照受试者均接受了过夜的可携式头皮脑电图（EEG）监测以及长期随访的认知测评。我们比较了从脑电图中提取的睡眠微观结构特征（包括纺锤体活动）在脑部不同拓扑区域及两组间的差异。随后采用混合效应模型开展纵向分析，检验这些睡眠特征与蒙特利尔认知评估量表（MoCA）评分随时间变化之间的关联，随访最长达7年，每年评估一次。
> 
> 结果：AD组（n = 47，平均年龄74.1岁，女性占66%）与CTL组（n = 43，平均年龄72.6岁，女性占56%）在各睡眠阶段所占时间比例上相似。然而，AD组的睡眠效率较低（平均值：CTL组75.1%，AD组70.9%；p = 0.034）。在纺锤波频段功率（11–16 Hz）方面，AD患者较对照组显著降低，尤其在颞叶区域（EEG通道T3/T4的平均归一化功率：CTL 3.13 ± 1.13 vs AD 2.48 ± 1.01；p = 0.005）。在具备纵向MoCA数据的受试者中（AD = 26，CTL = 25），颞叶纺锤体密度降低（β = 0.61，95% CI 0.35–0.87；经错误发现率校正后p < 0.001）及颞叶纺锤功率降低（β = 0.56，95% CI 0.22–0.88；经FDR校正后p = 0.005）均与认知下降速率加快显著相关。
> 
> 结论：在AD的早期临床阶段，颞叶睡眠纺锤体活动减少，且与认知功能衰退加速相关。本研究结果强调，在评估AD患者的睡眠神经生理时，应纳入颞叶相关测量，而这在传统多导睡眠图中尚非常规。未来研究需进一步探讨AD生物标志物与纺锤体活动减少之间的关系，以阐明其潜在机制。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究聚焦于阿尔茨海默病早期的睡眠神经生理特征，首次系统地考察了纺锤体活动变化与认知衰退速度之间的纵向关系。研究采用头皮EEG采集并结合多年认知追踪，揭示颞叶区域纺锤体密度与功率的下降与认知下降加速密切相关，提出了颞叶睡眠生理指标作为早期疾病标志物的潜力。此研究在方法上具纵向特征和区域精细定位的优势，但样本量有限，机制性探索仍需后续多模态验证。
