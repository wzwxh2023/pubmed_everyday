## 1. 左氧氟沙星在中国儿童重症难治性肺炎患者中的群体药代动力学及暴露-反应分析

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42003577)
**期刊：** Antimicrobial agents and chemotherapy
**PMID：** 42003577
**DOI：** 10.1128/aac.01853-25

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Population pharmacokinetics and exposure-response analysis of levofloxacin in Chinese pediatric patients with severe refractory  pneumonia.

> **英文摘要：**
> Levofloxacin is recommended as an optional treatment for pediatric patients with severe refractory  pneumonia (SRMPP), but its use remains off-label due to limited pharmacokinetic/pharmacodynamic (PK/PD) data in this population. This study aimed to characterize the PK/PD properties of levofloxacin in pediatric SRMPP patients and define optimal dosing regimens. Scavenged blood samples from patients aged ≤18 years were collected using opportunistic sampling strategy to develop a population pharmacokinetic (PopPK) model of levofloxacin. The Cox proportional hazards model was applied to identify the optimal AUC cutoff associated with improved efficacy (shorter levofloxacin treatment duration and shorter cough resolution). Monte Carlo simulations were then performed to evaluate target attainment for this efficacy cutoff under guideline regimens (8-10 mg/kg q12 h for ages 1-5 years; 8-10 mg/kg q24 h for ages 5-16 years). A total of 191 pediatric patients (0.16-16 years) with SRMPP receiving intravenous levofloxacin were included in the PK analysis, and 161 patients were included in the exposure-response analysis. A two-compartment PopPK model was developed, identifying body weight as a significant covariate on clearance and central volume of distribution, and serum creatinine as an additional covariate on clearance. Patients with AUC ≥ 30.74 mg·h/L achieved shorter levofloxacin treatment duration ( = 0.019). Guideline-recommended dosing regimens result in overexposure in children <5 years and underexposure in those ≥5 years. Our model-based analysis suggests that 11 mg/kg q24 h or 5.5 mg/kg q12 h may provide more appropriate dosing regimens for children aged 1-16 years for the treatment of SRMPP.

> **中文摘要：**
> 左氧氟沙星被推荐作为重症难治性肺炎（SRMPP）儿科患者的可选治疗方案，但由于该人群中药代动力学/药效学（PK/PD）数据有限，其使用仍属于超说明书用药。本研究旨在表征左氧氟沙星在儿科 SRMPP 患者中的 PK/PD 特性，并确定最佳给药方案。利用机会性采样策略收集了 ≤18 岁患者的废弃血样，以建立左氧氟沙星的群体药代动力学（PopPK）模型。应用 Cox 比例风险模型来确定与疗效改善（左氧氟沙星治疗持续时间更短和咳嗽缓解时间更短）相关的最佳 AUC 切点。随后进行蒙特卡罗模拟，评估在指南方案（1-5 岁：8-10 mg/kg q12 h；5-16 岁：8-10 mg/kg q24 h）下，该疗效切点的目标达成情况。PK 分析共纳入了 191 名接受静脉注射左氧氟沙星的 SRMPP 儿科患者（0.16-16 岁），暴露-反应分析纳入了 161 名患者。建立了一个二室 PopPK 模型，确定体重是清除率和中央分布容积的显著协变量，血清肌酐是清除率的额外协变量。AUC ≥ 30.74 mg·h/L 的患者实现了更短的左氧氟沙星治疗持续时间（P = 0.019）。指南推荐的给药方案导致 5 岁以下儿童暴露过度，而 5 岁及以上儿童暴露不足。我们的基于模型的分析建议，11 mg/kg q24 h 或 5.5 mg/kg q12 h 可能为 1-16 岁儿童治疗 SRMPP 提供更合适的给药方案。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究通过建立二室群体药代动力学模型，系统评价了左氧氟沙星在中国重症难治性肺炎患儿中的暴露-反应关系。研究明确了体重和肌酐是影响药物清除的关键因素，并确定了与临床获益相关的关键暴露阈值（AUC ≥ 30.74 mg·h/L）。其创新性在于揭示了现行指南剂量在不同年龄段存在的暴露偏差，并据此提出了优化的精准给药建议，为临床超说明书用药提供了坚实的证据支持。
