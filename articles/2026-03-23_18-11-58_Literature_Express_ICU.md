## 1. 院外心脏骤停患者心肺复苏期间经食管超声心动图的应用：EXECT-CPR随机临床试验

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41870444)
**期刊：** JAMA internal medicine
**PMID：** 41870444
**DOI：** 10.1001/jamainternmed.2026.0102

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Transesophageal Echocardiography During CPR in Patients With Out-of-Hospital Cardiac Arrest: The EXECT-CPR Randomized Clinical Trial.

> **英文摘要：**
> IMPORTANCE: Cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) guidelines recommend chest compressions at the lower half of the sternum. This may lead to aortic valve compression, which is associated with poor outcomes, while compressions over the left ventricle are seldom achieved.
> 
> OBJECTIVE: To test the hypothesis that transesophageal echocardiography (TEE) guidance during CPR to avoid aortic valve compression and target the left ventricle would improve outcomes in patients with nontraumatic out-of-hospital cardiac arrest compared with conventional CPR.
> 
> DESIGN, SETTING, AND PARTICIPANTS: This cluster-randomized clinical trial (the EXECT-CPR study) was conducted from June 26 to November 19, 2023, at 1 tertiary medical center in Taiwan. Participants were adults who consecutively presented to the emergency department (ED) with nontraumatic out-of-hospital cardiac arrest. Exclusion criteria were prehospital return of spontaneous circulation, extracorporeal CPR, contraindications to TEE, prior do-not-resuscitate orders, and obvious signs of death. Complete blinding was not feasible; the allocation schedule was disclosed only to the principal investigator.
> 
> INTERVENTION: Post-ED arrival CPR at TEE-guided (avoid aortic-valve compression and target the left ventricle) or guideline-recommended (the lower half of the sternum) site.
> 
> MAIN OUTCOMES AND MEASURES: The primary outcome was a sustained return of spontaneous circulation (≥20 minutes). Secondary outcomes were any return of spontaneous circulation, survival to intensive care unit admission, survival to hospital discharge, cerebral performance category of 2 or lower at discharge, and intra-CPR end-tidal carbon dioxide levels.
> 
> RESULTS: A total of 132 patients underwent randomization (66 in each group; median [IQR] age, 68 [55-74] years; 87 [66%] male). The primary outcome was similar between groups (TEE-guided group, 29 [44%]; conventional group, 26 [39%]; cluster-adjusted odds ratio, 1.21; 95% CI, 0.64-2.29). The secondary outcomes also did not significantly differ, except for higher intra-CPR end-tidal carbon dioxide levels in the TEE-guided group during the 11th to 20th minutes after arrival. Adverse event rates related to TEE and CPR were comparable.
> 
> CONCLUSIONS AND RELEVANCE: In this randomized clinical trial among adults transported to the emergency department with ongoing CPR for nontraumatic out-of-hospital cardiac arrest, TEE-guided CPR with an adjusted compression site after arrival did not significantly improve clinical outcomes compared with conventional CPR, although it produced potential hemodynamic benefits without increasing adverse events. Given that the trial was underpowered due to optimistic effect size assumptions, these neutral findings should be interpreted with caution.
> 
> TRIAL REGISTRATION: ClinicalTrials.gov Identifier: NCT05907460.

> **中文摘要：**
> 重要性：心肺复苏（CPR）指南建议在胸骨下半部分进行胸外按压。这可能导致主动脉瓣受压，从而导致不良结局，而针对左心室的按压则很少能实现。
> 
> 目的：旨在验证以下假设：与常规CPR相比，在CPR期间通过经食管超声心动图（TEE）引导以避免主动脉瓣受压并靶向左心室，可改善非创伤性院外心脏骤停患者的临床结局。
> 
> 设计、场所和参与者：这项集群随机临床试验（EXECT-CPR研究）于2023年6月26日至11月19日在台湾的一家三级医疗中心进行。参与者为因非创伤性院外心脏骤停而连续转至急诊科（ED）的成人。排除标准包括院前恢复自主循环、体外心肺复苏、TEE禁忌症、预先存在的“拒绝复苏”医嘱以及明显的死亡体征。完全盲法不可行；分配计划仅向主要研究者披露。
> 
> 干预措施：到达急诊科后，在TEE引导下（避开主动脉瓣受压并靶向左心室）或按指南推荐（胸骨下半部分）的部位进行CPR。
> 
> 主要结局和衡量指标：主要结局是持续恢复自主循环（≥20分钟）。次要结局包括任何形式的自主循环恢复、存活至入住重症监护室、存活至出院、出院时脑功能分类评分为2分或更低，以及CPR期间的呼气末二氧化碳水平。
> 
> 结果：共有132名患者接受了随机分组（每组66人；中位[IQR]年龄为68 [55-74]岁；87人 [66%] 为男性）。主要结局在两组之间相似（TEE引导组29人 [44%]；常规组26人 [39%]；集群调整后的比值比为1.21；95% CI，0.64-2.29）。次要结局亦无显著差异，但在到达后的第11至20分钟内，TEE引导组在CPR期间的呼气末二氧化碳水平较高。与TEE和CPR相关的不良事件发生率具有可比性。
> 
> 结论和相关性：在这项针对因非创伤性院外心脏骤停而转运至急诊科并持续接受CPR的成人的随机临床试验中，与常规CPR相比，到达后通过TEE引导调整按压部位并未显著改善临床结局，尽管它在不增加不良事件的情况下产生了潜在的血流动力学获益。鉴于由于乐观的效应量假设导致该试验效能不足，这些中性结果应谨慎解释。
> 
> 试验注册：ClinicalTrials.gov 标识符：NCT05907460。

## 2. 为捐献做好准备，而非为死亡：一项关于重症监护室中心源性死亡后控制性器官捐献家属体验的质性研究

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41870237)
**期刊：** Critical care medicine
**PMID：** 41870237
**DOI：** 10.1097/CCM.0000000000007122

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Ready for Donation, Not for Death: A Qualitative Study of Families' Experience With Controlled Donation After Circulatory Determination of Death in Intensive Care.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVES: Research on relatives' experiences with controlled donation after circulatory determination of death (cDCDD) is limited. This study aims to explore the challenges and experiences of relatives whose loved ones die under cDCDD, focusing on how they navigate the process.
> 
> DESIGN: A qualitative study using semi-structured, in-depth interviews conducted 6 months post-death. This research is part of a larger quantitative multicenter study on the psychologic burden in relatives of cDCDD patients. Relatives of patients in the cDCDD process were purposively sampled and invited to participate in qualitative interviews. Thematic analysis was used to explore the phenomenon from the participants' perspectives.
> 
> SETTING: This study was conducted in France between November 2022 and November 2023.
> 
> PATIENTS: Family members of ICU patients identified as potential cDCDD donors.
> 
> INTERVENTIONS: None.
> 
> MEASUREMENTS AND MAIN RESULTS: Of 23 family members approached, 19 (mean age 53 yr; 11 women) agreed to participate. The mean interview duration was 43 minutes. In three cases, organ retrieval was not possible. Thematic analysis revealed three key themes: 1) "Two decisions, two worlds"-discussing the decision to withdraw life-sustaining therapies and the organ donation request; 2) "Waiting for death"-the period between the decision and its implementation; and 3) "A controlled death"-dying and death in a monitored and technical environment.
> 
> CONCLUSIONS: Relatives can be deeply engaged in cDCDD when well-prepared. However, additional support is needed during the waiting period, as families witness their loved one's decline. Proper preparation for dying is crucial to prevent perceptions of suffering, thereby reducing distress. Healthcare professionals must balance emotional, ethical, and medical considerations to facilitate informed choices in a highly regulated system.

> **中文摘要：**
> 【目的】关于亲属在心源性死亡后控制性器官捐献（cDCDD）中体验的研究有限。本研究旨在探讨在cDCDD程序下失去亲人的家属所面临的挑战和体验，重点关注他们如何应对这一过程。
> 
> 【设计】一项质性研究，采用在死亡6个月后进行的半结构化深度访谈。本研究是一项关于cDCDD患者亲属心理负担的大型定量多中心研究的一部分。通过目的抽样选取cDCDD程序中患者的亲属，并邀请其参加质性访谈。采用主题分析法从参与者的角度探讨这一现象。
> 
> 【地点】本研究于2022年11月至2023年11月在法国进行。
> 
> 【患者】被确定为潜在cDCDD供体的ICU患者的家属。
> 
> 【干预措施】无。
> 
> 【测量指标及主要结果】在接触的23名家属中，有19名（平均年龄53岁；11名女性）同意参加。平均访谈时长为43分钟。在3个案例中，未能进行器官获取。主题分析揭示了三个核心主题：1）“两个决定，两个世界”——讨论撤除维持生命治疗的决定与器官捐献请求；2）“等待死亡”——决定与执行之间的时期；3）“受控的死亡”——在受监控的技术环境中的垂危与死亡。
> 
> 【结论】如果准备充分，亲属可以深度参与到cDCDD中。然而，在等待期间需要额外的支持，因为家属会亲眼目睹亲人的衰减。为死亡做好充分准备对于防止痛苦感、从而减轻心理压力至关重要。医护人员必须平衡情感、伦理和医学因素，以在高度规范的体系中促进知情选择。

## 3. 脓毒症及其随后的精神疾病发病率：一项基于全国人口的匹配队列研究，2008-2019年

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41870200)
**期刊：** Critical care medicine
**PMID：** 41870200
**DOI：** 10.1097/CCM.0000000000007105

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Sepsis and Subsequent Psychiatric Morbidity: A Nationwide Population-Based Matched Cohort Study, 2008-2019.

> **英文摘要：**
> OBJECTIVES: To quantify the risk of incident psychiatric morbidity after community-acquired sepsis and assess whether new chronic diseases mediate the association.
> 
> DESIGN: Nationwide, population-based matched register cohort; hazards estimated with weighted Cox regression.
> 
> SETTING: Sweden, linking the National Quality Sepsis Registry, National Patient Register, Prescribed Drug Register, and population registers.
> 
> PATIENTS: Ten thousand three hundred eight adults (≥ 18 yr) treated in an ICU for sepsis (2008-2019), matched to 155,705 population controls by sex, age, region, and year. Individuals with a psychiatric diagnosis within 5 years or psychotropic medication within 1 year before index were excluded.
> 
> INTERVENTIONS: None.
> 
> MEASUREMENTS AND MAIN RESULTS: The primary outcome, psychiatric event, was first occurrence after index date of either initiation of a psychotropic medication (anatomic therapeutic chemical classification system code N05A, N05BA, N05C, N06A) in the Prescribed Drug Register (capturing prescriptions from primary and specialist care) or a new International Classification of Diseases, 10th Edition mood (F3) or anxiety (F4) diagnosis in specialist care. Weighted Cox models balanced baseline covariates. We used a Landmark approach with risk sets at 0-30, 31-90, 91-365 days; 1-3, 3-5, and greater than or equal to 5 years after the index date. Sepsis was associated with increased hazards of psychiatric events vs. matched controls, with the strongest associations in the first year (0-30 d: adjusted hazard ratio [aHR], 6.2 [5.0-7.7]; 31-90 d: aHR, 7.4 [6.5-8.6]; and 91-365 d: aHR, 2.3 [2.1-2.5]) attenuating over time but remaining elevated through 5 years (1-3 yr: aHR, 1.2 [1.1-1.5]; 3-5 yr: aHR, 1.3 [1.1-1.5]; and ≥ 5 yr: aHR, 1.1 [0.9-1.3]). In mediation analyses considering incident chronic diseases, estimates changed little, suggesting that these conditions did not mediate the association.
> 
> CONCLUSIONS: Patients with sepsis had a higher subsequent incidence of psychiatric events compared with matched population controls, with a persistently elevated risk for at least 5 years. This increased risk suggests that sepsis may have a long-term impact on psychiatric health, warranting consideration of preventive strategies.

> **中文摘要：**
> 目的：量化社区获得性脓毒症后新发精神疾病的风险，并评估新发的慢性疾病是否在此关联中起中介作用。
> 
> 设计：基于全国人口的匹配登记队列；使用加权Cox回归估算风险比。
> 
> 地点：瑞典，关联了国家质量脓毒症登记库、国家患者登记库、处方药登记库和人口登记库。
> 
> 患者：2008-2019年间在重症监护室（ICU）接受脓毒症治疗的10,308名成人（≥18岁），根据性别、年龄、地区和年份匹配了155,705名人群对照。排除了索引日期前5年内有精神科诊断或前1年内服用过精神药物的个体。
> 
> 干预措施：无。
> 
> 测量指标及主要结果：主要结局指标（精神事件）是索引日期后首次发生以下情况：在处方药登记库（涵盖基层和专科医疗处方）中开始服用精神药物（解剖治疗化学分类系统代码N05A、N05BA、N05C、N06A），或在专科医疗中获得新的《国际疾病分类》第10版心境（F3）或焦虑（F4）障碍诊断。加权Cox模型平衡了基线协变量。我们使用了地标分析法，在索引日期后的0-30天、31-90天、91-365天、1-3年、3-5年以及大于等于5年设置风险集。与匹配对照组相比，脓毒症与精神事件风险增加相关，在第一年关联最强（0-30天：调整后风险比 [aHR] 为6.2 [5.0-7.7]；31-90天：aHR为7.4 [6.5-8.6]；91-365天：aHR为2.3 [2.1-2.5]），虽然随时间推移有所减弱，但在5年内仍保持升高（1-3年：aHR为1.2 [1.1-1.5]；3-5年：aHR为1.3 [1.1-1.5]；≥5年：aHR为1.1 [0.9-1.3]）。在考虑新发慢性疾病的中介分析中，估算值变化不大，表明这些状况并未介导该关联。
> 
> 结论：与匹配的人群对照组相比，脓毒症患者随后发生精神事件的发病率更高，且风险持续升高至少5年。这种增加的风险表明脓毒症可能对心理健康产生长期影响，值得考虑预防策略。

## 6. 基于临床数据的美国医院儿科脓毒症全国性估算

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41865411)
**期刊：** JAMA
**PMID：** 41865411
**DOI：** 10.1001/jama.2026.3100

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** National Estimates of Pediatric Sepsis in US Hospitals Using Clinical Data.

> **英文摘要：**
> IMPORTANCE: Pediatric sepsis causes substantial morbidity and mortality, but population surveillance relies on administrative codes with limited and variable accuracy.
> 
> OBJECTIVE: To estimate US national incidence, mortality, and trends of sepsis in nonneonatal children using a Pediatric Sepsis Event (PSE) definition adapted from the 2024 Phoenix criteria for scalable electronic health record (EHR)-based surveillance using routinely captured clinical data.
> 
> DESIGN, SETTING, AND PARTICIPANTS: Retrospective cohort study of 3.9 million hospitalizations (age, >30 days to 17 years) in 2 EHR datasets: Epic Cosmos (245 health care systems, 2016-2023) and HCA Healthcare (146 hospitals, 2018-2023). Secondary datasets were analyzed to assess feasibility of implementation and face validity across heterogeneous settings. The PSE was validated through medical record reviews of 581 high-risk encounters at 3 geographically diverse hospitals.
> 
> EXPOSURES: A PSE required presumed infection with concurrent organ dysfunction using Phoenix-derived thresholds adapted for routine EHR data. Septic shock was defined as a PSE with cardiovascular dysfunction.
> 
> MAIN OUTCOMES AND MEASURES: Sepsis incidence, characteristics, and in-hospital mortality were calculated. Sensitivity and specificity of PSE for physician-adjudicated Phoenix sepsis were compared with administrative codes for severe sepsis/septic shock. National sepsis case counts and deaths in 2022 and temporal trends from 2016 to 2022 were estimated using regression models.
> 
> RESULTS: Among 3 925 809 pediatric hospitalizations from 2016 to 2023, 51 542 sepsis cases (mean age, 6.6 [SD, 6.0] years; 22 840 [44.3%] female) were identified (1.3% incidence); 37 405 (72.6%) were community onset and 31 744 (61.6%) had septic shock. In-hospital mortality was 10.1% and sepsis was present in 17.8% of hospitalizations that culminated in death. Incidence, characteristics, and mortality were broadly consistent across secondary datasets. On medical record review, the PSE definition had 69.9% sensitivity (95% CI, 58.1%-79.8%) and 93.1% specificity (95% CI, 89.6%-95.7%), with higher sensitivity than and comparable specificity with administrative codes. National estimates for 2022 were 18 231 sepsis cases (95% CI, 16 129-20 334) and 1877 deaths(95% CI, 1629-2126). Neither sepsis cases nor deaths changed significantly from 2016 to 2022 (annual change, 0.2% [95% CI, -2.2% to 2.7%] and 0.3% [95% CI, -3.1% to 3.8%], respectively).
> 
> CONCLUSIONS AND RELEVANCE: An EHR-based definition for pediatric sepsis demonstrated strong validity compared with physician-adjudicated Phoenix sepsis and identified sepsis in 1.3% of pediatric hospitalizations with 10% mortality, corresponding to more than 18 000 cases and more than 1800 deaths annually in the US.

> 【重要性】儿科脓毒症导致了显著的发病率和死亡率，但人群监测目前仍依赖于准确性有限且波动较大的行政代码。【目的】利用基于2024年Phoenix标准改编的儿科脓毒症事件（PSE）定义，通过常规采集的临床数据进行基于电子健康档案（EHR）的可扩展监测，从而评估美国非新生儿儿童脓毒症的全国发病率、死亡率及其趋势。【设计、场所与受试者】本研究是对两个EHR数据集（Epic Cosmos，包含245个医疗系统，2016-2023年；以及HCA Healthcare，包含146家医院，2018-2023年）中390万次住院记录（年龄30天至17岁）进行的回顾性队列研究。通过分析次级数据集评估了其在异质性环境下的实施可行性和表面效度。通过对3家不同地理位置医院的581次高风险就诊进行病历审查，验证了PSE定义。【暴露因素】PSE定义要求在疑似感染的同时，器官功能障碍达到针对常规EHR数据调整后的Phoenix衍生阈值。脓毒症休克定义为伴有心血管功能障碍的PSE。【主要指标与衡量方法】计算了脓毒症的发病率、特征和院内死亡率。将PSE对于医生裁定的Phoenix脓毒症诊断的敏感性和特异性与严重脓毒症/脓毒症休克的行政代码进行了比较。利用回归模型估算了2022年美国全国脓毒症病例数和死亡人数，以及2016年至2022年的时间趋势。【结果】在2016年至2023年的3,925,809例儿科住院病例中，共识别出51,542例脓毒症病例（平均年龄6.6岁 [标准差6.0]；22,840例 [44.3%] 为女性），发病率为1.3%；其中37,405例（72.6%）为社区发病，31,744例（61.6%）伴有脓毒症休克。院内死亡率为10.1%，在最终死亡的住院病例中，17.8%存在脓毒症。次级数据集中发病率、特征和死亡率基本一致。在病历审查中，PSE定义的敏感性为69.9%（95% CI, 58.1%-79.8%），特异性为93.1%（95% CI, 89.6%-95.7%），其敏感性高于行政代码，特异性则与行政代码相当。2022年美国全国估算值为18,231例脓毒症病例（95% CI, 16,129-20,334）和1,877例死亡（95% CI, 1,629-2,126）。从2016年到2022年，脓毒症病例数和死亡人数均无显著变化（年度变化分别为0.2%和0.3%）。【结论与相关性】基于EHR的儿科脓毒症定义与医生裁定的Phoenix脓毒症相比显示出较强的有效性，并在1.3%的儿科住院病例中识别出脓毒症，死亡率为10%，这对应于美国每年超过18,000例脓毒症病例和1,800多例死亡。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

这项大规模回顾性队列研究利用超390万例住院数据，成功开发并验证了基于2024年新Phoenix标准的儿科脓毒症事件（PSE）监测定义，解决了传统行政代码准确性不足的难题。研究揭示了美国儿科脓毒症约1.3%的稳定发病率及其10.1%的高病死率，为全国流行病学监测提供了高可靠性的临床数据工具。其创新性在于实现了基于常规EHR数据的可扩展自动化监测，尽管在敏感性方面仍有优化空间，但为优化儿科脓毒症的公共卫生资源配置和质量改进奠定了坚实基础。
