## 2. 快速抗菌药物敏感性测试用于革兰氏阴性菌菌血症：FAST随机临床试验

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41999287)
**期刊：** JAMA
**PMID：** 41999287
**DOI：** 10.1001/jama.2026.5487

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Fast Antimicrobial Susceptibility Testing for Gram-Negative Bacteremia: The FAST Randomized Clinical Trial.

> **英文摘要：**
> IMPORTANCE: Novel blood culture diagnostics provide rapid antimicrobial susceptibility testing (AST) results for bacteria causing bloodstream infections (BSIs) but have unclear clinical impact.
> 
> OBJECTIVE: To evaluate clinical outcomes of blood culture testing using a rapid AST method compared with standard AST in patients with BSIs caused by gram-negative bacilli in regions with high prevalence of antimicrobial resistance.
> 
> DESIGN, SETTING, AND PARTICIPANTS: Open-label randomized clinical trial that enrolled participants from December 2023 to May 2025 (final follow-up, June 18, 2025) at 7 medical centers in Greece (n = 2), India (n = 1), Israel (n = 3), and Spain (n = 1). Hospitalized patients (adults and children) with BSIs caused by gram-negative bacilli were eligible. Statistical analysis was conducted from August 2025 to January 2026.
> 
> INTERVENTION: Patients were randomized to undergo blood culture evaluation using rapid, phenotypic AST directly from positive blood cultures plus standard susceptibility testing (n = 413) vs standard susceptibility testing alone (n = 437). Local antimicrobial stewardship teams reviewed all patients and provided treatment recommendations.
> 
> MAIN OUTCOMES AND MEASURES: The primary outcome was a desirability of outcome ranking (DOOR) at day 30, with 3 categories (alive without deleterious events, alive with deleterious events, and death). The difference between rapid and standard testing was summarized as the probability that the DOOR outcomes were more desirable in the rapid testing group. Superiority was concluded if the lower limit of the 95% CI exceeded 50%. Secondary outcomes included 30-day mortality, length of hospitalization, intensive care unit admission, acquisition of hospital-acquired infections, time to effective antibiotic therapy within 3 days, and time to antibiotic escalation or deescalation within 3 days.
> 
> RESULTS: Of the 899 patients randomized, 850 were included in the primary outcome analysis (median [IQR] age, 72 [21] years; 43% female). The probability that DOOR outcomes were more favorable in the rapid testing group was 48.8% (95% CI, 45.3%-52.4%). Median time to effective antibiotic therapy was not different between the groups in the as-randomized population. Median time to antibiotic escalation or deescalation was faster in the rapid testing group by 14 hours (95% CI, 6-22) in the as-randomized population. There were no differences between the groups in other secondary outcomes. In the prespecified subgroup with carbapenem-resistant infections, median time to effective therapy was 9.5 hours in the rapid testing group vs 28 hours in the standard testing group (difference, -18 hours [95% CI, -42 to 6]).
> 
> CONCLUSIONS AND RELEVANCE: Among patients with gram-negative bacilli BSIs, rapid blood culture AST was not superior to standard testing by DOOR. When considered with other efficacy and safety outcomes, these findings may help inform the use of rapid susceptibility testing in clinical practice.
> 
> TRIAL REGISTRATION: ClinicalTrials.gov Identifier: NCT06174649.

> **中文摘要：**
> 重要性：新型血培养诊断技术可为引起血流感染（BSI）的细菌提供快速的抗菌药物敏感性测试（AST）结果，但其临床影响尚不明确。
> 
> 目的：在抗菌药物耐药性高发地区，评估在革兰氏阴性杆菌引起的BSI患者中，使用快速AST方法与标准AST方法进行血培养测试的临床结局。
> 
> 设计、地点和参与者：一项开放标签的随机临床试验，于2023年12月至2025年5月期间在希腊（2个）、印度（1个）、以色列（3个）和西班牙（1个）的7个医疗中心招募参与者（最后随访日期为2025年6月18日）。因革兰氏阴性杆菌导致BSI的住院患者（成人和儿童）符合入选条件。统计分析于2025年8月至2026年1月进行。
> 
> 干预措施：患者被随机分配接受直接从阳性血培养物进行的快速表型AST加标准药敏测试（n=413），或仅接受标准药敏测试（n=437）。当地的抗菌药物管理团队对所有患者进行了审查并提供了治疗建议。
> 
> 主要结局和测量指标：主要结局是第30天的期望结局排序（DOOR），分为3个类别（存活且无有害事件、存活但有有害事件、死亡）。快速测试与标准测试之间的差异被概括为快速测试组的DOOR结局更优的概率。如果95%置信区间的下限超过50%，则可判定为优效。次要结局包括30天死亡率、住院时长、重症监护室入住情况、院内感染获得情况、3天内获得有效抗生素治疗的时间，以及3天内抗生素升级或降级的时间。
> 
> 结果：在随机分配的899名患者中，850名被纳入主要结局分析（中位[IQR]年龄为72[21]岁；43%为女性）。快速测试组的DOOR结局更优的概率为48.8%（95% CI, 45.3%-52.4%）。在随机人群中，两组获得有效抗生素治疗的中位时间没有差异。在随机人群中，快速测试组的抗生素升级或降级的中位时间快了14小时（95% CI, 6-22）。两组在其他次要结局上没有差异。在预先设定的碳青霉烯类耐药感染亚组中，快速测试组获得有效治疗的中位时间为9.5小时，而标准测试组为28小时（差异为-18小时[95% CI, -42至6]）。
> 
> 结论与相关性：在革兰氏阴性杆菌BSI患者中，通过DOOR评价，快速血培养AST并未优于标准测试。当结合其他有效性和安全性结局考虑时，这些发现可能有助于为快速药敏测试在临床实践中的应用提供信息。
> 
> 试验注册：ClinicalTrials.gov 标识符：NCT06174649。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究是一项多中心随机临床试验，旨在评估快速抗菌药物敏感性测试（AST）相对于标准方法，能否改善耐药高发地区革兰氏阴性菌菌血症患者的临床结局。研究发现，尽管快速AST未能在一个综合性主要终点（DOOR）上证实其优越性，但它能显著缩短抗生素调整时间约14小时。尤其值得关注的是，在碳青霉烯类耐药感染的亚组中，快速AST显示出大幅缩短获得有效治疗时间的潜力。此研究的结论提示，快速诊断技术虽能加速决策，但其临床价值的体现可能更集中于特定的耐药困境中，而非普适性的结局改善。

## 3. 儿童期重症疟疾后的长期认知能力与学业成就

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41999286)
**期刊：** JAMA
**PMID：** 41999286
**DOI：** 10.1001/jama.2026.0704

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Long-Term Cognitive Ability and Academic Achievement After Childhood Severe Malaria.

> **英文摘要：**
> IMPORTANCE: Cerebral malaria and severe malarial anemia are associated with cognitive impairment and decreased academic achievement 1 to 2 years after the initial episode. The extent to which impairment persists into later childhood and adolescence is unknown.
> 
> OBJECTIVE: To determine whether severe malaria in children is associated with long-term cognitive impairment or decreased academic achievement.
> 
> DESIGN, SETTING, AND PARTICIPANTS: Assessment of Ugandan children enrolled in 2 prior cohort studies of severe malaria; 1247 children completed the prior studies (2008-2018), of whom 958 were traceable (77%), and 939 (75%) enrolled in the present study (2020-2023). Data from 889 individuals younger than 18 years were analyzed.
> 
> EXPOSURES: Cerebral malaria (n = 184), severe malarial anemia (n = 249), other forms of severe malaria (respiratory distress, complicated seizures, or prostration, n = 239), and unaffected community children (n = 217).
> 
> MAIN OUTCOMES AND MEASURES: Descriptive analysis including age-adjusted z scores of overall cognitive ability, attention, and academic achievement (math, reading).
> 
> RESULTS: Participants (mean age, 11.1 [SD, 3.4] years; 44.2% female) were tested 4 to 15 years (mean, 8.4 [SD, 2.7] years) after their severe malaria episode. Compared with community children, children with a history of cerebral malaria or severe malarial anemia had lower scores in overall cognition (adjusted mean difference, -0.41 [Bonferroni-corrected 95% CI, -0.74 to -0.09] and -0.31 [95% CI, -0.61 to -0.01], respectively) and math (-0.46 [95% CI, -0.78 to -0.14] and -0.32 [95% CI, -0.61 to -0.03], respectively), while attention and reading scores did not differ significantly. Cognitive and academic scores were not significantly different between children with other forms of severe malaria and community children. In children with cerebral malaria or severe malarial anemia, acute kidney injury, hyperuricemia, and elevated plasma angiopoietin-2 levels at the time of the severe malaria episode were associated with worse z scores in overall cognitive ability (-0.44 [95% CI, -0.80 to -0.08], -0.45 [95% CI, -0.88 to -0.02], and -0.33 [95% CI, -0.63 to -0.03], respectively). In children with cerebral malaria or severe malarial anemia, acute kidney injury was additionally associated with lower z scores in reading (95% CI, -0.42 [95% CI, -0.79 to -0.05]) and math (95% CI, -0.39 [-0.74 to -0.04]).
> 
> CONCLUSIONS AND RELEVANCE: Among survivors of a severe childhood malaria episode, cerebral malaria and severe malarial anemia in childhood are associated with cognitive impairment and decreased academic achievement in some metrics 4 to 15 years after the index episode.

> **中文摘要：**
> 重要性：脑型疟和重症疟疾贫血与初次发病后1至2年的认知损害和学业成就下降相关。这种损害持续到儿童晚期和青春期的程度尚不清楚。
> 
> 目的：旨在确定儿童期重症疟疾是否与长期认知损害或学业成就下降相关。
> 
> 设计、地点与参与者：对先前参与2项重症疟疾队列研究的乌干达儿童进行评估；1247名儿童完成了先前的研究（2008-2018年），其中958名可追踪（77%），939名（75%）参加了本研究（2020-2023年）。分析了889名18岁以下个体的数据。
> 
> 暴露因素：脑型疟（n = 184）、重症疟疾贫血（n = 249）、其他形式的重症疟疾（呼吸窘迫、复杂性惊厥或虚脱，n = 239）以及未受影响的社区儿童（n = 217）。
> 
> 主要结局与测量指标：描述性分析，包括总体认知能力、注意力和学业成就（数学、阅读）的年龄校正z分数。
> 
> 结果：参与者（平均年龄11.1 [SD, 3.4]岁；44.2%为女性）在重症疟疾发作后4至15年（平均8.4 [SD, 2.7]年）接受了测试。与社区儿童相比，有脑型疟或重症疟疾贫血史的儿童在总体认知（校正后平均差分别为-0.41 [Bonferroni校正的95% CI, -0.74至-0.09]和-0.31 [95% CI, -0.61至-0.01]）和数学（分别为-0.46 [95% CI, -0.78至-0.14]和-0.32 [95% CI, -0.61至-0.03]）方面的得分较低，而注意力与阅读得分则无显著差异。其他形式重症疟疾儿童与社区儿童之间的认知和学业得分无显著差异。在患有脑型疟或重症疟疾贫血的儿童中，重症疟疾发作时的急性肾损伤、高尿酸血症和血浆血管生成素-2水平升高与更差的总体认知能力z分数相关（分别为-0.44 [95% CI, -0.80至-0.08]、-0.45 [95% CI, -0.88至-0.02]和-0.33 [95% CI, -0.63至-0.03]）。在患有脑型疟或重症疟疾贫血的儿童中，急性肾损伤还与较低的阅读（95% CI, -0.42 [95% CI, -0.79至-0.05]）和数学（95% CI, -0.39 [-0.74至-0.04]）z分数相关。
> 
> 结论与相关性：在儿童期重症疟疾的幸存者中，脑型疟和重症疟疾贫血与初次发病后4至15年的某些认知损害指标和学业成就下降相关。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

本研究通过对乌干达儿童队列进行长达15年的随访，旨在揭示儿童期重症疟疾对远期认知功能与学业成就的影响。研究发现，与社区健康儿童相比，脑型疟和重症疟疾贫血的幸存者在总体认知和数学能力上表现出持续性损害。该研究的创新之处在于其超长随访周期，首次证实了特定重症疟疾亚型可导致持续至青少年期的认知缺陷，并识别出急性肾损伤等早期风险预测因子。
