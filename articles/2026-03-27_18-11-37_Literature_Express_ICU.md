## 2. 冠状动脉疾病的生殖系与体细胞基因组整合模型

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41888514)
**期刊：** Nature communications
**PMID：** 41888514
**DOI：** 10.1038/s41467-026-70379-2

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** An integrated germline and somatic genomic model for coronary artery disease.

> **英文摘要：**
> Multiple germline and somatic genomic factors are associated with risk of coronary artery disease, but there is no single measure of risk that integrates all information from a DNA sample. To address this gap, we develop an integrated genomic model that includes six germline and somatic genetic drivers for coronary artery disease, including polygenic risk score, genetically-proxied proteomic/metabolomic risk scores, and clonal hematopoiesis of indeterminate potential. We evaluated its predictive power in the UK Biobank (N = 391,536), and validate it using data from the TOPMed program (N = 34,177). The 10-year coronary artery disease risk based on the integrated genomic model profile ranges from 1.1% to 15.5% in the UK Biobank and from 3.8% to 33.0% in TOPMed, with a more pronounced gradient in males than females. The integrated genomic model captures the cumulative effect of multiple genetic drivers, identifying individuals at high risk for coronary artery disease despite lacking any single high-risk genetic factor, as well as individuals at low risk despite carrying known high-risk factors. In middle age, the integrated genomic model augments the performance of the Pooled Cohort Equations, a clinical risk calculator for coronary artery disease. While the integrated genomic model yields only modest incremental predictive value over polygenic risk score at the population level, it identifies approximately 13% of high-risk individuals not detected by polygenic risk score alone.

> **中文摘要：**
> 多种生殖系和体细胞基因组因素与冠状动脉疾病风险相关，但目前尚无单一的风险衡量指标能够整合来自DNA样本的所有信息。为了填补这一空白，我们开发了一种整合基因组模型，其中包括六种冠状动脉疾病的生殖系和体细胞遗传驱动因素，包括多基因风险评分、遗传代理的蛋白质组/代谢组风险评分以及意义不明的克隆性造血（CHIP）。我们在英国生物样本库（N = 391,536）中评估了其预测能力，并利用来自TOPMed项目（N = 34,177）的数据进行了验证。基于整合基因组模型特征的10年冠状动脉疾病风险在英国生物样本库中为1.1%至15.5%，在TOPMed中为3.8%至33.0%，且男性的风险梯度比女性更为显著。该整合基因组模型捕捉了多种遗传驱动因素的累积效应，能够识别出尽管缺乏任何单一高风险遗传因素但仍处于冠状动脉疾病高风险的个体，以及尽管携带已知高风险因素但仍处于低风险的个体。在中年人群中，该整合基因组模型增强了汇总队列方程（Pooled Cohort Equations，一种冠状动脉疾病临床风险计算器）的预测性能。虽然在群体层面，该整合基因组模型相对于多基因风险评分仅产生了适度的增量预测价值，但它识别出了约13%仅靠多基因风险评分无法检出的高风险个体。

**英文原标题：** Amplifying metabolic profiling of extracellular vesicle dynamics with ACTIVITY.

> **英文摘要：**
> Extracellular vesicles (EVs) are emerging as promising circulating biomarkers for liquid biopsy due to their abundant molecular information, such as proteins, nucleic acids, and metabolites. However, the metabolic profiling of EVs remains largely unexplored, much less exploiting their intrinsic metabolic features for disease diagnosis. In this study, we demonstrate that the metabolic-related inducible nitric oxide synthase (iNOS) activity of macrophage-derived EVs serves as an effective biomarker for phenotypic profiling and further evaluating lung inflammation. By integrating a cascade amplification strategy that combines the biocatalysis of EV iNOS activity with the electrocatalysis of defective tungsten disulfide quantum dots (WS QDs), we develop an ACTIVITY (Amplified Cascade-catalysis TestIng for VesIcular meTabolic activitY) method for rapid assaying of metabolically active EVs. When applied to bronchoalveolar lavage fluid samples, this activity-based EV profiling differentiates pneumonia patients from healthy controls and further facilitates the monitoring of disease treatment, suggesting the potential of EV metabolic activity for diagnostics.

> **中文摘要：**
> 细胞外囊泡（EVs）因其含有丰富的分子信息（如蛋白质、核酸和代谢物），正成为液体活检中极具前景的循环生物标志物。然而，EVs的代谢图谱分析在很大程度上仍未被探索，利用其内在代谢特征进行疾病诊断的研究则更为匮乏。在本研究中，我们证明了巨噬细胞源性EVs中与代谢相关的诱导型一氧化氮合酶（iNOS）活性可作为表型分析及进一步评估肺部炎症的有效生物标志物。通过整合一种级联放大策略（结合了EV iNOS活性的生物催化与缺陷硫化钨量子点(WS QDs)的电催化），我们开发了一种名为ACTIVITY（用于囊泡代谢活性的放大级联催化检测）的方法，用于快速测定具有代谢活性的EVs。当应用于支气管肺泡灌洗液样本时，这种基于活性的EV图谱分析能够区分肺炎患者与健康对照组，并进一步辅助疾病治疗的监测，表明了EV代谢活性在诊断中的潜力。

## 4. 间质性肺疾病相关肺动脉高压的预后因素：来自 HYPID 队列和法国肺动脉高压登记库的数据

**期刊：** The European respiratory journal
**PMID：** 41887671
**DOI：** 10.1183/13993003.02233-2025

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Prognostic factors in interstitial lung disease-associated pulmonary hypertension: data from the HYPID cohort and the French Pulmonary Hypertension Registry.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: Pulmonary hypertension (PH) frequently complicates interstitial lung diseases (ILD), adversely affecting outcome. Identifying prognostic factors of patients with ILD-PH could facilitate early identification of patients who may benefit from PH therapy. METHODS: We included patients with ILD-PH from the prospective HYPID cohort and the French National Pulmonary Hypertension Registry (2007-2022). Univariable and multivariable analyses were performed to identify predictors of 1-year mortality. RESULTS: A total of 581 patients (mean age 69.4±9.3 years, 450 males) were analysed. ILD diagnoses were combined pulmonary fibrosis and emphysema syndrome (30.8%), idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (29.6%), unclassifiable ILD (13.1%), and fibrotic hypersensitivity pneumonitis (10.3%). Mean pulmonary artery pressure was 40.7±9.1 mmHg, and mean pulmonary vascular resistance was 7.6 ±3.5 Wood units. Off-label PH therapy was initiated after initial evaluation in 215 patients (37%). The median transplant-free survival time was 17 months (95% CI 15.2 - not reached). Multivariable analysis identified male sex (p<0.001), functional class III (p=0.003) or IV (p=0.003), 6 MWD≤228 m (p<0.001), PVR>5 Wood units (p=0.008), and absence of PH therapy (p<0.001) as independent predictors of death or lung transplantation at 1 year. CONCLUSION: Non-invasive (6 MWD, WHO FC) and invasive (PVR) variables are associated with prognosis in patients with ILD-PH, including in patients with CPFE. PH medication might improve outcomes in this patient population.

> 背景：肺动脉高压（PH）常并发于间质性肺疾病（ILD），对预后产生不利影响。确定 ILD-PH 患者的预后因素有助于早期识别可能从 PH 治疗中获益的患者。方法：我们纳入了来自前瞻性 HYPID 队列和法国国家肺动脉高压登记库（2007-2022 年）的 ILD-PH 患者。通过单变量和多变量分析确定 1 年死亡率的预测因子。结果：共分析了 581 例患者（平均年龄 69.4±9.3 岁，男性 450 例）。ILD 诊断包括组合性肺纤维化和肺气肿综合征（30.8%）、特发性肺纤维化（29.6%）、不可分类的 ILD（13.1%）和纤维化型超敏性肺炎（10.3%）。平均肺动脉压为 40.7±9.1 mmHg，平均肺血管阻力（PVR）为 7.6±3.5 Wood 单位。初步评估后，215 例患者（37%）开始接受超说明书 PH 治疗。中位无移植生存时间为 17 个月（95% CI 15.2 - 未达到）。多变量分析确定，男性（p<0.001）、心功能分级 III 级（p=0.003）或 IV 级（p=0.003）、6 分钟步行距离（6 MWD）≤228 m（p<0.001）、PVR >5 Wood 单位（p=0.008）以及未接受 PH 治疗（p<0.001）是 1 年死亡或肺移植的独立预测因子。结论：无创变量（6 MWD、WHO 功能分级）和有创变量（PVR）与 ILD-PH 患者（包括 CPFE 患者）的预后相关。PH 药物治疗可能会改善这一患者群体的预后。

本研究利用来自法国大型队列的 581 例 ILD-PH 患者数据，系统性地识别了影响其 1 年生存率的关键预测因子。研究强调了 6 MWD 和 PVR 等生理参数在风险分层中的核心作用，并特别指出 CPFE 患者同样受这些预后因素影响。尽管是一项观察性研究，但其关于 PH 靶向治疗与死亡风险降低之间相关性的发现，为 ILD-PH 的临床管理提供了强有力的循证依据，具有重要的临床指导意义。

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Nerandomilast in progressive pulmonary fibrosis: data from the whole follow-up period of the FIBRONEER-ILD trial.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: In the FIBRONEER-ILD trial in patients with progressive pulmonary fibrosis (PPF), nerandomilast 9 mg bid and 18 mg bid reduced the decline in forced vital capacity at week 52 compared with placebo (the primary endpoint). We assessed the effects of nerandomilast up to the final database lock. METHODS: Time to first acute exacerbation of ILD, hospitalisation for respiratory cause, or death (key secondary endpoint) and other time-to-event endpoints were assessed. RESULTS: 1176 patients, of whom 512 were taking background nintedanib, received nerandomilast or placebo. At final database lock, mean (sd) exposure to trial medication was 15.1 (5.7) months and mean (sd) observation period was 17.0 (4.1) months. Compared with placebo, the hazard ratio (95% CI) for the key secondary endpoint was 0.78 (0.61, 1.00) for nerandomilast 9 mg bid and 0.77 (0.60, 0.99) for nerandomilast 18 mg bid; hazard ratios were lower among patients not taking nintedanib (0.69 [0.49, 0.97] and 0.65 [0.46, 0.92], respectively) than among those taking background nintedanib (0.90 [0.63, 1.30] and 0.93 [0.65, 1.34], respectively). For death, the hazard ratio (95% CI)  placebo was 0.51 (0.34, 0.78) for both nerandomilast doses. Adverse events led to discontinuation of trial medication in 12.5%, 12.0% and 12.3% of the placebo, nerandomilast 9 mg bid and nerandomilast 18 mg bid groups, respectively. CONCLUSIONS: In the FIBRONEER-ILD trial in patients with PPF, nerandomilast reduced the risk of clinically important outcomes, including death, over the whole trial. Nerandomilast had a favourable safety and tolerability profile.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：在针对进展性肺纤维化（PPF）患者的FIBRONEER-ILD试验中，与安慰剂相比，nerandomilast 9 mg每日两次（bid）和18 mg bid可减少第52周时用力肺活量（FVC）的下降（主要终点）。本研究评估了nerandomilast直至最终数据库锁定时的疗效。方法：评估了至首次发生间质性肺疾病（ILD）急性加重、因呼吸原因住院或死亡的时间（关键次要终点）以及其他时间-事件终点。结果：共有1176名患者接受了nerandomilast或安慰剂治疗，其中512名患者正在接受尼达尼布作为基础治疗。在最终数据库锁定时，试验药物的平均（标准差）暴露时间为15.1（5.7）个月，平均（标准差）观察期为17.0（4.1）个月。与安慰剂相比，nerandomilast 9 mg bid的关键次要终点风险比（HR）（95% CI）为0.78 (0.61, 1.00)，18 mg bid为0.77 (0.60, 0.99)；在未服用尼达尼布的患者中，风险比更低（分别为0.69 [0.49, 0.97] 和 0.65 [0.46, 0.92]），而服用基础尼达尼布的患者风险比分别为0.90 [0.63, 1.30] 和 0.93 [0.65, 1.34]。在死亡风险方面，两种剂量的nerandomilast相对于安慰剂的HR均为0.51 (0.34, 0.78)。安慰剂组、nerandomilast 9 mg bid组和18 mg bid组因不良事件导致试验药物停药的比例分别为12.5%、12.0%和12.3%。结论：在针对PPF患者的FIBRONEER-ILD试验中，nerandomilast在整个试验期间降低了包括死亡在内的具有临床意义结局的风险。Nerandomilast具有良好的安全性和耐受性。

## 6. Smad7下调通过TGF-β1-Smad2/3通路促进肺动脉高压中的铁死亡

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41886369)
**期刊：** Shock (Augusta, Ga.)
**PMID：** 41886369
**DOI：** 10.1097/SHK.0000000000002832

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Smad7 Downregulation Promotes Ferroptosis in Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension via the TGF-β1-Smad2/3 Pathway.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: In pulmonary arterial hypertension (PAH), ferroptosis could influence vascular remodeling by affecting the pulmonary artery endothelial cells (PAECs). However, the underlying pathogenic mechanisms of ferroptosis in PAH and its impact on the pulmonary vascular cellular landscape remain poorly understood. METHODS: In this study, we integrated single-cell transcriptomic sequencing, proteomics, and summary-data-based Mendelian randomization (SMR) to investigate the regulatory mechanisms of ferroptosis in PAH. Our findings were validated using monocrotaline (MCT)-induced PAH rat models and hypoxia-induced PAEC models. RESULTS: Single-cell transcriptomic analysis revealed an increased propensity for ferroptosis in PAECs, while general capillary endothelial cells (gCAPs) showed enhanced proliferative tendencies. Integrated analysis of single-cell transcriptomics, proteomics and SMR suggested that SMAD7 may serve a negative regulator of ferroptosis in PAECs. Additionally, experiments conducted both in vitro and in vivo showed that reducing SMAD7 expression enhanced ferroptosis in PAECs, whereas SMAD7 overexpression alleviated ferroptosis and pulmonary vascular remodeling, potentially by blocking the TGF-β1-Smad2/3 signaling pathway. CONCLUSIONS: This study provides preliminary evidence that SMAD7 may negatively regulate ferroptosis via the TGF-β1-Smad2/3 Pathway in pulmonary artery endothelial cells and thereby contribute to pulmonary vascular remodeling in PAH. These findings highlight SMAD7 as a potential therapeutic target for PAH.

> **中文摘要：**
> 背景：在肺动脉高压（PAH）中，铁死亡可通过影响肺动脉内皮细胞（PAECs）来影响血管重构。然而，PAH中铁死亡的潜在致病机制及其对肺血管细胞景观的影响仍知之甚少。方法：在本研究中，我们整合了单细胞转录组测序、蛋白质组学和基于总结数据的孟德尔随机化（SMR）来研究PAH中铁死亡的调节机制。我们的研究结果利用单克隆抗体（MCT）诱导的PAH大鼠模型和缺氧诱导的PAEC模型进行了验证。结果：单细胞转录组分析显示，PAECs的铁死亡倾向增加，而普通毛细血管内皮细胞（gCAPs）表现出增强的增殖倾向。单细胞转录组学、蛋白质组学和SMR的综合分析表明，SMAD7可能是PAECs铁死亡的负调节因子。此外，体内外实验表明，降低SMAD7表达会增强PAECs的铁死亡，而SMAD7过表达则可减轻铁死亡和肺血管重构，这可能是通过阻断TGF-β1-Smad2/3信号通路实现的。结论：本研究提供了初步证据，表明SMAD7可能通过肺动脉内皮细胞中的TGF-β1-Smad2/3通路负向调节铁死亡，从而参与PAH的肺血管重构。这些发现强调了SMAD7作为PAH潜在治疗靶点的价值。
