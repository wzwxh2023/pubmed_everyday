## 3. 评估重症监护室中普及莫匹罗星去定植对 MRSA PCR 鼻拭子检测效用的影响

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41834148)
**期刊：** Clinical infectious diseases : an official publication of the Infectious Diseases Society of America
**PMID：** 41834148
**DOI：** 10.1093/cid/ciag183

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Evaluation of the impact of universal mupirocin decolonization in intensive care units on the utility of MRSA PCR nasal swab testing.

> **英文摘要：**
> BACKGROUND: The reliability of methicillin-resistant Staphylococcus aureus (MSRA) polymerase chain reaction (PCR) nasal swab testing in the context of mupirocin nasal decolonization has been questioned. This study investigated whether the negative predictive value (NPV) of MSRA PCR nasal swab testing following mupirocin decolonization was non-inferior to testing prior to decolonization. METHODS: This retrospective cohort study included adult inpatients admitted to intensive care units (ICUs) from February 2023 - August 2024 who underwent both MRSA PCR nasal swab testing and universal mupirocin decolonization. Patients were divided into 3 groups based on timing of PCR testing: prior to receiving any doses of topical mupirocin, no more than 7 days after, or greater than 7 days after a first dose. The primary outcome was the NPV of MRSA PCR nasal swab testing. RESULTS: 1034 patients were included in the primary analysis; 508 (49.1%) had a MRSA PCR nasal swab collected prior to the start of decolonization and 388 (73.8%) and 138 (26.2%) received nasal swabs > 0 to ≤7 days and >7 days after the start of mupirocin decolonization, respectively. The differences between the NPV of MRSA PCR testing in patients with swabs collected prior versus in patients with swabs collected >0 to ≤7 days and >7 days after the start of mupirocin were 0.3% and -0.4% (1-sided 95% CI -0.9% and CI -2.3%), respectively. CONCLUSIONS: The NPV of MRSA PCR nasal swabs is not diminished after mupirocin decolonization. MRSA PCR testing within 7 days of initiation of decolonization is non-inferior to testing prior to decolonization.

> **中文摘要：**
> 【背景】在莫匹罗星鼻部去定植的背景下，耐甲氧西林金黄色葡萄球菌（MRSA）聚合酶链反应（PCR）鼻拭子检测的可靠性受到了质疑。本研究调查了莫匹罗星去定植后 MRSA PCR 鼻拭子检测的阴性预测值（NPV）是否非劣于去定植前的检测。【方法】这项回顾性队列研究纳入了 2023 年 2 月至 2024 年 8 月期间入住重症监护室（ICU）并同时接受了 MRSA PCR 鼻拭子检测和普及莫匹罗星去定植的成年住院患者。根据 PCR 检测的时间将患者分为三组：在接受任何剂量的局部莫匹罗星之前、第一剂后 7 天内、或第一剂后 7 天以上。主要结局指标是 MRSA PCR 鼻拭子检测的 NPV。【结果】初步分析纳入了 1034 名患者；508 名（49.1%）在去定植开始前采集了 MRSA PCR 鼻拭子，分别有 388 名（73.8%）和 138 名（26.2%）在莫匹罗星去定植开始后 >0 至 ≤7 天和 >7 天接受了鼻拭子检测。在去定植开始前采集拭子的患者与在开始后 >0 至 ≤7 天及 >7 天采集拭子的患者相比，MRSA PCR 检测的 NPV 差异分别为 0.3% 和 -0.4%（单侧 95% CI 分别为 -0.9% 和 -2.3%）。【结论】莫匹罗星去定植后，MRSA PCR 鼻拭子的 NPV 并未降低。去定植开始后 7 天内的 MRSA PCR 检测非劣于去定植前的检测。

## 4. 巨噬细胞-神经胶质细胞相互作用在西尼罗河病毒感染期间调节肠神经元的免疫损伤

**原文链接：** [点击跳转](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41805580)
**期刊：** Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America
**PMID：** 41805580
**DOI：** 10.1073/pnas.2537058123

### 第一部分 原文与翻译

**英文原标题：** Macrophage-glia interactions regulate immune damage to enteric neurons during West Nile virus infection.

> **英文摘要：**
> Functional gastrointestinal (GI) tract disorders affect a substantial proportion of the global population and are often preceded by intestinal infections that cause injury to enteric neurons and glia through unrestrained immune responses. However, the mechanisms that limit infection-induced inflammation and protect the enteric nervous system remain poorly understood. Here, we defined such neuron-glia-macrophage interactions after West Nile virus (WNV) infection; this model neurotropic virus causes GI tract dysmotility in mice via injury of enteric neurons through a T cell-mediated cytolytic mechanism. In response to WNV infection, RNA sequencing analysis showed that resident muscularis macrophages upregulate antiviral, proinflammatory, and immunomodulatory genes. Whereas pharmacological depletion of resident macrophages did not affect the viral burden in the GI tract, it instead reshaped the enteric glial response to WNV, resulting in excessive production of T cell and neutrophil chemoattractants. The amplified recruitment of these immune cell types worsened enteric neuronal injury. Together, our findings identify resident muscularis macrophages as key regulators of glia-driven inflammation during enteric viral infection and reveal their role in protecting enteric neurons from immune-mediated damage.

> **中文摘要：**
> 功能性胃肠道（GI）疾病影响着全球相当比例的人口，且通常发生于肠道感染之后，此类感染通过不受约束的免疫反应造成肠神经元和神经胶质细胞损伤。然而，限制感染诱导的炎症并保护肠道神经系统的机制仍不为人所知。在此，我们定义了西尼罗河病毒（WNV）感染后这种神经元-神经胶质-巨噬细胞之间的相互作用；这一嗜神经病毒模型通过T细胞介导的细胞毒性机制损伤肠神经元，从而导致小鼠胃肠道动力障碍。针对WNV感染，RNA测序分析显示常驻肌层巨噬细胞上调了抗病毒、促炎和免疫调节基因。虽然药理学清除常驻巨噬细胞并不影响胃肠道内的病毒载量，但它却重塑了肠道神经胶质细胞对WNV的反应，导致T细胞和中性粒细胞趋化因子的过度产生。这些免疫细胞募集的扩增加剧了肠神经元的损伤。总之，我们的研究结果确定常驻肌层巨噬细胞是肠道病毒感染期间神经胶质驱动炎症的关键调节因子，并揭示了它们在保护肠神经元免受免疫介导损伤中的作用。

### 第二部分 AI 大师评价

该研究利用西尼罗河病毒感染诱导的胃肠动力障碍模型，深入探讨了肠道“神经-胶质-免疫”轴在病毒感染背景下的调节机制。研究创新性地发现，常驻肌层巨噬细胞并非通过直接控制病毒载量发挥保护作用，而是通过调节神经胶质细胞的炎症反应，防止其分泌过量的趋化因子，从而避免T细胞和中性粒细胞对肠神经元的免疫介导性损伤。这一发现为理解感染后功能性胃肠道疾病的病理生理学提供了重要的免疫学视角，并突显了巨噬细胞在维持肠道神经稳态中的关键枢纽地位。
